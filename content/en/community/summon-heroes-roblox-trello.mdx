## Dominating the Tournament Mode

With the introduction of the Tournament mode, the competitive landscape of the game has shifted. This mode, found adjacent to the PvP section in the main hub, places players into brackets where they must compete for weekly rewards. Unlike standard PvP, this mode requires you to build three distinct teams, testing the depth of your unit roster.

When you enter a tournament match, you aren't fighting a live player in real-time. Instead, you face an NPC version of another player's team. This allows for strategic planning, as you can see the units you are up against before the rounds begin. Winning these matches increases your rank within your bracket, with the top three players receiving massive payouts.

### Tournament Reward Tiers

| Rank | Gem Reward | Mythic Shards |
|---|---|---|
| **Rank 1** | 10,000 | 20 |
| **Rank 2-3** | 5,000 | 10 |
| **Participation** | 50-100 | 0-1 |

Building three balanced teams is the key to success. Don't put all your Legendary units in Team 1; if you lose the subsequent two rounds, you lose the match. Distribute your power units across all three parties to ensure you can win at least two out of three engagements.

## Dungeon Mechanics and Resource Farming

The developers have reworked how dungeons function to respect the player's time while maintaining the grind. You are now limited to three dungeon runs per 24-hour period. While this might seem restrictive, you now have the ability to rerun the same dungeon multiple times, and the rewards have been buffed to compensate for the lower frequency.

For players seeking Mythic Shards, **Mythic Dungeon 2** is now the gold standard, offering two shards per completion. If you are short on Gems, the new **Gem Dungeons** provide a substantial influx of currency:
*   **Gem Dungeon 1:** 500 Gems per run.
*   **Gem Dungeon 2:** 1,000 Gems per run (Unlocks at Level 130).

By running Gem Dungeon 2 three times a day, you can earn 3,000 Gems daily, making it much easier to pull for the latest Legendary units featured on the **summon heroes roblox trello** tier lists.

## Essential Tips for Progression

To keep up with the rapid updates, you need to optimize your daily routine. Follow these steps to ensure you don't fall behind the level cap:

1.  **Claim Daily PvP Rewards:** Even if you aren't a top-tier player, ranking in the top 100 grants you 2 Mythic Shards.
2.  **Use Active Codes:** Always check for codes like "Sunset," which provides five legendary fusion crystals. These are vital for upgrading your units' star levels.
3.  **Prioritize Story Mode:** Completing a story stage for the first time now rewards 100 Gems. While the reward decreases by 10 for each subsequent completion, it remains one of the fastest ways to farm currency for new players.
4.  **Check the Unit Dex:** Don't forget to claim your "New Entry" gems every time you pull a unit you haven't owned before.

The community frequently updates the [official Roblox group](https://www.roblox.com/groups/15941610/Summon-Heroes) with news on upcoming balance changes, which is a great supplement to the information found on the Trello.
