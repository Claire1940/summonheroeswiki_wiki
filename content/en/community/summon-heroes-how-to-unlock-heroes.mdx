## The Fundamentals of Summoning

Unlocking new units in Summon Heroes revolves around the Summoning Altar. This is the primary hub where players spend their hard-earned Gems or Summoning Tickets to roll for new characters. Units are divided into several rarities, ranging from Common to the elusive Mythical tier. 

When you first enter the game, your priority should be accumulating enough Gems for a "Multi-Summon." While single summons are available for 100 Gems, saving for larger batches or reaching the "Guaranteed Legendary" threshold is generally more efficient for long-term progression.

## How to Farm Gems to Unlock Heroes

Since Gems are the lifeblood of the summoning system, knowing how to acquire them quickly is the most important part of learning **summon heroes how to unlock heroes**. In 2026, there are several "low-effort" ways to boost your Gem count before you even step into a dungeon.

### Loyalty and Social Rewards
The developers offer significant boosts for players who engage with the game's community. By simply "favoriting" the game on the [Roblox platform](https://www.roblox.com/) and joining the official developer group, you can claim a one-time bonus of 500 Gems. This is often enough to get your first five units and jumpstart your campaign.

### Using Active Codes
Codes are frequently released during updates or community milestones. These can provide anywhere from 500 to 2,000 Gems instantly. To use them, look for the "Codes" or "Items" tab in the main menu. 

### The Unit Index
A commonly overlooked method for earning Gems is the **Unit Index**. Every time you unlock a brand-new hero, they are recorded in your index. Navigating to the "Units" tab and selecting "Unit Decks" allows you to claim Gem rewards for every unique character discovered. This creates a "loop" where summoning new heroes actually gives you the currency needed to summon even more.

## The AFK Story Mode Method

The most effective way to unlock heroes consistently is through the AFK Story Mode method. This allows you to farm Gems and Experience (XP) while away from your computer.

1.  **Select the Right Stage:** Choose the final stage of the highest world you can comfortably clear. For beginners, this is usually the last stage of Rookie Island.
2.  **Use "Sweeps":** If you have already cleared a stage with a high rating, use your daily "Sweeps" to instantly claim rewards without fighting.
3.  **The Auto-Clicker Setup:** If you are out of sweeps, equip an auto-clicker. Position your mouse cursor over the area where the "Retry" or "Repeat Stage" button appears (usually near the third character ability slot).
4.  **Stay Active:** The auto-clicker prevents the Roblox "Idle Kick" (20-minute timer) while ensuring you restart the level immediately after victory.

> **⚠️ Warning:** While auto-clickers are generally permitted in Summon Heroes, always ensure you are using a reputable tool and not a "script" that modifies game files, as the latter can result in a ban.

## Enhancing Your Unlocked Heroes

Unlocking a hero is only the first step. To progress through harder content like the Ever Crisis Academy, you must invest in your units. A low-rarity hero with high-rank stats will often outperform a Legendary hero with poor stats.

### Stat Rerolling
Every unit has a set of base stats (Attack, Defense, etc.) ranked from D to X. **Attack is the most important stat.** Use the Stat Reroll NPC to aim for an **X Rank** in Attack. This significantly boosts your DPS and makes farming much faster.

### Fusions and Leveling
If you have duplicate units or "fodder" heroes you don't plan on using, use the Fusion system. Sacrificing these units into your main team increases their base stats. Additionally, feed your heroes "Food" or "Candy" items to reach the current level cap of 140.

## FAQ

**Q: How many gems do I need for a guaranteed Legendary in Summon Heroes?**

A: You typically need between 9,500 and 9,700 Gems to hit the pity threshold for a guaranteed Legendary unit. This ensures that even with bad luck, you will eventually unlock a high-tier hero.

**Q: What is the difference between Pack A and Pack B?**

A: These packs contain different sets of heroes. Before you spend your Gems, check the "Index" or the preview window at the Summoning Altar to see which heroes are featured in each pack.

**Q: Can I unlock Mythical heroes with Gems?**

A: While you can get lucky with standard rolls, Mythical heroes often require 50 Mythical Shards. Shards are earned through Daily Quests, high-level Dungeon clears, or as rare drops from the Nightmare Circus.

**Q: Is there a way to unlock heroes for free without farming?**

A: The only way to get heroes without active gameplay is by using promotional codes or claiming the loyalty rewards for favoriting the game and joining the developer group. This provides a significant initial boost to your Gem count.

**Q: What should I do with duplicate heroes?**

A: Do not delete them! Use the Fusion menu to sacrifice duplicates into your primary heroes. This is the best way to increase their stats beyond their base level and reach the power levels required for end-game content.
