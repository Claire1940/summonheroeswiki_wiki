## The Starcross Romance Event: Hero Analysis

The "Starcross Romance" event is the centerpiece of the current **summon heroes event guide**. This event introduces two highly anticipated units: **Roselia** and **Pierre**. These heroes are "bonded," meaning they gain unique stat boosts and skill modifications when placed on the same team.

### Roselia: The AoE Queen
Roselia has quickly ascended to the top of the tier list for 2026. Her primary strength lies in her massive Area of Effect (AoE) magic damage. She is considered a "goddess" for Matrix content and the Conqueror challenges.

*   **Key Strengths:** High knockback capability, superior magic DPS, and essential for Matrix stages.
*   **Weakness:** Heavily countered by enemies with high Magic Resistance.
*   **Recommendation:** A must-pull for players lacking a top-tier AoE mage. Even at A0 (Awakening 0), she outperforms most legacy units.

### Pierre and the Prismark Mechanic
Pierre introduces the Prismark summon, a stationary entity that copies Pierre’s stats at the moment of summoning. This makes Pierre a "stat-snapshotting" hero, requiring a specific playstyle to optimize.

*   **Strategy:** Before summoning the Prismark, stack Pierre with every possible buff (Inspiration, Crit Damage boosts, and Attack power). The Prismark will retain these boosted stats for its entire duration.
*   **Bond Synergy:** When paired with Roselia, Pierre’s summons stay on the field longer and deal increased damage based on Roselia's current stacks.

## Advanced Summoning Tactics: Ancient Summoning and Pity

Ancient Summoning remains the most difficult way to acquire heroes, but it offers the highest rewards. In 2026, the "Lord Pity" system has been refined. After a certain number of pulls without a Legendary Lord, your chances increase significantly.

1.  **Check Your Pity Counter:** Always track how many pulls you are away from a guaranteed legendary.
2.  **Save for Ancient Banners:** Only spend Ancient Summoning crystals during events that feature a Lord you specifically need (like **Ezareth** for Cultist teams).
3.  **Divine Summoning Windows:** Use Divine Summoning for specific epics that round out your faction teams. While legendaries are the goal, high-awakening epics are often more viable for mid-game players.

**Q: Is the summon heroes event guide applicable to all platforms in 2026?**

**Q: How often are new codes released for Summon Heroes?**

**Q: What is the "Lord Pity" system?**

**Q: Can I use the same promo code multiple times?**

A: No, promo codes are strictly one-time use per account. However, some event-specific codes may be refreshed during the second half of a month-long celebration.
