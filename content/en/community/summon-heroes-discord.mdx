## Troubleshooting Connection and Invite Issues

Sometimes, you might encounter errors when trying to join the **summon heroes discord**. These issues are usually easy to fix but can be frustrating for players eager to get started.

*   **"Invite Invalid"**: This usually means the link has expired or the server has reached its maximum capacity. Try finding a newer link on the game's official social media pages.
*   **"Unable to Accept Invite"**: This often occurs if you are in too many servers (the limit is 100 for free accounts, 200 for Nitro). Leave a server you no longer use and try again.
*   **Verification Fails**: Ensure your Roblox inventory is set to "Public" so the verification bot can see your account details. You can change this in your Roblox privacy settings.
