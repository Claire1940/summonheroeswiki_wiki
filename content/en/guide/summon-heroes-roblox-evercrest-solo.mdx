## Step-by-Step Wave Strategy

The Evercrest stage consists of 20 waves of increasingly difficult enemies, culminating in a fight against the Evercrest Sentinel. Managing your resources across these waves is essential for a successful **summon heroes roblox evercrest solo** experience.

### Waves 1-10: Resource Management
The early waves are designed to bait out your ultimate abilities. Avoid using your strongest skills here. Instead, rely on basic attacks and low-cooldown abilities to clear the mobs. If you find your health dropping too low, use your healer's basic skill but save the "Ultimate Heal" for the elite spawns in Wave 10.

### Waves 11-19: Positioning and Crowd Control
This is where most solo players struggle. The enemies in these waves include ranged casters that can kite your heroes. 
1. **Lure enemies** into corners to maximize your AoE damage.
2. **Prioritize casters** first, as their debuffs can stack and reduce your damage output by up to 50%.
3. **Keep your tank** in the front, but ensure your DPS units are close enough to benefit from any defensive auras.

### Wave 20: The Boss Fight
The Evercrest Sentinel has three phases. In Phase 1, focus on breaking his shield. In Phase 2, he will summon adds; ignore the minor adds and focus on the Sentinel unless the adds are healing him. In Phase 3, he enters a "Berserk" state where his damage doubles. This is the moment to unleash every ultimate ability you have saved.

To truly excel at the **summon heroes roblox evercrest solo** challenge, you should learn to "flash-step" or animation-cancel your hero abilities. This is vital when the Evercrest Sentinel uses his "Skyfall" ability, which creates large red circles on the ground that deal massive damage after a 2-second delay.

Furthermore, ensure you are utilizing the [Official Summon Heroes Community Hub](https://www.roblox.com) to stay updated on the latest patch notes.
