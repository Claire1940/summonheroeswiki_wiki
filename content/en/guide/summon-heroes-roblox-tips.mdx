## Mastering the Summoning System

The heart of the game lies in the summoning area, where players use gems to acquire new units. Unlike many other games that feature static banners, Summon Heroes utilizes a rotating system that demands constant attention. There are generally two different packs available at any given time, and these rotate every 30 minutes. This means if you don't see the specific hero you want, you only have a short wait before a new set of units becomes available.

Each banner contains a specific distribution of rarities. Understanding these odds is crucial for managing your expectations and your gem wallet.

| Unit Rarity | Number of Units per Banner | Drop Chance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mythic** | 1 | 0.1% |
| **Legendary** | 1 | Varies (Pity at 100) |
| **Epic** | 2 | ~14.5% |
| **Rare** | 3 | High |

> **💡 Tip:** The legendary pity system is set at 100 summons. However, be aware that pity is **not shared** between different banners. If you start summoning on one pack, stick with it until you hit your legendary pull to avoid wasting your progress.

A single summon costs 100 gems, while a 10-pull requires 1,000 gems. While it may be tempting to "single pull" as soon as you have the currency, saving for 10-pulls is generally more efficient for tracking your pity progress.

## Unit Fusion and Growth Strategies

One of the most common mistakes new players make is enabling the "Auto-Sell" feature for Rare and Epic units. While this might keep your inventory clean, it is a massive setback for your progression. In Summon Heroes, units are not just for combat; they are the primary resource for the fusion system.

The fusion mechanic in this game is remarkably flexible. Unlike other titles where you need an exact duplicate of a hero to upgrade them, Summon Heroes allows you to use any unit of the same star rarity as fodder. 

### How Fusion Works
1.  **Select your Target:** Choose the unit you want to strengthen (e.g., a 4-star Dragoon).
2.  **Select Fodder:** Choose any other 4-star unit you own.
3.  **Upgrade:** Combine them to turn your target into a 5-star unit.

This system makes every single pull valuable. Even if you pull a "trash" Epic unit, that unit is actually a vital stepping stone to turning your Legendary hero into a powerhouse.

## Essential Daily Activities for Resources

To keep up with the rising costs of summons and upgrades, you must maximize your daily resource intake. There are two primary ways to earn gems and materials outside of standard stage progression: the Nightmare Circus and the AFK Dojo.

### The Nightmare Circus
This is a daily activity that can be performed three times every 24 hours. It functions as an endless wave mode where the difficulty ramps up significantly as you progress. The rewards scale based on how far you get. For instance, reaching level 130 can net you upwards of 300 gems in just a few minutes. It is the single best way to earn quick currency for your next 10-pull.

### The AFK Dojo
If you are busy or need to step away from your computer, the AFK mode is your best friend. Located in the main hub, entering the AFK Dojo allows you to passively accumulate rewards.
*   **Gems:** Approximately 500 gems per hour.
*   **Gold:** Steady income for shop purchases.
*   **Food:** Essential items like rice cakes and candy used to level up your units.

| Activity | Frequency | Primary Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Nightmare Circus** | 3x Daily | **Gems & High-tier Loot** |
| **AFK Dojo** | Unlimited | **Gems & Unit Food** |
| **Item Shop** | Every 5 Mins | **Fusion Crystals & Tickets** |

## Best Early-Game Heroes to Target

When you are just starting, you won't have a team full of Mythics. You need reliable units that are easy to obtain but punch above their weight class. Based on current **summon heroes roblox tips** from top players, these are the units you should look for on the rotating banners.

### The Bear Tamer (Rare)
Do not let the "Rare" tag fool you. The Bear Tamer is widely considered one of the best value units in the game. He provides excellent frontline support and damage, making him a staple for early-game stage clearing. Because he is a Rare rarity, he is very easy to pull and even easier to fuse into higher star levels.

### The Mermaid (Rare)
Crowd control and Area of Effect (AoE) damage are vital when facing large waves of slimes or bandits. The Mermaid excels at wiping out groups of enemies simultaneously. A common beginner strategy is to run a team consisting of two Mermaids and one Bear Tamer to balance high AoE damage with a sturdy frontline.

### Dual Wielder (Epic)
If you manage to pull a Dual Wielder, equip him immediately. He functions as a high-HP "tank" who can soak up damage from bosses like the Slime Summoner while your ranged units (like the Archer or Mage) chip away at the boss's health from a distance.

> **💡 Warning:** Avoid using the "Equip Best" button blindly. While it usually picks the highest stat units, it doesn't account for elemental synergy or the balance between melee and ranged attackers.

## The Item Shop and Resource Management

The Item Shop in the main hub is a gold mine for players who check it frequently. Unlike the banners which rotate every 30 minutes, the shop resets every **5 minutes**. This high frequency means you can quickly stock up on essential materials if you have the gold.

**Items to prioritize in the shop:**
*   **Summon Tickets:** Occasionally appear for gold, allowing for "free" summons.
*   **Fusion Crystals:** These are the primary currency used for fusing units. Buy every rarity available whenever they appear.
*   **Rice Cakes/Food:** Used to "bubble up" or level up your units quickly without grinding stages.

## Combat Progression and Boss Tactics

Progression in Summon Heroes is linear but rewarding. Each stage consists of several waves of enemies followed by a final boss. To level up your units effectively, you must consistently clear these stages to earn "Loot Drops" and experience.

1.  **Stage 1 - The Forest:** Focus on leveling your initial trio of heroes to Level 5.
2.  **The Slime Summoner:** This is the first major hurdle. Ensure your melee unit (Dual Wielder or Bear Tamer) is positioned to take the brunt of the hits.
3.  **Auto-Ability Toggle:** Once you unlock ultimate abilities, keep "Auto" toggled on for your damage dealers to ensure they are using their high-impact moves as soon as the cooldown expires.

Keep an eye on the health bars of your units. If your Dual Wielder is consistently falling below 20% health before the boss spawns, it is a sign that you need to return to the AFK Dojo or use more food items to increase his base HP.

## FAQ

**Q: How often do the summon banners change in Summon Heroes?**

A: The banners rotate every 30 minutes. Each rotation features a different Mythic, Legendary, and set of Epic/Rare units. If you are looking for a specific hero, check back twice an hour.

**Q: What are the best summon heroes roblox tips for earning gems quickly?**

A: The fastest way to earn gems is by completing your three daily Nightmare Circus runs. For passive income, leave your character in the AFK Dojo, which provides roughly 500 gems per hour.

**Q: Should I sell my Rare units for extra gold?**

A: No! You should never sell Rare or Epic units. These units are required for the fusion system. You can use any unit of the same star rarity to upgrade your favorite heroes to higher star levels.

**Q: Does the pity system carry over between different banners?**

A: Unfortunately, no. Each banner has its own independent pity counter. If you have done 50 summons on Banner A, you will still be at 0 pity on Banner B. Always try to finish your pity on a single banner before switching.
