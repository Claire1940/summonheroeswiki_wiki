One of the most exciting aspects of the **summon heroes latest update** is the introduction of four distinct units that diversify the current meta. These units range from accessible Rare options to high-performance Legendaries that excel in specific combat scenarios.

## Exploring Sunset City and the Level 120 Cap

The world of Summon Heroes continues to expand with the addition of **Sunset City**, a visually stunning map consisting of five stages. The design team has leaned heavily into a "cyber-techno" aesthetic, featuring orange holographic trees, animated billboards, and intricate hotel lobby interiors. 

The level cap has also been increased from 100 to **120**, giving players a reason to return to the grind. Reaching the end of Sunset City brings you face-to-face with the boss **Cyber DJ Nikki**, a high-mobility encounter that tests your unit placement and timing.

## The New Dungeon System

The **summon heroes latest update** introduces a dedicated Dungeon portal in the central hub. Unlike standard story missions, Dungeons are designed for high-frequency farming, resetting every **6 hours**. This allows dedicated players to run these missions up to four times a day for maximum efficiency.

### Dungeon Types and Rewards

| Dungeon Name | Boss | Boss HP | Key Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Food Dungeon** | Sarif | ~4,000 | 250 Gems, Unit XP Food |
| **Fusion Dungeon** | Divine | ~10,000 | 250 Gems, Fusion Crystals |
| **Mythic Dungeon** | Reaper | ~46,000 | 250 Gems, **Mythic Shards** |

The Mythic Dungeon is the most critical addition for endgame players. Completing it grants Mythic Shards, which are the only way to obtain the new guaranteed Mythic unit, **The Emperor**.

> **⚠️ Warning:** While the dungeons are not excessively difficult for players at level 100+, the Reaper boss in the Mythic Dungeon has significantly more health than standard story bosses. Ensure your single-target DPS units (like Frame Rate) are leveled appropriately.

## The Path to Mythic: The Emperor

The Emperor is the crown jewel of this update. To summon this unit, you must collect **50 Mythic Shards**. Under the current system, there are two primary ways to farm these shards:

1.  **Dungeon Runs:** Four shards per day (one every 6 hours).
2.  **Daily Quests:** Complete the "Nightmare Circus" three times to earn one additional shard.

By staying consistent with these tasks, players can secure a guaranteed Mythic unit every **10 days**. This provides a predictable progression path that doesn't rely solely on luck-based summoning.

## PvP Rewards and Economy Changes

Competitive players have new incentives to climb the leaderboards. The **summon heroes latest update** has introduced daily rewards for the top 100 players in the PvP rankings.

*   **Top 10 Players:** 5 Mythic Shards daily.
*   **Players 11-100:** 2 Mythic Shards daily.

Furthermore, the PvP token system has been adjusted to be more forgiving. Previously, losing a match felt like a waste of time, but now, players receive **1 PvP Token** for a loss and **3 PvP Tokens** for a win. This ensures that even those struggling with the meta can eventually purchase rewards from the PvP shop.

## Controversial Changes: Story Mission Time-Gating

Not every change in the **summon heroes latest update** has been met with universal praise. The developers have implemented a restriction where story missions only provide rewards **once every 24 hours**. 

The reasoning behind this move is to combat the use of auto-clickers and third-party scripts that allowed players to farm infinite resources overnight. While this protects the game's economy, it has significantly reduced the amount of replayable content for active players. To compensate, the developers have boosted the rewards in the new Dungeon system, encouraging players to pivot their focus there instead of looping old story stages.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get more Mythic Shards in the summon heroes latest update?**

A: You can earn up to five shards daily by completing the Mythic Dungeon every 6 hours and finishing the Nightmare Circus daily quest. High-ranking PvP players can earn additional shards based on their leaderboard position.

**Q: What is the best unit to use for the new Sunset City boss?**

A: **Frame Rate** is highly recommended for the Cyber DJ Nikki boss fight. Its high-speed single-target physical attacks allow you to burn through the boss's HP quickly before her mechanics become too overwhelming.

**Q: Why am I not getting rewards from story missions anymore?**

A: Following the latest update, story mission rewards are limited to once per 24 hours per stage. This was implemented to prevent exploitative farming. You should focus on Dungeons for repeatable rewards.

**Q: How many gems do I get from the new Unit Index?**

A: Each time you register a new unit in the Index, you can claim 800 Gems. This is a great way to quickly accumulate currency for the new Mythic pack.
