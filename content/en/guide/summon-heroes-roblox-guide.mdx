## Understanding the Summoning System

The core of your progression lies in the Summon Area. Unlike many other gacha-style games, Summon Heroes features a rotating banner system that changes every 30 minutes. This means if you don't see a unit you want, you only have to wait a short duration for a fresh set of heroes. Each banner contains a specific pool of seven units, ranging from common Rares to the elusive Mythics.

| Unit Rarity | Pull Chance | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mythic** | **0.1%** | The rarest tier in the game; extremely high stats. |
| **Legendary** | **~1.5%** | Guaranteed Pity at 100 summons per banner. |
| **Epic** | **~14.5%** | Strong mid-tier units like the Dual Wielder. |
| **Rare** | **Remaining %** | Essential for fusion and early-game utility. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Legendary Pity system (100 summons) is **not shared** between banners. If you switch banners before hitting 100 pulls, your progress on the previous banner's pity will not carry over.

To summon, you will need Gems. A single pull costs **100 Gems**, while a 10-pull costs **1,000 Gems**. It is generally recommended to save for 10-pulls to ensure you are consistently working toward that 100-pull pity mark on a banner you truly care about.

## Mastering the Summon Heroes Roblox Guide Fusion Mechanics

One of the most innovative features in Summon Heroes is the fusion system. Most players make the mistake of enabling "Auto-Sell" for Rare and Epic units early on. You should avoid this at all costs. In this game, units are not just fodder for gold; they are the key to increasing the "Star" rarity of your main team.

The fusion system allows you to take any unit of a specific star rating and fuse it into another unit of the same rating to increase its power. For example, if you have a 4-star Dragoon, you can use any other 4-star unit you own—regardless of its name or type—to upgrade that Dragoon into a 5-star powerhouse.

### How to Power Up Your Team

1.  **Check the Item Shop:** The shop resets every 5 minutes in the main hub. Look for **Fusion Crystals** and **Rice Cakes**.
2.  **Use Food for Levels:** Items like Rice Cakes and Candy are used to "bubble up" your units' base levels quickly.
3.  **Match Star Ratings:** Ensure your "fodder" units match the star count of the hero you wish to upgrade.
4.  **Equip Best:** Always use the "Equip Best" button in your inventory to ensure your highest-stat units are on the field.

## Resource Farming: Gems and Gold

To keep summoning, you need a steady stream of Gems. This **summon heroes roblox guide** highlights three primary ways to stay wealthy in-game without spending Robux.

| Method | Rewards | Frequency |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Nightmare Circus** | **300+ Gems** per run | 3 Times Daily |
| **AFK Mode** | **~500 Gems** per hour | Unlimited |
| **Stage Progression** | **30+ Gems** per boss | One-time per stage |

### Nightmare Circus
This is an endless wave mode that tests the endurance of your team. You can enter this three times per day. The further you progress, the higher the Gem payout. A decent mid-game team can easily clear enough waves to net 300 Gems in under five minutes, making this the most time-efficient method for active players.

### AFK Mode (The Dojo)
If you are planning to step away from your computer or mobile device, head to the main Dojo. By sitting in AFK mode, you passively earn approximately 500 Gems every hour, along with a significant amount of gold and unit food. This is the best way to prepare for a major banner rotation.

## Best Early-Game Units to Target

While Mythics are the end-goal, you can clear a significant portion of the game using high-tier Rare and Epic units. For more information on the latest updates and developer notes, you can visit the [official Roblox page](https://www.roblox.com/) to see the current game version.

### 1. Bear Tamer (Rare)
Despite being a Rare unit, the Bear Tamer is widely considered one of the best units in the current 2026 meta. He offers incredible sustainability and consistent damage. Because he is a Rare, he is very easy to pull and even easier to fuse into higher star ratings.

### 2. Mermaid (Rare)
The Mermaid is the queen of Area of Effect (AoE) damage. In modes like Nightmare Circus where you are swarmed by dozens of slimes and bandits, her ability to clear the screen is invaluable. A team consisting of two Mermaids and one Bear Tamer is a classic "starter" build that can carry you through the first several islands.

### 3. Dual Wielder (Epic)
If you are lucky enough to pull a 4-star Dual Wielder early on, equip him immediately. He acts as a primary "tank," standing at the front of the formation and absorbing hits while your mages and archers deal damage from the backline.

> **💡 Tip:** Always keep an eye on the "Auto" button during combat. This triggers your units' ultimate abilities as soon as they are off cooldown, which is essential for boss fights like the Slime Summoner.

## Advanced Progression Tips

As you move past the initial tutorial stages, the difficulty spikes significantly. To stay ahead, you must treat the Item Shop as your most important destination. Since it resets every 5 minutes, checking it frequently allows you to stock up on Summon Tickets, which effectively act as free pulls.

*   **Don't Rush Stages:** If you find your units' health bars dropping too fast, return to the previous stage and "farm" it for a few minutes to gain extra levels.
*   **Focus on Synergy:** Pairing a tank (Dual Wielder) with a healer or AoE specialist (Mermaid) is much more effective than having three high-damage units with low health.
*   **Decal Details:** Pay attention to the map environments. While the graphics are impressive, some terrain can be used to funnel enemies into your AoE attacks.

## FAQ

**Q: Does the pity system reset when the banner rotates?**

A: No, the pity is tied to the specific banner slot. However, keep in mind that the two different packs (Pack 1 and Pack 2) have completely separate pity counters. This **summon heroes roblox guide** recommends sticking to one pack until you hit your Legendary.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get Gems in Summon Heroes?**

A: For active players, the Nightmare Circus is the fastest way to earn Gems. For passive players, leaving your character in the AFK Dojo overnight will yield thousands of Gems by morning.

**Q: Can I use a 3-star unit to fuse into a 4-star unit?**

A: No. The units must have the same star rarity to be used for fusion. You must first upgrade your 3-star units into 4-star units before they can be used as fodder for your main 4-star heroes.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many units I can own?**

A: There is an inventory limit, which is why the fusion system is so important. Instead of selling units, fuse them to clear space and make your primary team stronger.
