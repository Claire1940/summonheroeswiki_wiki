## Understanding the Summoning System

The core of your progression lies in the Summon Area. Here, you will spend your hard-earned gems to acquire new units that vary in rarity and power. In Summon Heroes, the summon banners are dynamic, meaning they rotate every 30 minutes. This rotation is crucial because it changes which legendary and mythic units are available, allowing you to target specific heroes for your team composition.

Each banner pack typically contains a specific distribution of units. Understanding these odds is the first step in planning your pulls.

| Unit Rarity | Number of Units on Banner | Pull Chance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mythic** | 1 | **0.1%** |
| **Legendary** | 1 | Varies (Pity at 100) |
| **Epic** | 2 | ~14.5% |
| **Rare** | 3 | Common |

One of the most player-friendly features in Summon Heroes is the **Legendary Pity System**. If you do not pull a legendary unit within 100 summons, the game guarantees one. However, a vital tip for any new player is that **pity is not shared between banners**. If you have 50 pulls on one banner and it rotates, those 50 pulls do not count toward the pity of the new banner. Always ensure you have enough gems (at least 10,000 for a guaranteed legendary) before committing heavily to a specific rotation.

## The Art of Unit Fusion and Leveling

A common mistake highlighted in this **summon heroes roblox new player guide** is the use of the "Auto Sell" feature for Rare and Epic units. While it may seem like an easy way to clear inventory and get quick coins, it is generally discouraged. This is because of the game's unique **Fusion System**.

Unlike many other Roblox games where you need duplicates of the exact same character to evolve them, Summon Heroes allows you to use any unit of the same star rarity as fusion fodder. For example, if you have a four-star Dragoon and want to upgrade it to a five-star version, you can feed it any other four-star units you have in your inventory. 

### Fusion Requirements and Benefits

To maximize your team's potential, you should focus on the following fusion steps:

1.  **Collect Fodder**: Keep your Rare and Epic units instead of selling them.
2.  **Match Star Ratings**: Ensure the fodder units have the same star count as the hero you want to upgrade.
3.  **Use Fusion Crystals**: These are specialized currencies used during the process to facilitate the upgrade.
4.  **Level Up First**: Use food items like rice cakes or candy to max out a unit's level before attempting a star-rating increase.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Never auto-sell your Epics or Rares early on. You will eventually need hundreds of these units to fuse your Legendaries and Mythics into their final, most powerful forms.

## Farming Gems and Resources

Gems are the lifeblood of Summon Heroes. Without them, you cannot summon new units or purchase essential items from the shop. Fortunately, there are several ways to accumulate gems quickly, even as a free-to-play player.

### Efficient Farming Methods

| Method | Rewards | Frequency |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Nightmare Circus** | **300+ Gems** per run | 3 Times Daily |
| **AFK Mode (Dojo)** | **~500 Gems** per hour | Unlimited |
| **Main Stages** | Coins, Gems, and Food | Repeatable |
| **Item Shop** | Summon Tickets & Crystals | Resets every 5 mins |

The **Nightmare Circus** is perhaps the most lucrative daily activity. It is an endless wave mode where the difficulty increases the further you progress. Reaching level 130 can net you upwards of 300 gems in just a few minutes of gameplay. Meanwhile, the **AFK Mode** located in the main dojo is perfect for players who want to progress while away from their keyboard. Not only do you gain roughly 500 gems per hour, but you also accumulate a significant amount of food items to level up your units.

## Best Starting Units for New Players

When you first start, you won't have access to Mythic or Legendary units immediately. You will need to rely on high-tier Rare and Epic units to clear the early stages and the bandit bosses. Based on current meta-analysis, certain units provide much higher value than their rarity suggests.

*   **Bear Tamer (Rare)**: Surprisingly, the Bear Tamer is considered one of the best units in the game due to its high survivability and consistent damage output. Since it is a Rare unit, it is very easy to pull and max out via fusion.
*   **Mermaid (Rare)**: The Mermaid is the queen of early-game Area of Effect (AoE) damage. Her ability to clear waves of slimes and minions quickly makes her essential for speeding up stage clears.
*   **Dual Wielder (Epic)**: This unit acts as a fantastic "tank" for your team. During the tutorial and early stages, the Dual Wielder can soak up damage from bosses while your ranged units like the Archer or Mage deal damage from safety.

> **💡 Tip:** A balanced starting team often consists of one "Tank" (like the Dual Wielder) and two "AoE/DPS" units (like the Mermaid or Bear Tamer).

## Advanced Tips for 2026

To truly excel, you must look beyond the basic combat. The **Item Shop** in the main hub is a goldmine for savvy players. It resets every 5 minutes, which is incredibly fast for a Roblox game. You should check it frequently to buy Summon Tickets, Fusion Crystals, and high-quality food. 

Additionally, pay attention to the "Auto" toggle on your units. While the game handles basic attacks, toggling "Auto" ensures your units use their special abilities as soon as they are off cooldown. This is often the difference between defeating a boss and failing the stage. ## FAQ

**Q: How often do the summon banners change in Summon Heroes?**

A: The banners rotate every 30 minutes. Each rotation features a different set of Legendary and Mythic units, so if you don't see a hero you want, check back soon.

**Q: Does the pity system carry over to the next banner?**

A: No, the pity system is unique to each individual banner. If a banner rotates, your progress toward a guaranteed Legendary on that specific banner is lost.

**Q: What is the best way to get gems according to this summon heroes roblox new player guide?**

A: The most efficient way is a combination of doing your three daily Nightmare Circus runs for quick bursts of gems and using the AFK Dojo mode overnight to earn roughly 500 gems per hour.

**Q: Should I sell my low-rarity units for coins?**

A: Generally, no. It is better to keep your Rare and Epic units to use as fusion fodder to upgrade your stronger units to higher star ratings. Only sell them if your inventory is completely full and you have no units left to upgrade.
