## Deep Dive into S-Tier Units

### Reaper (The AOE King)
The Reaper is arguably the most sought-after unit in the game. His scythe swings cover a massive area, consuming the health of entire waves instantly. In 2026, he remains the gold standard for "wave clear." If you manage to pull a Reaper, focus all your resources on leveling him to the cap (currently level 50) and rerolling his stats for an X-rank attack.

### Bear Tamer (The Budget S-Tier)
As mentioned previously, the Bear Tamer is the "GOAT" of Rare units. He summons a giant bear and two smaller cubs. This utility is unmatched for a unit that is relatively easy to pull from the basic banner. He serves as your primary tank, drawing aggro away from your summoner so you don't get wiped by stray projectiles.

### Demon Knight (The PvP Specialist)
While the Reaper excels at clearing swarms, the Demon Knight is a beast in 1v1 and PvP scenarios. His combo-based DPS and awakening buffs make him a nightmare to face in the arena. If you are looking to climb the PvP leaderboards, the Demon Knight is a mandatory inclusion in your squad.

| Stat Rank | Potential | Recommendation |

### Efficiency and AFK Farming
Farming gems and gold is a necessity for summoning. Stage 44 is widely considered the best place to farm in the early-to-mid game. Many pro players utilize tools like TinyTask on PC to automate the "Ready" and "Retry" buttons. By setting the playback speed to 100x and recording a simple loop of clicking the UI elements, you can accumulate rewards overnight.

> **⚠️ Warning:** While AFK farming is a common practice, ensure your character doesn't get stuck on map geometry. Some maps in the current beta have "sticky corners" that can ruin an automated run.

You can check the latest updates and community events on the official [Roblox platform](https://www.roblox.com) to stay ahead of any balance changes that might shift the meta.

## Summoning Strategy and Pity Mechanics
The gacha system in Summon Heroes features a pity mechanic that guarantees a high-rarity pull after a certain number of summons. 2026 players recommend saving your gems for limited banners that feature either the Reaper or the Jester. 

There are typically two banners active at any time. Unlike other games where banners rotate simultaneously, Summon Heroes often has staggered timers. This allows you to check back every 30 minutes to see if a superior unit has entered the rotation. If you are close to your pity count, wait for a banner that features a unit that complements your current team's weaknesses.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the absolute best unit for a beginner to aim for?**

A: The Bear Tamer is the best unit for beginners. He is a Rare rarity, making him easier to pull, but his summoning abilities provide enough protection to carry you through most of the early story mode stages.

**Q: How do I get more Traits for my heroes?**

A: Traits are rare and difficult to come by. You can obtain them through limited-time codes provided by the developers in the official Discord, or you can purchase them directly from the item shop using premium currency.

**Q: Is there a level cap for heroes in Summon Heroes?**

A: Currently, heroes are capped at level 50. Once you reach this cap, your focus should shift toward rerolling for X-rank stats and acquiring better Traits to further increase your power.

**Q: Which stage is best for building the summon heroes roblox best team through farming?**

A: Stage 44 is the community-favorite for farming resources. It offers a high gold-to-time ratio and is relatively easy to automate using an auto-clicker or macro tool.

**Q: Does the game have a PvP mode?**

A: Yes, there is a PvP area where players can test their teams against others. Note that units are often level-synced in PvP, meaning your victory depends more on unit synergy and stat ranks than raw level advantage.
