## Technomancer: The Queen of Crowd Control

While Frame Rate focuses on the individual, the Technomancer focuses on the battlefield. She has quickly become a fan favorite for players who find it difficult to obtain ultra-rare units like The Reaper. The Technomancer offers a similar playstyle but with a more accessible entry point for free-to-play users.

Her primary attack releases two massive waves of digital energy. These waves have an incredible range, stretching across a significant portion of the arena. This makes her one of the best choices for stages like Leviathan's Eye, where enemies often spawn in tight clusters. Her special ability summons eight seeking orbs that track down enemies, ensuring that no stragglers are left behind.

### Technomancer Ability Breakdown

*   **Digital Energy Waves:** Two-wave attack that covers a wide horizontal area.
*   **Seeking Orbs:** Eight projectiles that automatically target the nearest threats.
*   **Crowd Control:** High stagger potential against groups of enemies.

For many, the Technomancer is the **best legendary in summon heroes roblox** because of her versatility. She can handle both waves of minions and provide consistent chip damage to bosses while staying at a safe distance.
