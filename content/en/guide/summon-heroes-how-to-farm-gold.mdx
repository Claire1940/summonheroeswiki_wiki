## The Main Dojo: Passive AFK Gold Generation

The most reliable method for consistent income is the AFK mode located within the Main Dojo. This is the cornerstone of any strategy regarding **Summon Heroes how to farm gold** because it requires zero active input once you enter the zone. While players often focus on active clicking or stage clearing, the passive rewards in the Dojo accumulate into significant totals over long sessions.

In the Dojo, you will passively earn approximately **500 gems per hour**, alongside a steady stream of gold (money) and unit food. This is particularly useful for players who can leave their devices running overnight or while at work. The food items gained here, such as rice cakes and ramen, are essential for "bubbling up" your units without spending your hard-earned gems on expensive shop items.

| Resource Type | Estimated Rate (Per Hour) | Primary Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gems** | 500 | Summoning new units and shop purchases |
| **Gold (Money)** | Variable (High) | Upgrading units and buying fusion crystals |
| **Unit Food** | 10 - 20 Pieces | Leveling up heroes quickly |
| **Experience** | Passive Growth | Increasing player and hero rank |

> **💡 Tip:** Always enter the AFK Dojo before logging off for the day. The cumulative gold and gems you earn overnight can often fund a full 10-pull on the next morning's banner rotation.

## Maximizing Returns in the Nightmare Circus

If you prefer active farming over passive waiting, the Nightmare Circus is your best destination. This daily activity can be performed three times per day and functions as an endless wave survival mode. The further you progress, the higher the rewards, making it a premier method for **Summon Heroes how to farm gold** and gems in short bursts.

A well-optimized team can reach wave 130 or higher in just about four minutes, yielding upwards of 300 gems per run. When multiplied by three daily attempts, this provides nearly 1,000 gems a day just from this single mode. To succeed here, you should focus on units with high Area of Effect (AoE) damage to clear the initial 100 waves as fast as possible.

## Dungeons and the Sweep System

For mid-to-late game players, the Sweep system is a revolutionary way to handle **Summon Heroes how to farm gold** and EXP. Once you have cleared a stage with a perfect rating, you can "sweep" it to instantly receive rewards without replaying the entire battle. This is best utilized in high-level areas like **Calamity Canyon** or **Sakura Town**.

Dungeons operate on a 6-hour refresh cycle. Completing these four times a day is mandatory for anyone serious about top-tier progression. Unlike standard stages, dungeons provide mythic shards—averaging about eight shards per day. At this rate, a dedicated player can guarantee a mythic unit every week just through consistent dungeon clears.

### Top Farming Stages for 2026

1.  **Calamity Canyon (Stage 4):** The gold standard for mythic treats and high-tier Ramen. Sweeping this stage can grant over 3 million EXP and significant gold.
2.  **Sakura Town (Kitsune Stages):** Ideal for farming Tacos, which provide a massive boost to unit levels.
3.  **Dungeon Gauntlets:** These should be cleared every 6 hours to maximize gem and shard income.

## PvP Strategy: Farming Tokens and Trait Rerolls

While gold and gems are the primary currencies, **Trait Rerolls** are arguably the most valuable endgame resource. These allow you to customize your heroes' stats to reach peak efficiency. The most effective way to farm these is through the PvP Arena.

By participating in PvP, you earn PvP Tokens. These tokens can be spent in a specialized shop that refreshes every 10 minutes. High-level players often "AFK farm" PvP by staying in the lobby and maintaining win streaks. A win streak of 100+ can generate enough tokens to buy dozens of trait rerolls in a single afternoon.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Your win streak resets whenever you re-enter the PvP lobby. To maximize your token gains, try to stay in the same session for as long as possible.

## The Item Shop and Fusion Economy

A major part of understanding **Summon Heroes how to farm gold** is knowing how to spend it. The Item Shop in the main hub resets every 5 minutes. This is where you should spend your accumulated gold on Fusion Crystals and Summon Tickets.

Fusion is a critical mechanic. Instead of needing identical units to rank up, you can use any unit of the same star rarity. For example, any 4-star unit can be fused into your primary 4-star Dragoon to elevate it to 5-star status. Because of this, you should **never auto-sell rare or epic units**. Every "trash" unit you pull is actually a valuable fusion material that saves you thousands of gems in the long run.

### Item Shop Priority List
*   **Fusion Crystals:** Always buy these; they are the bottleneck for unit progression.
*   **Summon Tickets:** High value for gold; essentially free summons.
*   **Rice Cakes/Ramen:** Buy only if you are actively pushing a new unit to max level.
*   **Mythic Shards:** Rare appearances; buy immediately regardless of cost.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way for a beginner in Summon Heroes to farm gold?**

A: The fastest way for beginners is a combination of the Nightmare Circus (3x daily) and staying in the AFK Dojo. The Dojo provides a steady stream of gold and gems even when you aren't playing, while the Circus gives a quick injection of currency to help you buy your first few 10-pulls.

**Q: Should I use the auto-sell feature for Rare units?**

A: No, you should avoid auto-selling units. In Summon Heroes, any unit of the same star rarity can be used as fusion material. Using "junk" units to upgrade your main heroes is much more cost-effective than selling them for a small amount of gold.

**Q: How often does the Item Shop refresh?**

A: The Item Shop refreshes every 5 minutes. It is highly recommended to check the shop frequently to stock up on Fusion Crystals and Summon Tickets, which are essential for long-term progression.

**Q: How do I get more Trait Rerolls?**

A: While you get a few from daily missions, the best way to farm Trait Rerolls is through PvP Tokens. By participating in PvP and maintaining a win streak, you can earn tokens to spend in the PvP shop, which refreshes its inventory every 10 minutes.
