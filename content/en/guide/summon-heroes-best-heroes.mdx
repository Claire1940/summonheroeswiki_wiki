## Understanding the Summoning System

To get the **summon heroes best heroes**, you need to master the banner system. The game features two distinct packs that rotate every 30 minutes. Each pack contains a specific set of units, and knowing when to spend your gems is vital for efficient progression.

### Banner Odds and Costs
Each summon costs **100 gems**, while a 10-pull costs **1,000 gems**. The rarity distribution on a standard banner is as follows:

| Rarity | Number of Units | Drop Chance |
|---|---|---|
| **Mythic** | 1 | **0.1%** |
| **Legendary** | 1 | Pity at 100 Summons |
| **Epic** | 2 | Moderate |
| **Rare** | 3 | High |

The pity system is a lifesaver for players with poor RNG. If you perform 100 summons on a single banner, you are guaranteed a Legendary unit. However, be warned: **pity does not carry over between different banners.** If you start summoning on Banner A, you must finish your pity there; otherwise, your progress toward a guaranteed Legendary will be lost when the banner rotates.

## How to Manage and Upgrade Your Heroes

Once you have secured some of the **summon heroes best heroes**, your next step is to make them stronger. Summon Heroes uses a unique fusion system that allows you to sacrifice lower-tier units to boost your favorites.

### The Fusion Mechanic
Unlike other games that require duplicate units, Summon Heroes allows you to fuse any units of the same star rarity. For example, if you have a four-star Dragoon, you can use any other four-star units you own as "fodder" to upgrade it to a five-star unit.

*   **Avoid Auto-Selling:** Many players make the mistake of auto-selling Rare and Epic units for quick cash. Do not do this. These units are essential "fusion food" for your S-tier heroes.
*   **Fusion Crystals:** These are specialized items used to facilitate the fusion process. You can find these in the Item Shop located in the main hub.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Item Shop resets every 5 minutes. If you see high-rarity fusion crystals or summon tickets, buy them immediately before the stock refreshes.

## Resource Farming: Gems and Food

You cannot summon the **summon heroes best heroes** without a steady supply of gems. In 2026, there are three primary ways to farm resources effectively:

1.  **Nightmare Circus:** This is a daily activity that can be performed three times a day. It is an endless wave mode where rewards scale based on how far you progress. Reaching level 130 can net you approximately 300 gems in just four minutes.
2.  **AFK Mode:** Located in the main dojo, this mode allows you to earn rewards while away from your computer. You can expect to earn roughly **500 gems per hour**, along with a significant amount of food to level up your units.
3.  **Item Shop Sniping:** Keep an eye on the shop for "Rice Cakes" and other food items. These are the most efficient way to "bubble up" your units and reach max level quickly.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the summon heroes best heroes for beginners?**

A: For new players, the **Bear Tamer** is the best choice. It is a Rare unit, making it very easy to obtain, yet its damage output rivals many Legendary units. Pair it with a **Mermaid** for excellent AOE coverage.

**Q: Does the pity system reset if I get a Legendary early?**

A: Yes, if you pull a Legendary unit before hitting 100 summons, the pity counter for that specific banner will reset to zero.

**Q: How do I get more fusion crystals?**

A: Fusion crystals can be purchased from the Item Shop in the main hub. Since the shop resets every 5 minutes, it is recommended to check back frequently to stock up on different rarities.

**Q: Is the AFK mode worth using?**

A: Absolutely. Earning 500 gems per hour passively is one of the most reliable ways to build up your currency for 10-pulls on the premium banners. It is the best way to ensure you always have enough gems when a top-tier unit like the Reaper appears on rotation.
