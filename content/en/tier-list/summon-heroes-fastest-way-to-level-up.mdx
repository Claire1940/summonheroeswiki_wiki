## Preparing Your Heroes for the Grind

Before you can dive into the high-level stages that grant massive experience, your team needs to be properly "tuned." Leveling your account is intrinsically tied to your units' ability to clear stages quickly. If a stage takes ten minutes to clear, your experience-per-hour will plummet.

### Summoning the Right Units
While mythical units are the ultimate goal, you do not need them to start the **summon heroes fastest way to level up** process. Focus on obtaining high-tier Legendaries or even reliable Epics like the **Slime Summoner** or **Demon Hunter**. These units provide the consistent AOE (Area of Effect) damage necessary to wipe waves of enemies in Story Mode.

### Stat Re-rolling and Traits
Damage is the only metric that truly matters when speed-leveling. You should visit the stat re-roll NPC immediately upon summoning a core team member. Your goal is to achieve an **X Rank** in the Attack stat.

| Stat Type | Priority | Target Rank | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Attack** | **Highest** | **X** | Maximizes raw DPS for faster wave clears. |
| **Speed** | Medium | A or S | Reduces time between attacks. |
| **Health** | Low | Any | Generally unnecessary if your damage is high enough. |
| **Critical** | Medium | S | Provides burst damage for boss rooms. |

> **💡 Tip:** Don't waste resources trying to get X rank on every stat. Lock the Attack stat once you hit X and move on to the next hero to save your currency.

## Unit Fusion and Feeding: The Instant Level Boost

While AFK grinding is the best way to level your account, you can level individual heroes instantly using the Fusion and Feeding systems. This is vital for preparing a weak unit to lead an AFK farm.

### Feeding Fruit
As you play, you will accumulate various fruits. These are essentially XP potions.
*   Navigate to the **Units** menu.
*   Select the hero you wish to upgrade.
*   Use your stored food to jump-start their level.
*   **Warning:** Do not spread your food across too many units. Focus on one "Carry" unit that can solo stages.

### The Fusion System
Fusion allows you to sacrifice unwanted NPCs to boost the base stats of your main team. This doesn't just increase their level; it increases the multiplier of their stats. A level 100 unit with high fusion stacks will vastly outperform a level 140 unit with zero fusion.

| Fusion Material | Stat Increase | Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Common NPCs** | +0.1% - 0.5% | Use for early-game padding. |
| **Rare NPCs** | +1.0% - 2.0% | Main source of fusion fodder. |
| **Legendary Duplicates** | +5.0%+ | Save for your primary DPS unit only. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Fusion is permanent. Ensure you are not accidentally sacrificing a unit you intended to use in a secondary team.
