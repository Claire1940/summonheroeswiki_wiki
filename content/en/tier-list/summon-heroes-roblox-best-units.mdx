## A-Tier: Reliable Mid-to-Late Game Anchors

A-tier units are the backbone of most successful rosters. While they might not "one-shot" entire waves like the S-tier gods, they are reliable anchors for sustained tower defense. These units are often used by top players to fill specific niches or to provide elemental coverage that S-tier units might lack.

*   **Diver:** A water-based unit that excels in aquatic or hybrid maps. His dive ability allows him to close gaps and initiate massive clears, making him a versatile pick for boss defense.
*   **Captain Dreadmore:** A pirate-themed unit that provides cannon support. He is excellent for "trail" strategies where you chain waves together for maximum efficiency.
*   **Laser Cyborg:** For those who prefer precision, the Laser Cyborg offers beam attacks that can snipe high-priority targets from across the map.
*   **Mermaid:** Her charm ability is a game-changer for crowd control. By turning enemies against each other, she reduces the pressure on your base during chaotic waves.

## B-Tier and Below: What to Reroll

In the competitive landscape of [Summon Heroes on Roblox](https://www.roblox.com/games/), keeping low-tier units is a recipe for frustration. While units like the **Ice Mage** or **Fire Mage** offer decent elemental effects (slow and burn, respectively), they lack the scaling necessary for high-level play.

**Units to Reroll ASAP:**
1.  **Bandit Nick:** His gold-stealing gimmick is too minor to justify his lack of damage.
2.  **Swordsman:** A basic melee unit that gets crushed by any boss with a range or AOE attack.
3.  **Archer:** While safe due to range, the lack of AOE means you will eventually be overwhelmed by swarms.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not over-invest in C-tier units. They may help you clear the first few maps, but they will fail you the moment you step into Sunset City or attempt infinite runs.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the summon heroes roblox best units for beginners?**

A: For beginners, the **Diver** and **Captain Dreadmore** are excellent A-tier picks that are easier to pull than legendaries but still provide enough power to clear early and mid-game maps.

**Q: How do I get more gems for summoning?**

A: The best way to farm gems is by completing the Sunset City stages and checking the new Unit Index. Every new unit you collect grants a gem reward. Additionally, participating in PvP can net you shards and gems depending on your rank.

**Q: Is Reaper still better than the new Legendary units?**

A: In terms of raw AOE and infinite wave scaling, **Reaper** is still widely considered the best unit. However, **Frame Rate** is superior for single-target boss encounters, making a combination of both the ideal setup.

**Q: What does the level 120 cap increase mean for my units?**

A: The increased cap means enemies have significantly more health in later stages. You will need to focus on units with "percentage-based damage" or those that can scale their attack power throughout a match, such as the Demon Knight or Reaper.
