## Top Tier Unit Deep Dive

Understanding the **summon hero roblox tier list** requires looking beyond simple letter grades. The "X" and "S" tier units possess unique mechanics that allow them to bypass standard gameplay hurdles.

### Matriarch and BET: The Powerhouse Pair
**Matriarch** stands at the pinnacle of the meta because of her versatility. She provides enough raw power to clear waves while maintaining relevance in boss encounters. On the other hand, **BET** is the undisputed king of health. If you are struggling with story progression or high-damage bosses, BET serves as the ultimate meat shield, allowing your backline DPS to fire without interruption.

### Divine: The Ranged Specialist
**Divine** is a specialized ranged unit that focuses heavily on ability usage. Unlike units that rely on basic attack cycles, Divine's kit is designed to inflict massive burst damage. This makes Divine particularly effective in PvP where burst damage can delete an opponent's unit before they have a chance to react.

### Ranger: The Boss Killer
The **Ranger** is a significant milestone in the game's development as the first unit to introduce Damage Over Time (DOT) mechanics. With homing projectiles and impressive range, the Ranger can "burn down" high-HP targets. This makes him an essential pick for boss raids and any content where the enemy has a massive health pool.

## Understanding Unit Roles and Mechanics

To effectively use a **summon hero roblox tier list**, you must understand the roles assigned to each character. Placing a ranged unit in a melee position or vice versa will result in a quick defeat regardless of the unit's tier.

*   **Academy Units:** Generally focus on close-range DPS. While some are low-tier, they are meant to be your primary damage dealers in the very early stages.
*   **Archers/Rangers:** These are your ranged specialists. They should always be placed behind a tank to maximize their uptime.
*   **Banders:** Melee DPS units that typically have higher health than Academy units but less than dedicated Tanks.
*   **Bruisers:** A hybrid role (like the Construct) that balances survivability with moderate damage output.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid units with "Animation Lock." Some high-rarity units, such as Frame Rate, have long attack animations that leave them vulnerable. These units often rush into combat only to die before their attack connects.

## How to Optimize Your Team Build

Building a balanced team is more important than simply stacking S-tier units. A common mistake is ignored the synergy between health and damage. A team of five glass-cannon DPS units will likely fail against a balanced team that includes a dedicated tank like **BET**.

### The Ideal Team Composition
For most content on the [official Roblox platform](https://www.roblox.com/), a balanced team should consist of:
1.  **One Main Tank:** (e.g., BET or Construct) to soak up initial damage.
2.  **Two Primary DPS:** (e.g., Divine, Seraph, or Matriarch) to clear the field.
3.  **One Boss Killer:** (e.g., Ranger) to handle high-health targets.
4.  **One Flex Unit:** This can be a secondary tank, a support unit, or an additional ranged DPS depending on the specific stage requirements.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the best overall unit in the summon hero roblox tier list?**

A: Currently, **Matriarch** and **BET** are considered the best. Matriarch provides elite damage and utility, while BET offers the highest health pool in the game, making him the ultimate tank for any game mode.

**Q: Should I keep Legendary units even if they are ranked low?**

A: It depends. While a unit like **Frame Rate** is ranked poorly due to slow animations, future balance patches could buff these units. However, for immediate progression, you are better off using high-tier Epic units like the **Construct** or **Thief**.

**Q: What is the first unit I should aim to replace?**

A: The **Ice Mage** and the **Academy** witch are starter units with very low scaling. You should aim to replace them with any Epic-tier unit as soon as you have enough gems for a few summons.
