## Understanding Unit Roles and Grading

Before jumping into the rankings, it is essential to understand how units are categorized. In Summon Heroes, every unit belongs to a specific "Academy" or role that dictates their behavior on the battlefield. 

| Role | Description | Primary Stat Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **Academy** | Close-range DPS units with high burst potential. | Attack & Speed |
| **Archer** | Ranged DPS units that provide consistent fire from afar. | Range & Cooldown |
| **Bander** | Traditional melee DPS units that fight on the front lines. | Attack & Health |
| **Bruiser** | Tanky melee units designed to soak up damage while counter-attacking. | Health & Defense |
| **Sage** | Specialized magic units often providing utility or area damage. | Ability Power |

We use a grading system ranging from **X (Core/God Tier)** to **S (Unusable/Trash Tier)**. While it might seem confusing that "S" is sometimes used for bad units in certain community lists, for the purposes of this guide, we will follow the standard industry ranking where X/S+ are the best, and D/F are the worst.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Just because a unit has a high rarity (Legendary or Mythical) does not automatically make it good. Some Legendary units suffer from "Animation Lock," making them vulnerable to faster enemies.

## Top Tier Unit Analysis

To truly master the **Roblox Summon Heroes tier list**, you need to know why these specific units sit at the top. Here is a breakdown of the "Big Four" and why they are essential for any serious player.

### Divine (Mythical - Ranged)
Divine is arguably the most versatile unit in the game. Specializing in ability usage, Divine can inflict massive area-of-effect (AoE) damage that clears waves of enemies in seconds. If you are struggling with crowd control in high-level PvE, Divine is the solution.

### BET (Mythical - Tank)
BET holds the record for the highest health pool in the game. As a premier tank, BET is indispensable for story progression where bosses hit incredibly hard. Unlike other tanks that deal negligible damage, BET maintains a respectable DPS, ensuring they contribute to the fight while soaking up hits.

### Seraph (Mythical - Support/DPS)
Seraph provides a blend of high-speed attacks and utility. In the 2026 meta, speed is everything. Seraph's ability to cycle through attacks quickly allows your team to maintain pressure on both players and AI mobs.

### Ranger (Legendary - Boss Killer)
The Ranger is a unique entry in the **Roblox Summon Heroes tier list** because it was the first unit introduced with Damage Over Time (DOT) mechanics. This makes the Ranger the ultimate boss killer. By applying burns that scale with the enemy's health, the Ranger can take down massive targets much faster than traditional "burst" units.

> **💡 Tip:** Always pair a high-health unit like BET with a DOT unit like Ranger for the most efficient boss-hunting team.

## Best Epic Units for Early Game

Not everyone will pull a Mythical unit on their first day. Fortunately, there are two Epic units that punch far above their weight class. If you see these in your inventory, do not discard them.

1. **Construct:** A melee bruiser that is incredibly bulky. For an Epic unit, its ability to tank damage is comparable to some lower-tier Legendaries. It is a perfect "budget" version of BET.
2. **Thief:** This unit is a melee specialist with one of the fastest attack speeds in the game. While it is somewhat niche due to its low health, its raw DPS in PvP can catch opponents off guard.

## Units to Avoid: The "Traps"

It is a common mistake for new players to assume a Legendary unit is always better than an Epic. In the current [Roblox environment](https://www.roblox.com), balancing updates can sometimes leave older units behind.

*   **Frame Rate (Legendary):** Despite its high rarity, Frame Rate is currently ranked poorly (B-Tier). The primary issue is "Animation Lock." The unit's attack animations take so long to complete that it often takes unnecessary damage or misses moving targets entirely.
*   **Academy Witch:** This unit has consistently stayed at the bottom of the list. With low health and underwhelming damage, it provides almost no value to a competitive team.

## How to Optimize Your Team Composition

Building a team based solely on the **Roblox Summon Heroes tier list** is a good start, but synergy is what wins games. A balanced team should generally follow this template:

*   **1 Main Tank:** (e.g., BET or Construct) to hold the frontline.
*   **2 Primary DPS:** (e.g., Divine for AoE and Ranger for single-target).
*   **1 Utility/Speed Unit:** (e.g., Seraph or Thief) to clean up stragglers.

Before finalizing your team, always check the "Unit Data" section in the game's index. This provides specific stats on attack range, cooldowns, and ability scaling. Use this data to ensure your units don't have overlapping weaknesses, such as all being "Slow" or all having "Short Range."

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the best overall unit in Summon Heroes?**

A: Currently, **Divine** is considered the best overall unit due to its massive AoE damage and ability scaling. However, for pure tanking, **BET** is the undisputed champion.

**Q: Is the starter Ice Mage worth keeping?**

A: The Ice Mage is a surprisingly competent A-tier unit for beginners. While you will eventually want to replace it with a Mythical unit from the **Roblox Summon Heroes tier list**, it can easily carry you through the first few worlds.

**Q: What does "Animation Lock" mean?**

A: Animation lock occurs when a unit is stuck in an attack or movement animation and cannot perform another action until it finishes. Units like **Frame Rate** suffer from this, making them slower and more vulnerable than their stats might suggest.

**Q: How do I get more Mythical units?**

A: Mythical units are obtained through the summoning system using gems or coins. Participating in limited-time events and completing daily quests are the most reliable ways to farm the resources needed for high-tier summons.
