## Top-Tier Legendary Units Ranked

In the current **summon heroes roblox meta**, not all legendary units are created equal. While the rarity suggests power, the actual utility in specific game modes like PvP or Defense Dungeons varies.

### 1. Reaper (The S-Tier Carry)
Reaper remains the undisputed king of wave clearing. His ability to summon spirits and perform wide-range crowd-controlling slashes makes him indispensable for clearing dense enemy stages. If you are looking to farm gems quickly by smashing through worlds, Reaper is your primary target.

### 2. Bear Tamer (The Utility Tank)
Bear Tamer is often considered the most consistent unit in the game. By summoning tanky bears, this unit provides a frontline that absorbs damage, allowing your high-DPS units to stay protected. Bear Tamer is one of the few units that transitions perfectly from the first world all the way to Sunset City.

### 3. Frame Rate (Single-Target Specialist)
Added in the most recent update, Frame Rate has quickly become a favorite for boss hunting. His attack speed is incredibly high, and his special move involves a dashing punch flurry that deals massive single-target damage. He is the perfect counter to the high-health bosses found in the level 100+ stages.

> **💡 Tip:** Pair Frame Rate with a CC unit like Reaper to keep bosses stationary while Frame Rate delivers his high-speed special attacks.

## Early Game Progression Strategy

For new players, the **summon heroes roblox meta** can seem daunting due to the sheer number of units. The "trap" many beginners fall into is over-investing in common or rare units. While these are necessary for the tutorial, they should be replaced as soon as possible.

### Recommended Starter Team
*   **Bear Tamer:** Aim for this unit early for frontline control.
*   **Dragoon Isadora:** Provides solid mid-range damage.
*   **Captain Dreadmore:** Excellent for early-game AOE.

As you progress, use your common units as fusion fodder. Fusion is the process of combining duplicates to increase a unit's star rating. A 5-star Epic unit will often outperform a 1-star Legendary unit, so do not ignore the power of fusion during your first 50 levels.

## PvP and Competitive Rankings

For those looking to dominate the PvP leaderboards, the meta is slightly different than the story mode. In PvP, speed and burst damage are the most critical factors. The top 10 players on the server receive five Mythic shards daily, which is the only reliable way to unlock Mythic-tier units without heavy spending.

**PvP Team Composition Tips:**
*   **Speed is King:** Units with short cooldowns on their specials usually win trades.
*   **Targeting:** Use single-target specialists like Frame Rate to pick off the enemy's carry unit first.
*   **Daily Rewards:** Even if you aren't in the top 10, staying in the top 100 provides two Mythic shards, which is vital for long-term growth.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best unit in the summon heroes roblox meta right now?**

A: Currently, Reaper is considered the best overall unit due to his incredible AOE damage and crowd control. However, for single-target boss damage, the new legendary unit Frame Rate is a top contender.

**Q: How do I get more Mythic shards?**

A: Mythic shards are primarily earned through PvP rankings. The top 10 players receive five shards daily, while players ranked 11 through 100 receive two shards. Consistency in the arena is key to unlocking Mythic heroes.

**Q: Should I use my gems on every banner?**

A: No. The banners rotate every 30 minutes. It is highly recommended to save your gems and only pull when a top-tier legendary like Reaper, Bear Tamer, or Techno Master is featured to ensure you are following the most efficient progression path.

**Q: What does the Sunset code give you?**

A: Redeeming the code `Sunset` provides five legendary fusion crystals, which are essential for increasing the star rating of your legendary units and pushing your level cap toward 120.
