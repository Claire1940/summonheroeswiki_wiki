## Progression Strategy: From Beginner to Master

One of the most common mistakes new players make is holding onto "Rare" or "Common" units for too long. In Summon Heroes, these units serve better as fusion fodder. To reach the leaderboard, you must embrace the fusion system to boost your stars.

1.  **Save Your Gems:** Banners rotate every 30 minutes. Do not pull on low-value banners; wait for the **summon heroes top picks** like Reaper to appear.
2.  **Use Active Codes:** Developers frequently release codes to help players catch up. Currently, the codes `legend` and `delay heroes` provide essential crystals and tickets.
3.  **Fuse Duplicates:** Stats jump exponentially when you increase a unit's star level. A 5-star Bear Tamer will often outperform a base-level legendary unit.
4.  **Farm Densely:** Focus your farming efforts on stages with the highest enemy density to maximize gem and experience gains per hour.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid rushing low-value pulls. The game features a pity system that guarantees better rewards over time—staying consistent with your pulls is better than sporadic spending.
