## The Absolute Best: X and S Tier Units

The top of the **summon heros tier list** is occupied by units that offer unique mechanics, such as Damage Over Time (DOT) or massive area-of-effect (AoE) bursts. These units are the backbone of any successful endgame account.

### Divine (Mythical/Legendary)
Divine remains a powerhouse in 2026. As a ranged unit specializing in ability usage, Divine can inflict massive damage from a safe distance. Her ability to clear waves in PvE makes her a staple for speed-running dungeons.

### BET (The Ultimate Tank)
If you are struggling with survivability, BET is the answer. Boasting the highest health pool in the game, BET serves as a primary tank for any story progression. Unlike other tanks that offer zero utility, BET maintains respectable DPS while soaking up damage.

### Ranger (The Boss Killer)
Ranger is a standout on the **summon heros tier list** because he was the first unit to introduce DOT (Damage Over Time) mechanics. His ability to burn down bosses quickly while utilizing homing projectiles makes him indispensable for high-level raids.

## Competitive Choices: A-Tier Units

A-Tier units are the "bread and butter" for most players. They are more accessible than the ultra-rare X-tier units but still provide enough power to clear the majority of the game's content.

### Thief and Construct (The Best Epics)
Interestingly, the **summon heros tier list** features two Epic units that often outperform Legendaries. The **Thief** is a melee unit with incredibly fast attack speed, making him a DPS monster in both PvP and PvE. The **Construct**, on the other hand, is a melee bruiser. He is bulky enough to tank several hits while dealing significant damage back to the enemy.

### Sage (Ranged Support/DPS)
Sage is a Legendary unit that holds a solid A-rank. While her DPS increases as enemies get closer, her primary value lies in her ability usage which keeps her relevant even when the meta shifts toward faster units.

## Units to Avoid: The "Trap" Heroes

Not every high-rarity pull is a win. Some units have fundamental flaws in their design that make them a liability on the battlefield.

1. **Frame Rate (Legendary):** Despite his high rarity, he is frequently ranked low on the **summon heros tier list**. His damage is underwhelming, but his biggest flaw is "animation lock." He often rushes into groups of enemies only to die because he cannot move during his attack animations.
2. **Academy Witch:** Widely considered the worst unit in the game. Her range and damage are abysmal compared to even basic starter units like the Ice Mage.
3. **Ice Mage:** While a decent starter, she should be replaced as soon as you acquire your first Epic unit.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid spending your rare upgrade materials on Frame Rate. Even if he is your only Legendary, a high-level Thief or Construct will serve you much better in the long run.

## Understanding Unit Roles and Stats

To truly master the **summon heros tier list**, you must understand the different roles units play within a team. A team of five DPS units will likely fail in harder stages because they lack a frontline.

### Roles Breakdown
*   **Academy:** Close-range DPS units that require high health to survive.
*   **Archer/Ranger:** Ranged DPS units that focus on single-target or DOT damage.
*   **Bander:** Melee DPS units that usually have balanced stats.
*   **Bruiser:** A hybrid between a tank and a DPS, capable of holding the frontline.
*   **Tank:** High-health units designed to pull aggro and protect squishy backline units.

## How to Build a Balanced Team

Using the **summon heros tier list** effectively means creating synergy between your units. A standard "Meta" team in 2026 usually follows this structure:

1. **One Main Tank:** (e.g., BET) to soak up initial damage.
2. **One Bruiser:** (e.g., Construct) to provide a secondary frontline.
3. **Two Ranged DPS:** (e.g., Divine and Seraph) to clear enemies from a distance.
4. **One Boss Killer/Utility:** (e.g., Ranger) to handle high-health targets with DOT.

| Stat | Importance | Best For... |
|---|---|---|
| **Attack** | High | DPS and Boss Killers |
| **Health** | High | Tanks and Bruisers |
| **Cooldown** | Medium | Ability-reliant units like Divine |
| **Speed** | Low/Medium | Melee units needing to close gaps |

## Unit Data and Hidden Mechanics

When looking at the **summon heros tier list**, experienced players also look at the "Unit Data" tab in-game. This section reveals the attack speed and specific ability modifiers that aren't always obvious from the rarity alone. For example, Divine's ability usage is her main selling point, allowing her to inflict massive burst damage that exceeds her base attack stats.

Similarly, understanding "Homing" mechanics is vital. Units like Ranger have projectiles that follow targets, ensuring that no damage is wasted even if the enemy moves. This makes them significantly more reliable than units with "straight-line" projectiles that can easily miss mobile bosses.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the best overall unit in the summon heros tier list?**

A: Currently, **Divine** and **Matriarch** are tied for the top spot. Divine offers the best ranged burst damage, while Matriarch provides incredible utility and consistent DPS across all game modes.

**Q: Is it worth keeping Epic units if I have Legendaries?**

A: Yes! Units like **Thief** and **Construct** are often better than several Legendary units (like Frame Rate) due to their faster attack speeds and better survivability mechanics.

**Q: What does "animation lock" mean?**

A: Animation lock occurs when a unit is stuck in an attack sequence and cannot move or react to enemy attacks. This is a major weakness for units like Frame Rate, who often take unnecessary damage while performing their skills.

**Q: How often is the summon heros tier list updated?**

A: The meta typically shifts with every major seasonal update or when new Mythical units are introduced. Always check the unit data index after a patch to see if any base stats have been buffed or nerfed.

**Q: What is the best way to get S-tier units?**

A: Saving your gems for "Banners" that feature Divine or Ranger is the most efficient strategy. Avoid pulling on general banners if you are looking for specific high-tier heroes.
