## S-Tier: The God-Tier Conquerors

The units in this category represent the pinnacle of power in Summon Heroes. If you manage to roll any of these, your account value increases exponentially. These heroes are characterized by their ability to obliterate waves and sustain massive DPS during boss chains.

### Reaper
The Reaper remains the undisputed king of the 2026 meta. Utilizing massive scythe swings that deal god-tier AOE damage, the Reaper consumes enemy health to fuel its own power growth. In a PvP context, the Reaper's ability to clear lanes faster than any other unit forces opponents into a defensive panic.
*   **Best For:** High-wave chaos and aggressive lane pressure.
*   **Key Vibe:** Dark, regal dominance that scales with kills.

### Bear Tamer
The Bear Tamer is a specialized beast hero that utilizes bear summons to nuke enemy packs from a distance. Unlike melee-reliant units, the Bear Tamer provides "bear judgment" from afar, allowing for flexible placement strategies that can bypass traditional enemy barrages.
*   **Best For:** Solo pushes and infinite map runs.
*   **Key Vibe:** Strategic phantom tamer with high burst potential.

### Demon Knight
The Demon Knight is the ultimate combo DPS unit. By applying demon buffs that echo through the match, this hero can shatter even the most tanky foes. When awakened, the Demon Knight turns the map into a "legend zone," where enemies vanish under historic might.

### Seraph and Divine
These two units function as the ultimate support-attack hybrids. Seraph provides holy buffs that act as a heavenly guardian for your other towers, while Divine offers supreme buffs that elevate every unit on the board to godhood.
*   **Synergy Tip:** Pairing Seraph or Divine with high-speed towers creates a "holy route" defense that is nearly impossible to leak.

> **💡 Expert Tip:** S-tier heroes are your multipliers. Even a single S-tier unit can outperform a full board of B-tier units due to their superior scaling and AOE capabilities.

## A-Tier: The Competitive Anchors

A-tier heroes are the backbone of most high-ranking players' squads. While they may not have the "one-shot" potential of a Reaper, they provide the consistency needed to hold deep lanes and compete on mid-level leaderboards.

### Diver and Mermaid
The Diver and Mermaid represent the best of the "utility" meta. The Diver’s ability to move through underwater battlegrounds allows for dynamic unit drops, while the Mermaid’s charm ability can turn a losing lane into a winning one by alluring enemy units into traps.

### Laser Cyborg
For players who prefer precision over raw AOE, the Laser Cyborg is the go-to choice. Its beam attacks are designed for futuristic precision, making it excellent for focusing down high-priority targets that other units might struggle to finish off.

## B-Tier: Reliable But Predictable

B-tier units are the "stepping stones" of Summon Heroes. They offer enough power to survive mid-tier maps and farm gems, but they lack the burst or tankiness required for the absolute highest levels of play.

*   **Ice Mage:** Excellent for mid-wave control with freeze effects, but the "slow" utility often falls off when enemies gain immunity or high resistance in later waves.
*   **Fire Mage:** Provides a decent burn DOT (Damage Over Time), which is great for clearing trash mobs, but fails to provide the burst damage needed for 2026 boss fights.
*   **Archer:** A safe, long-range pick for beginners. However, the lack of AOE means it is easily looped by swarms of enemies.

## C-Tier: The "Reroll Immediately" Tier

Units in the C-tier are generally considered "trash rolls." They offer basic damage with no real flare or utility, leaving you exposed to leaks almost immediately.

*   **Swordsman:** A basic melee unit that feels like a "toothpick" in boss fights. It lacks range and sustain.
*   **Bandit Nick:** While it offers minor "steal" mechanics for gold gain, the damage output is so low that you will likely lose the base before the gold even matters.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not waste your upgrade materials on C-tier units. It is much more efficient to save your resources for an A-tier or S-tier summon.
