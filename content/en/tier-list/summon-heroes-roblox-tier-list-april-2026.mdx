## A-Tier: Reliable Anchors

A-Tier heroes are the backbone of any competitive squad. While they might not possess the "one-shot" potential of an S-Tier monster, they offer incredible utility and sustained damage that makes mid-to-late maps manageable. Most leaderboard players use these units to fill specialized roles in their team compositions.

| Hero Name | Element/Type | Specialization | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Diver** | Water | Aquatic Gap Closing | Boss Defenses |
| **Captain Dreadmore** | Pirate | Cannon Support | Lane Chaining |
| **Mermaid** | Siren | Charm/Crowd Control | Swarm Management |
| **Laser Cyborg** | Tech | Precision Beams | Single Target DPS |

### Diver and Captain Dreadmore
The Diver is a unique unit that rewards aggressive placement. By closing gaps quickly, the Diver can initiate massive clears that set up the rest of your towers for success. Similarly, Captain Dreadmore provides naval-style cannon support that excels in dark maps, where his trails can chain multiple waves together for consistent gold farming.

### Mermaid and Laser Cyborg
The Mermaid is your go-to for crowd control. Her charm ability essentially turns the map into a siren battleground, slowing down high-speed threats. On the other hand, the Laser Cyborg offers the precision needed to take down high-health armored targets that your AOE units might struggle to finish.

## B-Tier: The Mid-Game Fillers

B-Tier units are decent picks that you will likely use while hunting for better rolls. They provide niche effects like ice slows or fire burns, which are helpful for clearing starter maps and beginning your gem farm. However, they lack the scaling necessary to survive the pressure of infinite waves.

*   **Ice Mage:** Provides essential freeze effects. Great for noobs, but enemies in higher tiers often shrug off the ice.
*   **Fire Mage:** Focuses on Damage Over Time (DOT). It offers a destructive vibe for early trash packs but lacks the burst damage required for bosses.
*   **Archer:** A safe, long-range pick. While precise, the Archer lacks AOE, meaning it can be easily looped by large swarms.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not over-invest your resources into B-Tier units. They are temporary placeholders and should be swapped for A or S-Tier heroes as soon as your gem count allows.
