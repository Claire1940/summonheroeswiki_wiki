## A-Tier: Reliable Anchors

A-tier heroes are the backbone of any successful mid-to-late game squad. While they might not "one-shot" every boss like the S-tier monsters, they offer incredible utility and consistent damage that can carry you through the most difficult standard maps. On this **summon heroes roblox pve tier list**, these are the units you keep and invest in until you hit the jackpot with a god-tier roll.

*   **Diver:** A water-based unit that excels in aquatic or hybrid maps. Its "Dive" ability allows it to close gaps and reposition, making it highly effective against fast-moving leaks.
*   **Captain Dreadmore:** The pirate king of the meta. His cannon support provides long-range suppression that is perfect for farming gems on dark or naval-themed maps.
*   **Mermaid:** Utilizing a "Charm" mechanic, the Mermaid can temporarily turn enemies against each other or stall them in place, giving your heavy hitters time to recharge.
*   **Laser Cyborg:** For players who prefer precision, the Laser Cyborg offers beam attacks that pierce through multiple enemies, making it a top-tier choice for narrow corridors.

## B-Tier: The Niche Specialists

B-tier units are functional but often limited by their scaling. They are fantastic for beginners who are just starting to explore mid-tier maps, but they tend to fall off when faced with the sheer health pools of late-game bosses. They often provide "elemental" utility like freeze or burn, which can be useful in specific team compositions.

### Ice Mage vs. Fire Mage
The Ice Mage is often preferred over the Fire Mage due to its crowd control capabilities. In a game where "leaks" result in an instant loss, the ability to slow down a fast-moving wave is often more valuable than a small damage-over-time (DOT) burn. However, the Fire Mage can still find a place in "burn builds" where multiple units stack fire damage to melt high-defense targets.

### Archer
The Archer is the quintessential "safe" pick. With a reliable range and decent fire rate, it can handle early waves with ease. Its main drawback is the lack of significant AOE, meaning it can easily be overwhelmed by swarms in the later stages of a match.

## C-Tier: Starter Units and Reroll Bait

Units in the C-tier are generally considered "trash rolls." While they serve a purpose in the first ten minutes of the game, they lack the stats, abilities, and scaling necessary to compete in the broader **summon heroes roblox pve tier list**.

1.  **Swordsman:** A basic melee unit with a very short range. In a game where bosses can crush units in seconds, the Swordsman often feels like "placing a paper unit against legends."
2.  **Bandit Nick:** While his ability to "steal" small amounts of gold sounds useful for the early game, the actual gold gain is negligible compared to the damage you lose by not having a real hero in that slot.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not spend your upgrade materials on C-tier units. It is much more efficient to save your resources for an A-tier or S-tier hero that you will use for the long term.

## Advanced Strategies for Summoning

To truly master the **summon heroes roblox pve tier list**, you must understand the economy of summoning. Gems are your most valuable resource, and how you spend them determines your power ceiling.

### Gem Management and Rerolling
Top players rarely settle for their first few rolls. They often "save-scum" or grind starter maps repeatedly to accumulate enough gems for a multi-summon. The goal is to hit at least one A-tier anchor as quickly as possible. Once you have a reliable carry like the Laser Cyborg or Diver, you can begin farming higher-level maps to hunt for the Reaper.

### Tower Pairing Hacks
A hero is only as good as the towers surrounding them. For example, placing a **Demon Knight** near high-attack-speed towers allows his buffs to echo more frequently, effectively doubling the DPS of your entire frontline. Conversely, placing a **Reaper** at the very front of the map ensures he gets the first "harvest," allowing his damage to scale before the wave even reaches your main defense line.
