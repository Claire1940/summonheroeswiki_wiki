## Top Tier Units: The X and S Rank Elites

The following units represent the pinnacle of power in Summon Heroes. If you manage to pull these from a summon, they should become the cornerstone of your team immediately.

### Matriarch (X-Tier)
Matriarch sits at the top of the food chain. Her utility in both PvE and PvP is unmatched, offering a balance of survivability and devastating area-of-effect damage. She is widely considered a "must-have" for anyone looking to clear endgame content in 2026.

### Divine (S-Tier)
Divine is a specialized ranged unit that focuses on ability usage to inflict massive burst damage. Unlike standard archers, Divine relies on timing and skill synergy to clear waves of enemies. When positioned correctly, Divine can solo carry through most story mode chapters.

### BET (S-Tier)
If you need a frontline, BET is the premier choice. Known as the highest health unit currently available, BET serves as a massive tank for any story progression. Beyond just soaking up hits, BET also provides respectable DPS, ensuring that your frontline isn't just a passive wall but an active threat.

### Seraph (S-Tier)
Seraph offers high-tier utility and consistent damage. Often used in high-level PvP, Seraph's abilities allow for crowd control and sustained pressure that few other units can match.

## High-Performance Epic and Legendary Units

While the X-tier units are rare, several Epic and Legendary units provide incredible value and are often easier to obtain. Using this **tier list summon heroes** perspective, we can see that units like the Ranger and Construct are vital for mid-to-late game success.

### Ranger (S-Tier Legendary)
The Ranger is a unique unit because it was the first to introduce DOT (Damage Over Time) mechanics to the game. It is a premier boss killer. With its homing abilities and long range, the Ranger can burn down high-health targets from a safe distance, making it indispensable for boss raids.

### Construct (A-Tier Epic)
Don't let the "Epic" rarity fool you. The Construct is a melee bruiser that is incredibly bulky and capable of tanking significant damage while dishing out high DPS. For players who haven't secured a BET yet, the Construct is the best alternative for a sturdy frontline.

### Thief (A-Tier Epic)
The Thief is a fast-attacking melee unit. While it can be a bit niche, its high DPS in both PvP and PvE makes it a favorite for players who prefer a "glass cannon" playstyle. It excels at flanking and taking out squishy backline units in the arena.

## Units to Avoid: The "Trap" Picks

One of the most important aspects of a **tier list summon heroes** guide is knowing which units look good on paper but fail in practice. Some units have high rarities but suffer from mechanical flaws that make them a liability.

### Frame Rate (Legendary)
Despite being a Legendary unit, Frame Rate is currently ranked in B-tier. The primary issue is "animation lock." Frame Rate is too slow to react, often rushing into the fray only to die before completing an attack. His damage output is significantly lower than other Legendaries, making him a "trap" pick for unsuspecting players.

### Academy (F-Tier)
The Academy unit is widely regarded as the worst unit in the game. It is a close-range DPS unit that lacks the health to survive in melee range and the damage to justify the risk. Regardless of your level, replace the Academy unit as soon as you pull literally anything else.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not be fooled by rarity alone. A well-built Epic Construct will almost always outperform a Legendary Frame Rate in 2026.

## Understanding Unit Roles and Data

To master the **tier list summon heroes** rankings, you must understand how different roles interact. A balanced team usually consists of one dedicated tank, one or two ranged DPS units, and a utility or melee bruiser.

1. **Tanks (e.g., BET):** These units should always be placed at the front. Their goal is to absorb initial cooldowns and protect your ranged units.
2. **Ranged DPS (e.g., Divine, Sage):** Place these in the back. They often have high "animation lock" or low health, meaning they need a clear line of sight without being targeted.
3. **Boss Killers (e.g., Ranger):** These are specialized units. In levels with a single high-health enemy, prioritize these over AOE units.
4. **Bruisers (e.g., Construct):** These units are versatile. They can act as off-tanks or secondary damage dealers if your main tank falls.

## How to Optimize Your Team

Building a team based on a **tier list summon heroes** is only the first step. You also need to manage your resources effectively. In 2026, the cost of upgrading units has increased, meaning you should focus on a core team of five units rather than trying to level up every character you summon.

*   **Focus on Synergy:** Pairing a DOT unit like the Ranger with a crowd-control unit like the Ice Mage can keep enemies trapped in damage zones longer.
*   **Check Cooldowns:** Units with high damage but extremely long cooldowns can leave you vulnerable. Balance your team with at least one fast-attacking unit like the Thief.
*   **Positioning Matters:** Even an X-tier unit will die quickly if placed in the wrong spot. Use the "Unit Data" tab to check the range of your units and place them accordingly on the grid.
