## Summon Heroes Ranking Criteria

Before diving into the specific units, it is important to understand the grading system used to evaluate these heroes. Not every legendary unit is a powerhouse, and some epic units can actually outperform higher-rarity counterparts if used in the right niche.

*   **X-Tier (Core):** These are the absolute best units in the game. They possess unparalleled stats and abilities that make them indispensable for any high-level team.
*   **S-Tier (Superior):** Excellent units that are nearly as powerful as X-Tier. They should be prioritized for upgrades.
*   **A-Tier (Great):** Highly viable units that perform well in most content. They are excellent fillers until you pull a Core unit.
*   **B-Tier (Niche):** These units have specific uses but are often held back by slow animations or lower damage scaling.
*   **F-Tier (Unusable):** Units that should be replaced as soon as possible. Their stats do not justify their deployment cost.

## X-Tier: The Core Meta Units

The units in this category represent the pinnacle of power in 2026. If you manage to summon any of these, they should immediately become the focal point of your team building strategy.

### Divine (Mythical)
Divine is widely considered the premier ranged DPS unit. Specializing in ability usage, Divine can inflict massive burst damage from a safe distance. In the current **tier list summon heroes roblox** meta, Divine is favored for clearing waves of enemies before they can even reach your frontline.

### BET (Tank)
Every team needs a solid frontline, and BET is the undisputed king of health. With the highest health pool in the game, BET functions as a primary tank for story progression. Unlike other tanks that offer little offensive value, BET also maintains respectable DPS, ensuring that your frontline contributes to clearing the stage.

### Matriarch & Seraph
Both Matriarch and Seraph are essential for players looking to maximize their efficiency. These units offer a blend of high-end utility and damage that simplifies even the most difficult boss encounters.

> **💡 Expert Tip:** When building a team around X-Tier units, ensure you have a balance of one dedicated tank (like BET) and at least two high-output DPS units (like Divine or Seraph).

## S-Tier and A-Tier: High-Performance Heroes

While they may not reach the "Core" status of the X-Tier, these units are incredibly powerful and often necessary for specific boss mechanics, such as Damage Over Time (DOT) requirements.

### Ranger (Legendary)
The Ranger is a standout unit because it was the first to introduce DOT mechanics to the game. This makes the Ranger a specialized boss killer. Its ability to burn down high-HP targets quickly, combined with homing projectiles and great range, earns it a solid S-Tier spot.

### Sage (Legendary)
Sage is a versatile ranged DPS unit. While her raw damage might be slightly lower than Divine's, her ability keeps her relevant in the late game. She performs exceptionally well when enemies get closer, making her a great secondary DPS for mobile boss fights.

### Construct & Thief (Epic)
Surprisingly, these two Epic units are often better than many Legendaries. 
*   **Construct:** A melee bruiser that is incredibly bulky and capable of tanking significant damage while dealing it back.
*   **Thief:** A fast-attacking melee unit with high DPS. It is a "niche" pick but excels in scenarios where rapid strikes are required to break enemy shields.

## Units to Avoid: The "Trap" Heroes

A common mistake among players is assuming that a high rarity automatically equates to high power. In Summon Heroes, some Legendary units are actually worse than well-optimized Epics or even Rares.

### Frame Rate (Legendary)
Despite its Legendary status, Frame Rate is currently ranked in B-Tier or lower by most experts. The primary issue is "animation lock." Frame Rate often rushes into the middle of a fight only to die because its attack animations are too slow, leaving it vulnerable. Furthermore, its damage output is surprisingly low for its rarity.

### Academy Witch (Common/Rare)
The Academy Witch is frequently cited as the worst unit in the game. With abysmal stats and a role that is easily filled by almost any other unit, she should be replaced the moment you pull your first Epic.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not invest resources into "Frame Rate" just because of its Legendary border. You are better off investing in a high-tier Epic like Construct.

## How to Optimize Your Team Composition

To make the most of this **tier list summon heroes roblox**, you must understand how to synergyze these units. A team of five X-Tier DPS units will often fail because they lack the survivability to endure boss ultimates.

1.  **The Anchor:** Always include one high-health unit. **BET** is the preferred choice, but **Construct** is a viable budget alternative.
2.  **The Primary DPS:** Use a unit like **Divine** or **Seraph** to handle the bulk of enemy waves.
3.  **The Specialist:** Include **Ranger** if you are facing a boss with a massive health pool, as the DOT (Damage Over Time) will be more effective than raw burst.
4.  **The Support/Secondary:** Units like **Sage** provide consistent backup damage and can cover the map if your primary DPS is on cooldown.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the best overall unit in the tier list summon heroes roblox?**

A: Currently, **Divine** is considered the best overall unit due to its massive range and ability-based damage. However, for tanking, **BET** is the essential choice.

**Q: Is the Ice Mage worth keeping?**

A: The Ice Mage is a decent starting unit for beginners. However, as you progress through the story, you should look to replace it with an A-Tier or S-Tier ranged unit like Sage or Divine.

**Q: Why is Frame Rate ranked so low despite being a Legendary?**

A: Frame Rate suffers from "animation lock," which makes it slow and prone to taking unnecessary damage. Its DPS-to-survivability ratio is much lower than other units of the same rarity.

**Q: How often does the Summon Heroes meta change?**

A: The meta typically shifts with every major content update. New units often introduce new mechanics, like the DOT mechanic introduced by the Ranger, which can push older units down the rankings. Always check for updated unit data in the game index.
