## S-Tier: The Map Conquerors

Units in the S-Tier are considered "Map Gods." They possess abilities that can solo entire lanes or provide buffs that make your entire team nearly invincible. In the current **summon heroes roblox lvl 100 tier list**, these heroes are the primary targets for your summoning gems.

### Reaper (Mythic)
The Reaper remains the undisputed king of DPS in 2026. With its massive scythe swings and health-consumption mechanics, it turns enemy waves into a resource rather than a threat. ### Bear Tamer (Rare)
Despite its lower base rarity, the Bear Tamer is a "broken" unit in the best way possible. By summoning bear phantoms that nuke enemies from a distance, it allows players to maintain a safe distance while dealing massive damage. It is highly recommended to evolve and level this unit to 100 immediately.

### Demon Knight
The Demon Knight excels in combo-based gameplay. It provides AOE devastation and "Demon Buffs" that scale over time. When placed correctly, the Demon Knight can create a "legend zone" where enemies simply vanish before they can reach your base.

## Evolution and the Fusion Crystal System

One of the most unique aspects of Summon Heroes in 2026 is the Fusion Crystal system. Unlike other tower defense games that require you to pull the exact same unit multiple times to evolve it, this game allows for much more flexibility.

1.  **Obtain Fusion Crystals:** These are earned by playing through story modes or completing daily challenges.
2.  **Match Rarity, Not Name:** To evolve an Epic unit, you simply need three other Epic units of any kind.
3.  **Upgrade Stars:** This system allows you to take a 3-star unit and push it all the way to 5-star or even 6-star status without the frustration of duplicate RNG.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Always check a unit's potential on the **summon heroes roblox lvl 100 tier list** before using it as fusion fodder. You don't want to accidentally sacrifice a high-tier unit like Bear Tamer just to upgrade a lower-tier hero.

## Resource Management: Gems, Gold, and Traits

To reach level 100 and dominate the leaderboards, you must manage your resources effectively. In 2026, the game has made gold significantly more valuable than in previous iterations.

*   **Gold for Stat Rerolls:** Instead of spending precious gems, you can use gold to reroll the stats of your heroes. This allows you to hunt for "God Rolls" without depleting your summon currency.
*   **Gem Farming:** Replaying story modes you have already beaten still yields 50 gems per run. This is the most reliable way to save up for the next big banner.
*   **Trait Rerolls:** Traits add a final layer of power to your units. While they are harder to come by, a "Godly" or "Mythic" trait can make even a B-tier unit perform like an A-tier carry.

## PvP and Endgame Strategies

The PvP system in Summon Heroes is a chaotic free-for-all. Players are teleported into a central circle where their units battle until only one summoner remains. Success in this mode relies heavily on "Target Prioritization." By clicking on specific enemy monsters, you can force all your units to focus fire, which is crucial for taking out high-threat targets like an opponent's **Reaper** or **Divine** hero.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best hero for beginners in the summon heroes roblox lvl 100 tier list?**

A: While the Reaper is the ultimate goal, the **Bear Tamer** is the best hero for beginners. It is easier to summon due to its Rare status but possesses S-Tier damage and utility that can carry you through the first five story worlds easily.

**Q: How do I get more gems quickly?**

A: The most efficient way to farm gems is by replaying story mode stages. Each replay grants 50 gems. Additionally, keep an eye out for hidden chests scattered throughout the map and use any active codes provided by the developers in the loyalty rewards menu.

**Q: Is the Swordsman worth leveling to 100?**

A: Generally, no. The Swordsman is a basic melee unit that lacks the range and AOE needed for high-level waves. It is better to save your resources for units like the **Diver** or **Laser Cyborg**, which offer much better scaling in the late game.

**Q: What does the Fusion Crystal do?**

A: Fusion Crystals allow you to evolve units without needing a duplicate of the same hero. As long as you have enough heroes of the same rarity and a Fusion Crystal, you can increase the star rating of your chosen unit, making it much easier to reach the power levels required for level 100 content.
