## Step-by-Step Summon Heroes Reroll Guide

The process for resetting your account varies depending on your platform. As of mid-2026, the PC version via Steam has a known bug that requires a specific workaround.

### Mobile Rerolling (Recommended)
1.  Launch Star Savior and log in as a **Guest**.
2.  Complete the initial tutorial (approx. 5 minutes).
3.  Navigate to the **Challenges** menu and claim the 10,000 crystal reward.
4.  Spend your crystals on the recommended banners (see priority below).
5.  If unsatisfied, go to **Account Management** and select **Reset Server Account**.

### PC/Steam Rerolling (Workaround)
The PC version currently features a bug where the account reset button may not function in English. Follow these steps to bypass the issue:
1.  Go to **Account Management**.
2.  Change the game language to **Korean** (the second option in the top dropdown).
3.  Select the reset option.
4.  You will be prompted to type a specific sequence of characters to confirm deletion. (Copy and paste the confirmation string usually found in community pins or the official Discord).
5.  Restart the game and repeat the tutorial.

By following this **summon heroes reroll guide** method, you can keep the game in Korean during the rerolling process to save time, only switching back to English once you have finalized your account.

## Character Priority: Who to Target

When using this **summon heroes reroll guide**, your primary target should always be **Sera**. However, simply getting one copy isn't enough for a "perfect" start.

| Character | Tier | Role | Why They Are Essential |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sera** | 0 | Healer/Support | **Requires 1 Duplicate (Base + 1)** to unlock her core healing kit. |
| **Emily** | 0 | Tank | The best PvE tank in the game, providing massive survivability. |
| **Hilde** | 0.5 | PvP Tank | A collaboration unit (Counterside) that dominates the arena meta. |
| **Forara** | 1 | DPS | A strong starter banner unit if you cannot land the Tier 0 tanks. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not settle for a single copy of Sera. Her healing capability is locked behind her first duplicate (dupe), making it the highest priority for any new account.

The **summon heroes reroll guide** suggests that if you manage to pull two copies of Sera within your first 50 pulls, you should pivot your remaining crystals toward the Arcana banner or the Hilde collaboration banner.

## Arcana Priority and "The Sharp Assault Trap"

Arcana are essentially equipment cards that provide massive stat boosts and passive abilities. Many players fall into the trap of rerolling for "Sharp Assault." While Sharp Assault is a top-tier card, it is eventually farmable through daily tickets.

For the absolute best start, the **summon heroes reroll guide** recommends focusing on non-farmable Arcana that provide unique utility:
*   **Fay (Green-Haired Arcana):** Provides exceptional utility and is considered the "high-roll" target for Arcanas.
*   **Value over Farmability:** Always prioritize Arcana that cannot be easily obtained through the daily ticket system.

If you are performing the 15-minute reroll, you will clear through stage 1-5 to unlock an additional 10-pull specifically for Arcana. This is where you should look for Fay to complement your Sera-based team.

## Account Linking and Data Transfer

Once you have successfully followed the **summon heroes reroll guide** and landed your dream team, you must secure the account. Star Savior uses a UID and Code system for data transfer.

1.  Navigate to **Account Management**.
2.  Select **Mobile Account Link** (even if you are on PC).
3.  Note down your **UID** and generate a **Transfer Code**.
4.  Keep these credentials in a safe place. You can use this code to move your progress between Steam and mobile devices seamlessly.

## Post-Reroll Progression: The Chapter 8 Goal

Landing the right heroes is only the beginning. To truly maximize the potential of your rerolled account, your immediate goal is to reach **Operation Chapter 8**. 

Players following our **summon heroes reroll guide** should focus all resources on their core team to push through the main story. Clearing Chapter 8 is vital because it unlocks the higher difficulty of **Journey**. This mode provides significantly higher stat growth for your characters and is the primary way to power up your units for endgame 2026 content.

If you find yourself stuck before Chapter 8, ensure you have:
1.  Unlocked Sera's healing by using her duplicate.
2.  Equipped your best Arcana on your primary tank (Emily or Hilde).
3.  Claimed all achievement rewards to perform extra pulls on the standard banner for more duplicates.

## FAQ

**Q: How long does the average reroll take in Star Savior?**

A: A standard character-focused reroll takes about 7 to 8 minutes. If you are aiming for a "God Account" with specific SSR Arcana, the process takes approximately 15 minutes as you need to clear stage 1-5.

**Q: Can I reroll on the Steam version?**

A: Yes, but it is currently bugged. You must change the language to Korean in the account management menu to successfully trigger the "Reset Server Account" function. Many players find it easier to reroll on a mobile device and then link the account to Steam.

**Q: Which character is the most important in a summon heroes reroll guide?**

A: Sera is the most important unit. However, the **summon heroes reroll guide** emphasizes that you need two copies of her (the base unit plus one duplicate) to unlock her healing abilities, which are crucial for mid-to-late game progression.

**Q: Should I pull for Hilde or Emily?**

A: If you care about PvP rankings, Hilde (the Counterside collaboration unit) is the better choice. If you want to focus on clearing difficult PvE stages and the main story, Emily is considered the Tier 0 tank for that content.
