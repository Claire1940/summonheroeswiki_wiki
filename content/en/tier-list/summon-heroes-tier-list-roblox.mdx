## Summon Heroes Ranking Criteria

To understand where each unit sits, it is important to look at the grading system used by the community. Units are not just ranked on rarity; a well-placed Epic can often outperform a poorly optimized Legendary.

*   **X Tier (Core):** These are the absolute best units in the game. They define the meta and are essential for high-level content.
*   **S Tier (Elite):** Highly powerful units that excel in almost every situation. They are usually the primary DPS or Tanks for top-tier teams.
*   **A Tier (Great):** Solid performers that are reliable and strong. They are excellent substitutes if you lack X or S tier units.
*   **B Tier (Niche):** Units that have specific uses but are generally outclassed by higher tiers.
*   **F Tier (Unusable):** These units should be replaced as soon as possible due to low damage, poor scaling, or long cooldowns.

## Top Tier Units: The X and S Rank Heroes

The following heroes represent the pinnacle of power in Summon Heroes. If you manage to pull these, your progression will become significantly easier.

### Matriarch (X Tier)
The Matriarch remains one of the most coveted units in the game. Her ability to control the battlefield while providing massive damage makes her a staple for any serious player. She fits into almost any team composition, providing the utility needed for both boss fights and wave clearing.

### BET (X Tier)
When it comes to survivability, **BET** is the undisputed champion. Boasting the highest health pool in the game, BET acts as a massive tank for story progression and high-level PvE content. However, BET is not just a meat shield; this unit also provides respectable DPS, making it a hybrid powerhouse that can hold the front line while contributing to the kill count.

### Divine (S Tier)
Divine is a specialized ranged unit that focuses heavily on ability usage. While some units rely on basic attacks, Divine’s power comes from inflicting massive burst damage through her skills. She is particularly effective in scenarios where you need to delete high-priority targets quickly.

### Seraph (S Tier)
Seraph provides a blend of high-end utility and damage. Often seen in top-tier PvP builds, Seraph can disrupt enemy formations and provide the necessary pressure to turn the tide of a match.

> **💡 Tip:** When building your team, always pair a high-health tank like BET with at least two high-DPS units like Divine or Matriarch to ensure a balance between survivability and damage output.

## Legendary Units: High-Impact Specialists

Legendary rarity units are often the goal for many players, but their effectiveness varies wildly. In this **summon heroes tier list roblox** update, two specific legendaries stand out as must-haves for 2026.

### Ranger (S Tier)
The Ranger is a revolutionary unit in Summon Heroes, being the first to introduce a consistent **Damage Over Time (DOT)** mechanic. This makes the Ranger an incredible boss killer. His ability allows him to burn down high-health units rapidly, and his homing projectiles ensure that no enemy escapes his reach. His range is among the best in the game, allowing him to stay safe while melting enemies.

### Sage (A Tier)
The Sage is a powerful ranged DPS unit. While she doesn't quite reach the "broken" status of the Ranger, her ability keeps her highly relevant in the current meta. She performs best when enemies get into mid-range, where her DPS scaling increases significantly.

## Best Epic Units for Mid-Game Progression

Not everyone will have a full roster of Legendaries or Mythics right away. Fortunately, there are several Epic units that punch well above their weight class.

### Construct (A Tier)
The Construct is a melee bruiser that is exceptionally bulky. It is capable of tanking significant damage while dealing consistent DPS. For players who haven't pulled BET yet, the Construct is the best alternative for a front-line tank.

### Thief (A Tier)
The Thief is a high-speed melee unit that excels in both PvP and PvE. With a very fast attack speed and decent DPS, the Thief is perfect for cleaning up weakened enemies or flanking in PvP matches. While slightly niche due to its low health, its offensive capabilities are top-notch for an Epic unit.

## Units to Avoid: The "Traps"

Just because a unit has a high rarity doesn't mean it is worth using. Some units suffer from poor design or outdated stats that make them a liability in 2026.

*   **Academy Witch:** Widely considered the worst unit in the game. Her damage is negligible, and her utility is non-existent. Replace her immediately.
*   **Frame Rate:** Despite being a Legendary, Frame Rate is a major disappointment. He suffers from "animation lock," meaning he stands still for too long during his attacks, making him an easy target. He often rushes into combat only to die before completing his move set.
*   **Ice Mage:** While a decent starter unit, the Ice Mage falls off incredibly fast. Do not invest heavy resources into this unit once you have access to Epics.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid investing heavily in "Frame Rate" even if he is your only Legendary. His low damage and slow speed make him less effective than most A-tier Epics.

## Advanced Team Building Strategies

To truly master the game, you must look beyond individual rankings and focus on synergy. A team of five S-tier DPS units will often lose to a balanced team with a Tank, a Support/Utility unit, and three DPS units.

1.  **The Anchor:** Every team needs a unit with high health. BET is the gold standard here, but Construct is a viable backup.
2.  **The Boss Killer:** Units with DOT or high single-target burst, like the Ranger or Divine, are essential for clearing boss stages.
3.  **The Wave Clearer:** Look for units with AOE (Area of Effect) abilities or fast attack speeds like the Thief to manage large groups of enemies.
4.  **The Range Advantage:** Having at least two ranged units (like Sage or Ranger) ensures you can deal damage while your tank soaks up hits at the front.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the best overall unit in the summon heroes tier list roblox?**

A: Currently, **BET** and **Matriarch** are tied for the top spot. BET is the ultimate tank, while Matriarch provides unparalleled utility and damage for almost all game modes.

**Q: Is the Ranger worth the resources to upgrade?**

A: Yes, the Ranger is highly recommended because he is the first unit to feature a DOT (Damage Over Time) mechanic, which is essential for killing high-health bosses in late-game content.

**Q: Why is Frame Rate ranked so low despite being a Legendary?**

A: Frame Rate suffers from severe animation lock, meaning his attacks take too long to execute. This makes his actual DPS much lower than his stats suggest, and he often dies before he can finish his attack cycle.

**Q: Can Epic units beat Legendary units in Summon Heroes?**

A: In many cases, yes. Units like **Construct** and **Thief** are significantly better than lower-tier Legendaries like Frame Rate because they have better speed, reliability, and lower cooldowns.
