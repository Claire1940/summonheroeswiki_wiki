## Deep Dive: S-Tier Hero Mechanics

To master the **summon heroes roblox best legendary** units, you must understand how to position them. A poorly placed Reaper is just a waste of gems, whereas a strategically placed one is a win condition.

### The Reaper’s Harvest
The Reaper thrives on "Scythe Surges." As the waves progress, the Reaper consumes enemy health to scale its own damage. This makes it the ultimate unit for infinite modes. You should place the Reaper at "choke points" where enemies are forced to cluster, maximizing the AOE reach of every swing.

### Bear Tamer’s Judgment
The Bear Tamer functions differently by summoning "Bear Phantoms." These bears act as secondary units on the path, looping enemies and applying "Bear Judgment." This slows down fast-moving mobs while dealing heavy explosive damage. It is widely considered the best unit for solo pushes.

### Demon Knight’s Awakening
The Demon Knight is all about the "Demon Buff." When paired with other melee units, the Demon Knight echoes buffs through time, increasing the attack speed of nearby allies. This makes it a "Legacy" unit—one that stays relevant regardless of how many new towers are added to the game.
