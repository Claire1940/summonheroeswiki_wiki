## The Summon Heroes Grading System

Before diving into the specific rankings, it is important to understand how units are evaluated. In Summon Heroes, units are not just judged by their rarity, but by their role, cooldowns, and ability synergy. A legendary unit is not automatically better than an epic unit if its animation lock makes it unusable in fast-paced combat.

| Grade | Description | Strategy |

## X-Tier: The Core Units (The Best of the Best)

The X-Tier represents the pinnacle of the **roblox summon hero tier list**. These units define the current meta in 2026 and are considered "core" to any successful late-game team. If you happen to pull one of these, they should immediately become the focus of your upgrades.

### Matriarch and BET
Matriarch remains a top-tier choice for her unparalleled utility, while **BET** stands as the premier tank in the game. BET currently holds the record for the highest health pool, making him a mandatory inclusion for story mode progression where survivability is key. His ability to soak up massive damage while still contributing respectable DPS makes him a "melee bruiser" that few others can match.

### Divine and Seraph
**Divine** is a specialized ranged unit that thrives on ability usage. Unlike units that rely purely on basic attacks, Divine’s kit allows for massive burst damage that can clear entire waves or chunk boss health bars instantly. **Seraph** complements this with high-tier scaling, ensuring that your team has the raw power needed for competitive PvP rankings.

## S-Tier: Elite Performers

Units in the S-Tier are exceptionally strong and often serve as the primary damage dealers for players who haven't yet secured X-Tier mythical units.

### Ranger (The Boss Killer)
The **Ranger** is a standout Legendary unit because he was the first to introduce the Damage Over Time (DOT) mechanic to the game. This makes him an elite boss killer. With his homing projectiles and significant range, he can burn down high-health targets while staying safely positioned. In the current **roblox summon hero tier list**, he is widely considered the best legendary-rarity damage dealer.

### Sage
While not as explosive as the Ranger, **Sage** provides consistent ranged DPS. Her ability ensures she remains relevant even when enemies close the distance. She is ranked as a solid A+ to S-tier unit depending on the specific map layout, as her DPS increases significantly the closer she is to her targets.

## Epic Units for Mid-Game Progression

You don't always need a Legendary or Mythical unit to succeed. Several Epic units punch well above their weight class and are vital for players transitioning from the early game to the mid-game.

| Unit Name | Role | Why They Are Good |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Thief** | Melee DPS | Extremely fast attack speed and high reliable DPS in both PvP and PvE. |
| **Construct** | Melee Bruiser | Very bulky and capable of tanking significant hits while dealing damage. |
| **Ice Mage** | Support/CC | A great starter unit that provides essential crowd control. |

The **Thief** and **Construct** are currently the two best Epic units you can keep. The Thief’s fast attack speed allows him to shred through enemies with lower defense, while the Construct acts as a "mini-BET," providing a frontline presence for players who haven't summoned a dedicated tank yet.

## Units to Avoid: The "Trap" Picks

Just because a unit is difficult to summon doesn't mean it is worth using. The 2026 meta has exposed several units that suffer from poor scaling or mechanical issues.

*   **Frame Rate (Legendary):** Despite his high rarity, Frame Rate is often ranked in B-tier. His damage output is significantly lower than his peers, and he suffers from "animation lock." This causes him to stand still during attacks, often leading to him being overwhelmed and killed before he can finish his rotation.
*   **Academy Witch:** Currently regarded as one of the worst units in the entire game. Her stats are abysmal for her role, and she should be replaced by almost any other unit as soon as possible.
*   **Basic Archers:** While useful in the first ten minutes of the game, basic archers lack the ability scaling needed to handle Chapter 2 and beyond.

> **💡 Tip:** Always check the "Unit Data" tab in the game index. This provides the exact DPS and cooldown numbers, which can help you identify if a unit is underperforming due to slow animations.

## How to Build a Balanced Team

A common mistake players make is filling their team with only high-DPS units. To succeed in the higher tiers of Summon Heroes, you need a balanced composition. A standard winning team should follow this structure:

1.  **The Anchor (Tank):** A unit like **BET** or **Construct** to hold the front line.
2.  **The Boss Killer:** A high-range or DOT unit like **Ranger** to handle elite enemies.
3.  **The Wave Clear:** An ability-focused unit like **Divine** or **Seraph** for area-of-effect damage.
4.  **The Speedster:** A fast-attacking unit like **Thief** to clean up stragglers.

By balancing your roles (Academy, Archer, Bander, and Bruiser), you ensure that your team can handle different enemy types, from high-armor slow movers to fast-swarming mobs.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the best overall unit in the roblox summon hero tier list for 2026?**

A: Currently, **BET** is considered the most essential unit due to his massive health and tanking capabilities, followed closely by **Divine** for raw damage output.

**Q: Is it worth keeping Epic units in the late game?**

A: Yes, units like the **Thief** and **Construct** remain viable deep into the game because of their specific roles (fast DPS and bruiser) until you can replace them with their Mythical equivalents.

**Q: Why is Frame Rate ranked so low despite being a Legendary?**

A: Frame Rate suffers from "animation lock," meaning he is immobile for too long during his attack sequence. This makes him vulnerable to dying quickly and results in lower overall DPS compared to more mobile units.

**Q: How often does the roblox summon hero tier list change?**

A: The tier list usually shifts with every major content update or when new units are introduced. Always keep an eye on new "Unit Data" releases to see if balance changes have occurred.
