## Combat Mechanics and Elemental Matchups

Combat in Summon Heroes is semi-automated, but the positioning and elemental matchups are entirely under the player's control. Each hero belongs to one of five elements: Fire, Water, Nature, Light, or Dark. Using the correct element against an enemy provides a significant damage multiplier.

| Attacking Element | Defending Element | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fire** | Nature | 1.5x Damage (Strong) |
| **Water** | Fire | 1.5x Damage (Strong) |
| **Nature** | Water | 1.5x Damage (Strong) |
| **Light** | Dark | 2.0x Damage (Counter) |
| **Dark** | Light | 2.0x Damage (Counter) |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Attacking an element you are weak against (e.g., Fire attacking Water) results in a 25% damage reduction and a higher chance of missing the attack.

## Resource Management: Gems, Gold, and Shards

To maintain a competitive edge, you must manage three primary resources. This **summon heroes wiki roblox** guide recommends focusing on one specific "Carry" hero to maximize your investment early on.

1. **Gems:** The premium currency used for summoning and purchasing limited-time items. Obtain these via daily quests, codes, and achievements.
2. **Gold:** Used for upgrading equipment and leveling up heroes. Gold is abundant but depletes quickly as you reach hero levels 80+.
3. **Hero Shards:** Acquired by summoning duplicate heroes. These are used to "Ascend" your units, increasing their star rating and unlocking new passive abilities.

## Evolution and Ascension Guide

Once a hero reaches their level cap, you must evolve them to continue their growth. Evolution requires specific elemental stones found in the "Trial of Elements" dungeon.

| Evolution Tier | Required Level | Materials Needed |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1** | 20 | 10 Common Stones |
| **Tier 2** | 40 | 25 Rare Stones |
| **Tier 3** | 60 | 50 Epic Stones |

Ascension is separate from Evolution. While Evolution increases the level cap, Ascension increases base stats and skill potency. You need duplicate copies of the hero to perform an Ascension. A 5-star Mythic hero is significantly more powerful than a base-level Mythic hero, even at the same level.
