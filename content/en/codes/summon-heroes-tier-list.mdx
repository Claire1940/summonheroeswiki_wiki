### Reaper
Reaper remains the undisputed king of the 2026 meta. With massive AOE scythe swings, this hero consumes enemy health pools at an exponential rate. In high-wave chaos, the Reaper's ability to scale damage ensures that even the tankiest enemies melt before they reach your inner defenses.

### Bear Tamer
The Bear Tamer offers a unique playstyle by summoning bear phantoms that nuke packs from a distance. This "Bear Judgment" allows for safe, long-range clearing, which is vital for solo pushes where you cannot afford to let enemies close the gap.

### Demon Knight
The Demon Knight is the pinnacle of combo-based damage. By stacking buffs that echo through the duration of a match, this unit turns the map into a "legend zone" where enemies vanish almost instantly upon spawning.

## A-Tier: Reliable Anchors

A-Tier heroes are the backbone of most successful mid-to-late game teams. While they might not possess the raw "one-shot" potential of S-tier monsters, they offer incredible utility and reliable damage that can carry you through most story maps and dungeons.

*   **Diver:** An aquatic powerhouse that excels in underwater battlegrounds. Its "Dive" ability allows it to close gaps and chain placements for massive clears.
*   **Captain Dreadmore:** The ultimate pirate commander. His cannon support provides consistent fire across naval-themed maps and creates devastating defense routes.
*   **Mermaid:** Utilizing the "Charm" ability, the Mermaid controls the flow of the battlefield by alluring enemies into traps, making her a versatile mid-game carry.
*   **Laser Cyborg:** For players who prefer precision, the Laser Cyborg offers beam attacks that cut through enemy lines with futuristic efficiency.

> **💡 Tip:** Pair A-tier units like the Mermaid with high-damage towers to maximize the time enemies spend in your kill zones while they are charmed.

## B & C Tier: The Reroll Zone

Units in these tiers are generally considered "starter" material. While they may help you clear the first few maps of Rookie Island, they quickly lose their effectiveness as enemy health and speed increase.

### B-Tier (Situational Mid-Game)
*   **Ice Mage:** Useful for early crowd control with freeze effects, but the lack of damage scaling makes it a liability in infinite runs.
*   **Fire Mage:** Provides decent Damage Over Time (DOT), but tanky bosses often ignore the burn damage entirely.
*   **Archer:** Offers safe range but lacks the AOE necessary to deal with swarms.

### C-Tier (Instant Rerolls)
*   **Swordsman:** A basic melee unit with no range or sustain. It is often compared to a "toothpick" in boss fights.
*   **Bandit Nick:** Despite the promise of gold stealing, his actual combat contributions are negligible. Most top players consider rolling Nick a "waste of gems."

## How to Farm Gems for Summons

To climb the **summon heroes tier list**, you need a steady supply of gems. One roll costs 100 gems, but a guaranteed legendary requires a staggering 9,700 gems. Because there is no traditional "pity" for mythical units, efficient farming is mandatory.

### 1. Loyalty and Social Rewards
The easiest way for beginners to jumpstart their account is through loyalty rewards. By "favoriting" the game and joining the official Roblox group, you can instantly claim 500 gems. This is usually enough for your first five rolls, which can help you escape the "Bandit Nick" trap.

### 2. The AFK Story Mode Method
The most effective way to farm gems overnight is the AFK Story Mode method. By using an auto-clicker (which is generally permitted for idle tasks in Roblox), you can repeat the final stage of a world you have already cleared.
*   Go to **Story Mode**.
*   Select the highest world you can comfortably clear (e.g., Ever Crisis Academy).
*   Position your auto-clicker over the "Repeat Stage" button location.
*   Leave the game running to accumulate thousands of gems while you sleep.

### 3. Nightmare Circus and Dungeons
For active players, the Nightmare Circus provides a massive payout. Reaching wave 170+ can net you over 3,000 gems per run. However, entries are limited to three per 24 hours, so ensure your team is optimized before entering.

| **Daily Quests** | 350 Gems | Once per day |

## Advanced Team Building Strategies

Simply owning an S-tier hero isn't enough; you must understand tower synergy. In Summon Heroes, units act as multipliers for your defense strategy.

1.  **The Buff Chain:** Always pair a high-DPS unit like **Reaper** with a support like **Divine**. The "Supreme Power" buffs from Divine can double the AOE radius of the Reaper, allowing you to cover multiple lanes simultaneously.
2.  **Placement Logic:** Use **Bear Tamer** for far-reaching corners of the map. Since the bears can be summoned at a distance, you can place the Tamer in a protected central area while her summons do the work on the front lines.
3.  **Resource Management:** Don't spend all your gems as soon as you hit 100. Save up for the 9,700 gem "Guaranteed Legendary" pack if you are struggling with RNG. This ensures that even the unluckiest players can eventually secure an A or S-tier unit.

> **Warning:** Avoid spending Robux on gems unless absolutely necessary. The exchange rate is often poor compared to the amount of gems you can earn through a single night of AFK farming.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the best hero in the summon heroes tier list for 2026?**

A: Currently, **Reaper** is considered the best hero due to his overwhelming AOE damage and ability to scale into the late-game infinite waves. **Bear Tamer** is a close second for players who prefer a summoning playstyle.

**Q: How many gems do I need for a guaranteed legendary?**

A: You need 9,700 gems to guarantee a legendary unit from the summon packs. Mythical units do not have a set pity and rely purely on their low drop percentage.

**Q: Is AFK farming gems allowed in Summon Heroes?**

A: Yes, using an auto-clicker to repeat story stages is a common practice among the community and is not currently forbidden by the game's developers. It is the most efficient way to earn gems without spending Robux.

**Q: Why is Bandit Nick ranked so low?**

A: Bandit Nick is ranked in C-tier because his damage output is extremely low and his gold-stealing utility does not provide enough of an advantage to justify a slot on a serious team. He is almost always a "reroll" unit.
