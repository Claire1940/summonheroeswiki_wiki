## Core Abilities and Combat Mechanics

The effectiveness of the **summon heroes reaper** stems from its ability to control the battlefield while simultaneously soaking up damage that would otherwise hit your backline. Unlike standard melee units that focus on single-target burst, the Reaper excels at managing large groups of enemies through its wide-reaching scythe waves.

### Basic Attack: Scythe Sweep
The Reaper's primary attack involves a massive swing of its glowing red scythe. This creates a wide AoE cone of red energy that travels forward, hitting all enemies in its path. This energy wave provides excellent crowd control by staggering smaller mobs and ensuring that the Reaper doesn't get overwhelmed when surrounded. In narrow corridors, such as those found on the bridges of Sunset City, this attack can hit an entire wave of enemies at once.

### Special Skill: Relentless Spirits
The true power of the **summon heroes reaper** lies in its special ability. Upon activation, the Reaper summons four Relentless Spirits. These spirits are not just visual flair; they serve three critical functions:
1. **Aggro Draw:** They immediately charge toward the nearest enemies, forcing them to attack the spirits instead of the Reaper or your other heroes.
2. **Damage Absorption:** They act as a meat shield, effectively extending the Reaper's health pool by thousands of points.
3. **Supplementary DPS:** Each spirit can deal upwards of 5,000 damage per hit, contributing significant chip damage during boss encounters.

## Mastering the Summon Heroes Reaper in Combat

To get the most out of your **summon heroes reaper**, you must look beyond just auto-battling. Strategic positioning can significantly increase your win rate, especially in PvP where enemy players will attempt to bypass your frontline.

### Offensive Deployment
When playing offensively, the Reaper should be used to "shotgun" individual targets. By commanding the Reaper to move directly next to a boss or a high-priority enemy hero, all the energy waves from its scythe swings will hit the target simultaneously. This concentrated fire mimics the damage profile of a shotgun, melting high-HP targets like the Mythic Barbette boss.

### Defensive Deployment (The Tank Strategy)
Interestingly, many top-tier players in 2026 are deploying their **summon heroes reaper** as a primary tank. Because the Relentless Spirits are summoned frequently (especially with high cooldown reduction), they create a permanent barrier of bodies between the enemy and your ranged units. This allows heroes like the Emperor to safely cast high-damage spells from the backline without fear of being interrupted.

> **⚠️ Warning:** While the Reaper is tanky, it is not invincible. If the Relentless Spirits are destroyed and the special is on cooldown, the Reaper can be burst down quickly by heavy hitters. Always monitor the cooldown timer.

## Best Traits and Team Synergies

Choosing the right trait is the difference between a good unit and an "insane" one. For the **summon heroes reaper**, there is one trait that stands above the rest: **God Speed**.

### Why God Speed?
God Speed allows the Reaper to cycle through its special ability much faster. Since the Reaper's survivability is tied directly to the presence of its four spirits, reducing the downtime between summons is paramount. A Reaper with God Speed and a 60%+ cooldown reduction stat can often have a new set of spirits ready before the old ones have even expired.

### Recommended Team Compositions
The Reaper functions best when paired with units that can capitalize on the chaos it creates. In 2026, the current "Meta Trio" consists of:
*   **The Reaper:** Frontline tank, crowd control, and aggro management.
*   **The Emperor:** Massive backline AoE damage.
*   **The Demon Knight:** Secondary tank and physical damage dealer.

This composition provides a perfect balance of survivability and damage, making it viable for both the hardest PvE stages and competitive PvP matches. ## Advanced Progression: Red Stars and Optimization

Once you have secured your **summon heroes reaper**, the journey isn't over. To truly compete at the highest levels, you must focus on Red Stars and gear optimization. Red Stars provide a percentage-based boost to all base stats, which is particularly effective on the Reaper due to its already high base HP and Attack.

1.  **Leveling Priority:** Always prioritize the Reaper's level over your support units. Its damage and the health of its summons scale directly with its level.
2.  **Skill Maxing:** In PvP, maximizing your skill levels is often more important than having the perfect trait. A level 10 special will summon much hardier spirits than a level 1 special.
3.  **Attribute Focus:** Look for gear that provides "X Stats" on cooldown reduction and flat Attack bonuses.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get the summon heroes reaper?**

A: The Reaper is a Mythic unit that can be obtained through the summoning banners. It has a base drop rate of 0.1%. While there is no guaranteed "pity" for specific Mythics, players often report pulling one within 1,000 to 2,000 summons.

**Q: Is the Reaper better than the Barbette unit?**

A: Both are Mythic units, but they serve different roles. The Reaper is superior for crowd control and multi-target tanking due to its spirits. Barbette typically excels in raw single-target damage. Most top-tier teams try to run both if possible.

**Q: What is the best trait for a summon heroes reaper in 2026?**

A: **God Speed** is widely considered the best trait. It allows for faster skill activation, which means more frequent spirit summons. This increases both the defensive and offensive output of the unit significantly.

**Q: Can the Reaper clear Sunset City Stage 5 solo?**

A: While it is possible for a highly-invested Reaper (high Red Stars and level) to clear Stage 5 solo, it is much safer and faster to run it with a full team. The spirits can handle most mobs, but the final boss has enough HP to eventually wear a solo Reaper down.
