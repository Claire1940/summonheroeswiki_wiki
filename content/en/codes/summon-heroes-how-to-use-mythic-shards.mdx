## Understanding Mythic Shards in Summon Heroes

Mythic Shards are a specialized currency introduced to provide a "pity" alternative for the game's most elusive units. While standard gems are used on rotating banners, Mythic Shards are exclusively used in the **Mythic Pack** found within the summon interface. 

The core mechanic is simple: once you accumulate **50 Mythic Shards**, you can exchange them for a guaranteed Mythic hero. This system was designed to alleviate the frustration of the standard 0.1% mythic drop rate found on common banners. In the current 2026 meta, these shards are the lifeblood of progression for free-to-play and low-spending players alike.

### The Mythic Pack vs. Standard Banners

| Feature | Standard Banner | Mythic Pack |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary Currency** | Gems (100 per pull) | **Mythic Shards** |
| **Mythic Drop Rate** | 0.1% | **100% (at 50 shards)** |
| **Rotation Speed** | Every 30 Minutes | Fixed / Update Based |
| **Pity System** | Legendary at 100 pulls | Guaranteed Mythic at 50 shards |

> **Warning:** Mythic Shards cannot be converted back into gems or used on limited-time event banners. Always save them until you have the full 50 required for a guaranteed pull.

## How to Obtain Mythic Shards Efficiently

To effectively utilize your resources, you must first know where to farm them. As of the latest 2026 updates, there are two primary methods to earn these shards. Both require a baseline level of power, but they are accessible to most players who have reached the mid-game stages.

### 1. The Mythic Dungeon
Located in the central hub's new dungeon portal area, the Mythic Dungeon is your most consistent source of shards. This dungeon operates on a **6-hour reset cycle**. This means you can potentially run it four times within a 24-hour period if you manage your schedule correctly.

The boss of this dungeon is the **Reaper**, an imposing foe with approximately **46,000 HP**. While the health pool sounds intimidating, a well-synergized team of level 100 units can dismantle the Reaper with relative ease.

### 2. Daily Quests and the Nightmare Circus
The second method involves completing the "Nightmare Circus" daily quest. The Nightmare Circus is an endless wave mode where rewards scale based on how long you survive. To earn your daily Mythic Shard, you must complete three runs of the Circus.

| **Mythic Dungeon** | Every 6 Hours | 1 Shard (4 total/day) |
| **Nightmare Circus Quest** | Once Daily | 1 Shard |

## Summon Heroes: How to Use Mythic Shards for Guaranteed Units

Once you have gathered 50 shards, it is time to redeem them. Navigate to the **Summon Area** in the main hub. Look for the specific **Mythic Pack** tab. Unlike the standard banners that rotate every 30 minutes, the Mythic Pack is a stable option that specifically tracks your shard count.

### Step-by-Step Redemption Process
1.  **Open the Summon Menu:** Approach the summon NPC or portal in the Dojo.
2.  **Select the Mythic Pack:** Ensure you are not looking at the Gem banners.
3.  **Check Shard Balance:** The UI will display your current progress (e.g., 50/50).
4.  **Confirm Summon:** Click the shard icon to spend your 50 shards.
5.  **Receive Hero:** A Mythic unit, such as the newly released **Emperor**, will be added to your inventory immediately.

> **💡 Tip:** Check your unit details before summoning. The 2026 update allows you to see full descriptions of a unit's attack and special abilities, helping you decide if the current Mythic in the pack fits your team composition.

## Hero Fusion and Power Scaling

Getting a Mythic unit is only half the battle. To make them viable for high-level dungeons, you must utilize the **Fusion System**. Unlike other gacha games that require duplicate units, Summon Heroes allows you to fuse any units of the same star rarity.

### Fusion Rarity Requirements
| Base Unit Rarity | Fodder Requirement | Result |
|---|---|---|
| 3-Star Rare | Any 3-Star Unit | 4-Star Rare |
| **4-Star Epic** | Any 4-Star Unit | **5-Star Epic** |
| 5-Star Legendary | Any 5-Star Unit | 6-Star Legendary |

You can purchase **Fusion Crystals** from the Item Shop in the main hub. This shop resets every 5 minutes, so check it frequently to stock up on crystals and summon tickets. Fusion is essential because even a Mythic unit can be outperformed by a highly fused Legendary if their star level is significantly higher.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I use Mythic Shards on the rotating 30-minute banners?**

A: No. Mythic Shards are a specific currency used only for the Mythic Pack. To summon on rotating banners, you must use Gems (100 per single pull, 1,000 for a 10-pull). Understanding **summon heroes how to use mythic shards** correctly means knowing they are a separate, guaranteed progression path.

**Q: What happens if I miss a 6-hour dungeon reset?**

A: If you miss a reset, you simply lose the opportunity for that specific shard. The timer is fixed, so if the dungeon resets at 12:00 PM and you don't run it until 6:01 PM, you will have missed the previous window and will be playing in the new window.

**Q: Is the Emperor unit worth the 50 Mythic Shards?**

A: Yes. As of the 2026 update, the Emperor is one of the highest-rated units for both PvP and high-level Dungeon clearing. His special attacks provide massive utility that lower-rarity units cannot match.

**Q: Do I get Mythic Shards from PvP?**

A: Currently, PvP rewards you with PvP Tokens (1 for a loss, 3 for a win). These tokens are used in a different shop. To get Mythic Shards, you must stick to the Mythic Dungeon and the Nightmare Circus daily quest.
