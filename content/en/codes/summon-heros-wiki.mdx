## Hero Evolution and Awakening

Once you have acquired a powerful unit, the journey is only beginning. The **summon heros wiki** emphasizes the importance of the Awakening system. Awakening a hero requires duplicate copies of that unit or "Universal Shards."

*   **Awakening Level 1-3:** Increases base stats by 15% per level.
*   **Awakening Level 4:** Unlocks a secondary passive ability.
*   **Awakening Level 5 (Max):** Significantly enhances the hero's Ultimate skill and changes their visual appearance.

Transcendence is the final step in hero development. This process requires "Transcendence Orbs," which are typically obtained through high-level PvP rewards or by dismantling Epic-tier heroes. This is an expensive process, so it is recommended to only transcend S-tier units that you plan on using for the long term.

## Advanced Combat Tactics

Mastering the combat in Summon Heroes involves more than just clicking skills. Element advantages play a massive role in damage calculation. The elemental wheel follows a standard cycle: Fire beats Nature, Nature beats Water, and Water beats Fire. Light and Dark elements are mutually effective against each other but neutral to the primary three.

Using this **summon heros wiki** elemental guide, you should swap your team members before entering a boss floor to exploit these weaknesses. Dealing 25% extra damage simply by switching a hero can be the difference between a victory and a wipe in the late-game stages.
