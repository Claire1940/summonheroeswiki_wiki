## Navigating Challenges with Divine

While pulling Divine from the **summon heroes banner** significantly boosts your account, she is not without her weaknesses. Strategic positioning is the difference between a victory and a quick defeat.

### Environmental Limitations
Divine struggles in narrow corridors. These tight spaces limit her ability to retreat or maneuver effectively, which can be a death sentence if a heavy-hitting enemy closes the gap. Because her movement is restricted in these zones, players must be proactive in controlling her positioning to ensure she isn't cornered.

### Ranged Enemy Threats
Another challenge involves the presence of multiple ranged enemies on the battlefield. Certain enemies can fire tracking projectiles of their own, specifically targeting Divine. If she is left isolated, she can take unnecessary damage that shortens her uptime.

> **Warning:** To mitigate the threat of ranged enemies, ensure you have a frontline hero deployed to soak up initial tracking shots, or keep all three of your active heroes engaged to split the enemy's focus.

## Optimal Team Compositions for 2026

To truly succeed after a lucky pull from the **summon heroes banner**, you must pair your Mythic units with complementary heroes. A balanced team usually consists of a tank, a crowd controller, and a primary damage dealer.

### The "Divine Dominance" PvP Build
In the competitive PvP scene, Divine shines when she has the space to charge her barrage. This requires a sturdy frontline and someone to lock down the opposition.

*   **Tank:** **Reaper** is currently the gold standard for tanking. His ability to absorb damage and maintain a presence on the front line allows Divine to stay safe in the back.
*   **Crowd Control (CC):** Using another unit with high CC capabilities is vital. If you do not have a second Divine for her laser displacement, consider units like the **Joker** or the **Demon Knight**. These heroes can disrupt enemy formations, making them easy targets for Divine’s homing missiles.

## Strategies for the Summon Heroes Banner

Maximizing your gem usage is critical for long-term success. The **summon heroes banner** can be unforgiving if you pull haphazardly. Follow these steps to ensure you get the most out of your resources in 2026:

1.  **The 10-Pull Rule:** Generally, performing ten pulls at once offers a better statistical chance for higher-rarity units or provides a small discount in currency, depending on the current game version.
2.  **Monitor the Meta:** Before committing to a banner, check community tier lists. A unit might look visually impressive but may lack the utility needed for late-game content.
3.  **Active Playstyle Synergy:** If you prefer an idle playstyle, heroes like Divine might be less effective since her best moves (like the Dual Lasers) require manual activation. Look for heroes on the **summon heroes banner** that have strong passive "Auto" skills if you don't plan on manual piloting.
4.  **Resource Hoarding:** Only spend your gems when a "Rate-Up" event is active for a Mythic hero you lack. Pulling on a standard banner without a specific target often leads to a diluted roster of mid-tier units.

## Advanced Tactics: Manual vs. Auto

While Summon Heroes allows for automated combat, the 2026 difficulty curve often necessitates manual intervention. When using Divine, manual control allows you to aim her lasers through the densest part of the enemy wave. This "piercing" effect can clear an entire lane in seconds, something the AI often fails to optimize.

Furthermore, manual control helps in managing the "Narrow Corridor" weakness. By stutter-stepping (moving between attacks), you can keep Divine at the maximum possible range, forcing enemies to travel further and giving her homing barrage more time to recharge.

A: In 2026, the banners typically rotate every 7 to 14 days. Major Mythic showcases, like the one for Divine, may last longer during special seasonal events.
