## The Summoning System and Banner Mechanics

To improve your **summon heroes hero stats**, you must interact with the summoning banners located in the main hub. Each banner features seven different units: one legendary, two epics, three rares, and a very elusive mythic. 

The cost of summoning is fixed at 100 gems per unit, or 1,000 gems for a 10-pull. One of the most player-friendly features of Summon Heroes is the legendary pity system. You are guaranteed a legendary unit within 100 summons. However, you must be careful: pity is **not shared** between banners. If you start summoning on one pack, try to stick with it until you hit your pity or the unit you desire.

## Enhancing Stats Through Fusion and Leveling

In most games, you need to pull the exact same hero to "limit break" or increase their power. Summon Heroes breaks this mold. As long as two units share the same star rarity, they can be fused. For instance, if you have a 4-star Dragoon and any other 4-star unit you don't need, you can fuse them to elevate the Dragoon to 5-star status.

This flexibility means that your **summon heroes hero stats** can be boosted significantly by using "trash" units to upgrade your "carry" units. 

### Key Resources for Stat Growth:
1. **Fusion Crystals:** These are the primary currency for the fusion process. You can find these in the item shop, which resets every 5 minutes.
2. **Food (Rice Cakes/Candy):** These items are used to "bubble up" your units, providing immediate experience points to increase their base level.
3. **Coins:** Used for general upgrades and purchasing items from the hub vendors.

| Item Type | Source | Best Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Fusion Crystals** | Item Shop | Increasing Star Rarity of Legendaries. |
| **Rice Cakes** | Item Shop / AFK Mode | Rapidly leveling new pulls to match your team. |
| **Summon Tickets** | Item Shop (Rare) | Free pulls on any active banner. |

## Top Early-Game Heroes to Watch For

While legendary and mythic units are the ultimate goal, certain rare and epic units punch far above their weight class due to their unique skill sets and scaling **summon heroes hero stats**. If you see these on a banner, they are worth your gems.

### The Bear Tamer (Rare)
Currently considered one of the best units in the game despite his low rarity. The Bear Tamer is incredibly easy to obtain and even easier to fuse to high star levels. His damage-to-cost ratio is unmatched for beginners.

### The Mermaid (Rare/Epic)
The Mermaid is the queen of Area of Effect (AoE) damage. In the early stages of Summon Heroes, you will often face large groups of slimes and bandits. The Mermaid can clear these waves much faster than single-target units like the Archer or Dual Wielder.

### The Dual Wielder (Epic)
The Dual Wielder serves as an excellent frontline tank. With high HP and defense stats, he can soak up hits from bosses like the Slime Summoner while your backline mages and archers deal damage safely.

## Farming Gems and Resources

To consistently pull for new units and improve your **summon heroes hero stats**, you need a steady stream of gems. The game provides several ways to earn these without spending Robux.

*   **Nightmare Circus:** This is a daily activity that can be performed three times. It is an endless wave mode where the difficulty ramps up over time. Reaching level 130 can net you upwards of 300 gems in just a few minutes.
*   **AFK Mode:** Located in the main dojo, this mode allows you to earn rewards while you are away from your computer. You can expect to earn roughly 500 gems per hour, along with a significant amount of food and gold.
*   **Stage Progression:** Each new stage you clear for the first time provides a gem bounty. Focus on pushing the main campaign as far as possible to build your initial gem reserve.

> **Warning:** Always ensure your inventory has space before starting a long AFK session, as you don't want to miss out on high-tier food drops or fusion materials.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I check my summon heroes hero stats?**

A: You can view your units' stats by opening the "Units" menu from the main UI. Here, you can see their current level, star rarity, HP, and damage output. Equipping units will also show how they compare to your currently active team.

**Q: Does the pity system reset if I get a legendary early?**

A: Yes, if you pull a legendary unit before reaching 100 summons, the pity counter for that specific banner will reset to zero.

**Q: What is the fastest way to level up a new hero?**

A: The fastest way is to use Rice Cakes or other food items purchased from the Item Shop. Additionally, leaving your game in AFK mode in the dojo will provide a passive stream of experience items.

**Q: Can I fuse a legendary unit with a rare unit?**

A: No. Fusion requires both units to have the same star rarity (e.g., both must be 4-stars), regardless of their original base rarity (Legendary, Epic, etc.).
