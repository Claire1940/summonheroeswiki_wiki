## The Need for Speed: Frame Rate Unit Analysis

Frame Rate has quickly earned a reputation as the most specialized single-target assassin in the game. His design philosophy centers around high-frequency hits that can shred through the health bars of even the tankiest bosses. Unlike other legendary units that focus on broad strokes, Frame Rate is a precision instrument.

His regular attack is a staggering 20-hit combo. While individual hits might seem modest, the cumulative damage over a short window is immense. However, the real star of the show is his special ability: a lightning-fast 110-jab combo. This move is currently one of the highest hit-count abilities in the game, making him the go-to choice for enemies with "hit-count" shields or those that require rapid interruption.

## Technomancer: The New Queen of Crowd Control

While Frame Rate handles the bosses, the Technomancer is designed to dominate the battlefield. She has quickly become a fan favorite due to her accessibility and her ability to clear entire screens of enemies with minimal effort. For players who have struggled to pull the elusive Reaper unit, the Technomancer serves as a formidable and more attainable alternative.

Her special ability summons eight seeking orbs that automatically track and destroy targets. This "set and forget" playstyle is perfect for high-density stages where manual aiming becomes a chore.

## Strategic Implementation of Framerate Summon Heroes

When integrating the **framerate summon heroes** into your active lineup, you must consider the synergy between speed and area denial. Frame Rate should almost always be your lead unit when entering a boss room, while the Technomancer should handle the preceding waves of minions.

In stages like Leviathan's Eye, the difficulty spikes significantly as you progress. Even at level 109, units can struggle if they aren't positioned correctly. The Technomancer’s digital waves provide a "safety net" by pushing back enemies, giving your other units time to recharge their specials. Meanwhile, the sheer speed of Frame Rate allows you to "kite" dangerous elites while chipping away at their health.

### Leveling and Progression Milestones

To get the most out of your **framerate summon heroes**, you need to focus on specific leveling milestones. The game’s scaling changes drastically after level 100, and these legendary units require specific materials found in the later stages of the story.

1. **Level 1-60:** Focus on basic attack upgrades. Frame Rate’s 20-hit combo gains a significant damage boost at level 50.
2. **Level 61-100:** Prioritize special ability cooldown reduction. Technomancer’s orbs become much more viable when their uptime is increased.
3. **Level 101+:** Focus on "Awakening" stats. This is where Frame Rate’s movement speed truly starts to outpace the rest of the roster.

## Mastery in Leviathan's Eye

Leviathan's Eye remains the ultimate testing ground for any new unit. When taking Frame Rate into this dungeon, your primary focus should be on the 110-jab combo. The animations for this move are incredibly clean, but they do lock the character into a fixed position for a brief moment. You must time this special ability during the boss's "recovery" frames to avoid taking unnecessary damage.

The Technomancer, on the other hand, excels in the early rooms of Leviathan's Eye. Her digital waves can clear out the "trash mobs" before they even reach your frontline. The range on her digital waves is particularly impressive, often hitting enemies that haven't even fully rendered on the screen yet. This makes her an essential unit for anyone looking to achieve a "Perfect" clear rating on Stage 1 and beyond.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the Frame Rate unit better than the Technomancer for general gameplay?**

A: It depends on your needs. Frame Rate is the king of single-target DPS and speed, making him better for bosses and speed-farming. The Technomancer is superior for crowd control and clearing large groups of enemies, making her more versatile for general stage progression.

**Q: How does the Technomancer compare to the Reaper in the current framerate summon heroes meta?**

A: The Technomancer is essentially a more accessible version of the Reaper. While the Reaper might have higher raw burst damage, the Technomancer offers better range and is significantly easier for free-to-play players to obtain and upgrade in 2026.

**Q: What is the best way to utilize the 110-jab combo?**

A: This ability is best saved for boss "break" states or when an enemy is stationary. Because it involves so many hits in a short period, it is also highly effective at stripping away multi-hit shields that some elite enemies in Leviathan's Eye possess.

**Q: Can I use Frame Rate for AoE damage?**

A: While Frame Rate is primarily a single-target unit, his high movement speed allows him to dart between enemies quickly. However, for true area-of-effect damage, you are much better off switching to the Technomancer or another dedicated AoE unit.
