## Advanced Farming: The AFK Method

Many pro players use the Bear Tamer to facilitate AFK (Away From Keyboard) farming. Because the bears are summoned automatically, you don't need to manually trigger abilities to maintain a defense. Using tools like Tiny Task on a computer, players can loop Stage 44 or higher to farm gems and traits without active play.

1.  **Set up your Bear Tamers** near the start of the lane to intercept spawns immediately.
2.  **Place a Reaper or Laser Cyborg** behind them for clean-up.
3.  **Use an auto-clicker** or macro script to hit the "Retry" and "Ready" buttons.
4.  **Check for Chests:** Remember that chests spawn in semi-random locations; manual collection every few hours is recommended to maximize rewards.

> **Warning:** While AFK farming is common, ensure your character is positioned correctly. Some maps have "stuck points" where units might fail to pathfind if placed too close to corners.

In the current **bear tamer summon heroes** meta, many players prefer the Tamer for high-difficulty story modes.

**Q: How many bears can one Bear Tamer have out at once?**

A: A single Bear Tamer typically summons one Giant Bear and two smaller bears, for a total of three units. By using multiple Bear Tamers (up to three), you can have nine bears active simultaneously.

**Q: What is the best trait for a Bear Tamer?**

A: The "Godly" or "X" tier traits in Attack Speed or Cooldown Reduction are the most sought after in 2026. These allow the Tamer to replenish his bear army almost instantly after they are defeated.

**Q: Can Bear Tamer be used in PvP?**

A: Yes! Bear Tamer is excellent in PvP because his summons can distract the opponent's units, forcing them to waste their high-damage abilities on temporary bears rather than your main hero.
