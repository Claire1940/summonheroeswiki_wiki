### Reaper
The Reaper is arguably the most coveted unit in the current meta. Its scythe swings provide massive AOE damage that can cleave through entire waves of enemies simultaneously. What makes the Reaper truly special is its ability to scale as the waves get tougher, making it a mandatory pick for anyone looking to push deep into infinite map runs.

### Bear Tamer
Do not let the "Rare" tag fool you. In the 2026 meta, the Bear Tamer is widely considered an S-tier unit because of its accessibility and raw power. It summons bears that act as phantoms, nuking enemy packs from a distance. Because it is easier to obtain than a Legendary or Mythic, it is the best unit for players to focus on during their early to mid-game progression.

### Demon Knight & Divine Units
The Demon Knight serves as a pinnacle of combo-based damage, rewriting defensive lines with buffs that shatter boss health bars. Meanwhile, Seraph and Divine units provide the holy utility needed to sustain your towers during the most intense wave chaos.

## A-Tier: Reliable Powerhouses

A-tier heroes are the anchors of a solid team. While they might not "solo" the entire game like a Reaper, they provide the necessary support and consistent damage required to clear late-game maps.

*   **Diver:** A water-based unit that excels in boss defense. Its "dive" ability allows it to close gaps and clear massive packs of enemies with fluid, aggressive movements.
*   **Captain Dreadmore:** This pirate-themed unit uses cannon support to create naval battlegrounds. It is highly versatile and shines when paired with other mid-range carries.
*   **Mermaid:** A siren-style unit that uses charm abilities to control the flow of the wave. It offers excellent AOE damage and is a favorite for farming gems on darker maps.
*   **Laser Cyborg:** For those who prefer precision, the Laser Cyborg provides futuristic beam attacks that can snipe high-priority targets from across the map.

> **💡 Tip:** Pair Captain Dreadmore with Laser Cyborg to cover both AOE cannon fire and high-precision single-target damage for a balanced defense.

## B-Tier: Situational Specialists

The B-tier is home to heroes that are "good enough" to get the job done but will eventually be replaced as you climb the **summoner hero tier list**. These units often have niche effects like freezing or burning.

| Hero | Element | Effect | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Ice Mage** | Ice | Freeze/Slow | Keep for crowd control |
| **Fire Mage** | Fire | Burn/DOT | Good for armored mobs |
| **Archer** | Physical | Long Range | Replace with Laser Cyborg |

These heroes are predictable and reliable for mid-level maps. However, they lack the scaling needed for endless modes. Pros often use them as "fillers" in a squad while they hunt for better rolls.

## C-Tier: The Starter Trap

Units in the C-tier are generally considered "fodder" or "trash rolls." While they might help a brand-new player clear the first few waves of a starter map, they offer almost zero utility in the long run.

*   **Swordsman:** A basic melee unit that feels like a toothpick against bosses. It lacks range and sustain.
*   **Bandit Nick:** Despite the sneaky aesthetic and minor gold-stealing mechanics, Nick fails to provide the damage necessary to stop tanky enemy loops.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not invest your precious fusion crystals into C-tier units. Save your resources for at least an A-tier or a high-potential Rare like the Bear Tamer.

## Understanding Summoning Mechanics in 2026

To climb the rankings, you must understand how the summoning system works. In [Roblox Summon Heroes](https://www.roblox.com/games/15317424610/Summon-Heroes), banners rotate every 30 minutes. Each banner features a specific pool of seven units:

1.  **Mythic Unit:** 0.1% Chance
2.  **Legendary Unit:** 1 per banner
3.  **Epic Units:** 2 per banner
4.  **Rare Units:** 3 per banner

The game utilizes a **pity system** that guarantees a Legendary unit within 100 summons. It is important to note that pity is *not* shared between the two available banners. If you are 50 pulls deep into Banner A, those pulls will not count toward the pity on Banner B.

### The Power of Fusion
One of the most innovative features of the game is the Fusion system. Unlike other tower defense games that require duplicate units to evolve, Summon Heroes allows you to fuse *any* units of the same star rarity.

*   **Example:** You can take a 4-star Dragoon and fuse it with any other 4-star unit you don't want (like a Bandit Nick) to create a 5-star Dragoon.
*   **Fusion Crystals:** These are essential for the process. You can find them in the Item Shop, which resets every 5 minutes.

## How to Farm Gems for More Summons

Since each pull costs 100 gems (or 1,000 for a 10-pull), you need a steady stream of currency to keep up with the **summoner hero tier list** meta.

| Method | Rewards | Efficiency |
|---|---|---|
| **Nightmare Circus** | 300+ Gems in 4 mins | High (3x Daily) |
| **AFK Dojo** | ~500 Gems per hour | Medium (Passive) |
| **Main Quests** | One-time rewards | High (Early Game) |

The **Nightmare Circus** is an endless wave mode that rewards you based on how far you progress. Reaching level 130 can net you several hundred gems in a very short time. For players who cannot actively play, the **AFK Dojo** is a lifesaver, providing gems, money, and food for leveling units while you are away from your screen.

## FAQ

**Q: Which unit should I reroll for first in the summoner hero tier list?**

A: If you are lucky, aim for the Reaper. However, for a more realistic and powerful start, the Bear Tamer is the best unit to target due to its high damage output and lower rarity, making it easier to pull and upgrade.

**Q: Does the pity carry over when the banner rotates?**

A: Yes, the pity on a specific banner slot usually carries over even when the units rotate every 30 minutes, but it does not transfer between the two different banner packs.

**Q: Should I use the "Auto Sell" feature for Rare and Epic units?**

A: Generally, no. Because of the Fusion system, even "bad" Rare and Epic units are valuable as fusion material to upgrade your S-tier heroes to higher star ratings.

**Q: How do I get more Fusion Crystals?**

A: Check the Item Shop in the main hub frequently. It resets every 5 minutes and often stocks Fusion Crystals and Summon Tickets for gold or gems.
