## S-Tier: The Gods of the Battlefield

Units in the S-tier are the gold standard for any serious player. These heroes don't just participate in the defense; they redefine the map's history by obliterating waves before they even reach your primary towers.

### Reaper (The Death Hero)
The Reaper is arguably the most coveted unit in the game. With god-tier DPS and massive AOE scythe swings, this unit consumes the health of entire packs endlessly. In high-wave chaos, the Reaper’s ability to scale damage based on kills makes it a "feast" unit—the more enemies it kills, the more dominated the battlefield becomes. It is the ultimate meta pick for players who want to turn endless maps into a routine farm.

### Bear Tamer (The Beast Master)
If you prefer a more tactical "summoner" playstyle, the Bear Tamer is your best bet. This hero summons bear phantoms that nuke packs from a distance. The Bear Tamer is a favorite for leaderboard emperors because it allows for flexible placement, weaving through enemy barrages while its bears provide constant, heavy-hitting judgment on the lanes.

### Demon Knight (The Combo King)
The Demon Knight represents the pinnacle of combo power. By utilizing demon buffs that echo through time, this unit provides spikes of damage that can shatter even the most legendary boss foes. It shines brightest in solo pushes where its "awakening" mechanic turns the map into a legend zone, causing enemies to vanish in historic bursts of might.

## A-Tier: Reliable Anchors for Late Game

A-tier heroes are the backbone of most successful mid-to-high level accounts. While they may not have the "one-shot" potential of a Reaper, they provide the necessary utility and sustained damage to handle tough maps.

*   **Diver:** A water-based unit that excels in aquatic or hybrid maps. Its dive ability allows it to close gaps instantly, making it a "fluid" commander on the battlefield.
*   **Captain Dreadmore:** This pirate unit brings cannon support to the fight. It is highly versatile in farm builds, where its cannon routes can chain multiple waves together for efficient clearing.
*   **Mermaid:** The Siren Queen of the meta. Her charm ability can turn the tide of a battle by alluring enemies into traps or slowing their progress significantly.
*   **Laser Cyborg:** For players who love precision, the Laser Cyborg offers beam attacks that cut through high-armor targets. It is a futuristic powerhouse that glows brightest in dark, high-difficulty maps.

| **Archer** | B | Lacks AOE | Early game range support |
| **Swordsman** | C | Melee range only | Absolute beginners |

The **Swordsman** and **Bandit Nick** are often referred to as "trash rolls." Bandit Nick's "steal" mechanic is a tease; the gold gain is rarely enough to offset the loss of DPS you experience by not having a higher-tier unit in that slot.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not over-invest your upgrade materials into B-tier units like the Ice Mage. While the freeze effect is helpful, you will eventually need to swap them for A or S-tier units to clear the 2026 infinite maps.

## Maximizing Your Summoning Strategy

To truly master the **heroes summon tier list**, you must treat your heroes as map treasures rather than simple gambles. Smart roll grinds are the secret to reaching the leaderboards.

1.  **Save for Multi-Summons:** Most top players avoid single rolls. Saving gems for 10x summons often provides better odds or "pity" mechanics that guarantee at least an A-tier unit.
2.  **Focus on Synergy:** An S-tier unit like the Seraph (Angel Hero) becomes even more powerful when paired with Divine (God Hero). Their holy buffs stack, creating a "heavenly guardian" effect that makes your base nearly wipe-proof.
3.  **Check Community Logs:** The meta shifts with every update. Stay active in community discords to see which units are currently melting the newest bosses.
4.  **Placement Matters:** Even a god-tier Reaper can fail if placed poorly. Always aim for "bend" or "U-turn" sections of the map to maximize the AOE scythe swings.

## FAQ

**Q: Why is Reaper considered the best in the heroes summon tier list?**

A: The Reaper is ranked at the top because of its unique combination of high DPS and massive AOE. In the 2026 meta, being able to hit multiple enemies simultaneously while scaling damage from kills is the most efficient way to handle infinite waves and boss chains.

**Q: Can I beat the game using only A-tier units?**

A: Yes, A-tier units like Captain Dreadmore and Laser Cyborg are strong enough to clear most standard maps and mid-level infinite runs. However, to reach the top of the global leaderboards, you will eventually need the raw power of S-tier heroes.

**Q: How often does the heroes summon tier list change?**

A: The meta typically shifts every 2-3 months with major game updates. New towers or buffs to existing heroes can move a unit from B-tier to A-tier overnight. It is always wise to keep at least one copy of every hero in case they receive a "buff" in a future patch.

**Q: Is Bandit Nick ever worth using?**

A: Only in the first 10 waves of a starter map. His gold-stealing ability provides a tiny economic boost, but his lack of damage makes him a liability as soon as tanky enemies appear. Most players recommend rerolling him as soon as you have enough gems for a better summon.
