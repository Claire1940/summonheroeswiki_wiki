## Top Starter Units for Mid-Game Progression

Not every player will start with a Mythic or S-tier unit. Fortunately, several Epic-level heroes can carry you into the end-game if you invest in their "Mythic Star Level." These units are more accessible but offer high consistency for players still building their **summon heroes best team**.

### 1. Demon Hunter
The Demon Hunter is a fan favorite for his reliable range. His special attack shoots eight times, dealing massive damage that can reach nearly 100,000 in just a few seconds during the late game. He is excellent for crowd control and maintaining a safe distance from aggressive mobs.

### 2. Dragoon
Commonly seen at high star levels, the Dragoon is one of the best tanks in the game. Her "Piercing Energy Spears" ability chases enemies down, and her high movement speed allows her to reposition quickly to plug leaks in your defense.

### 3. Wind Samurai
Based on classic agile warrior archetypes, the Wind Samurai excels in specific environments like the Sakura area. His tornado ability clears entire lanes, dealing up to 400% of his base damage to everything in its path.

### 4. Slime Summoner
Summoners are currently considered "broken" or overpowered in the 2026 meta. The Slime Summoner shoots homing slime balls and brings three melee slimes onto the field. These slimes often draw aggro away from your main towers and heroes, acting as a perfect meat shield for bosses.

## Constructing a Summon Heroes Best Team: Synergy and Placement

A **summon heroes best team** is not just about stacking the highest damage dealers; it is about how those units interact with your towers. In 2026, the most successful summoners focus on "Placement Chains" and "Tower Charges."

### Aquatic and Naval Synergies
Units like the **Diver** and **Captain Dreadmore** thrive when the map is treated as a tactical battleground. The Diver’s aquatic power allows for aggressive drops into enemy packs, while Captain Dreadmore’s cannon support creates "Naval Battlegrounds." When paired, these units can chain waves together, ensuring that no enemy escapes the AOE overlap.

### The Power of Summoners
As noted by top players, summoners like the **Bear Tamer** and **Slime Summoner** are essential for solo pushes. Because the game's AI often targets summoned entities (like cubs, bears, or slimes), these heroes provide an infinite layer of defense that traditional "stat-stick" heroes lack. 

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid over-relying on B-tier units like the Fire Mage or Ice Mage in the late game. While their burn and freeze effects are helpful for mid-map farming, they lack the damage scaling required to survive boss waves in infinite mode.

## Advanced Strategies for 2026

To truly master the leaderboard, you must look beyond base stats and focus on "Mythic Star Levels." Even an Epic unit can outperform a base Legendary if it has been ascended properly.

1. **Focus on Star Levels:** A Mythic-star Demon Hunter or Cyber DJ can clear final levels that un-upgraded legendaries struggle with.
2. **Utilize Homing Mechanics:** In maps with high mobility enemies, units like the **Laser Cyborg** and **Spellblade** are vital because their beams and sparks track targets, preventing "leaks" that end your run.
3. **The "Siren" Strategy:** Using the **Mermaid** for her charm ability can disrupt enemy formations, allowing your high-DPS units like the **Reaper** to cleave through them without taking return fire.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the single best unit for a summon heroes best team in 2026?**

A: Currently, the **Reaper** is widely considered the best overall unit due to his god-tier AOE DPS and ability to scale his damage based on the health of the enemies he consumes.

**Q: Are summoners better than direct attackers?**

A: In the late game and infinite modes, summoners like the **Bear Tamer** are often superior because their summons draw enemy aggro, protecting your base and other units while dealing consistent damage.

**Q: Should I keep Bandit Nick or Swordsman?**

A: No. These are C-tier units intended for absolute beginners. You should aim to reroll these as soon as possible for at least a B-tier unit like the Fire Mage or, ideally, an A-tier unit like the Diver or Captain Dreadmore.

**Q: How do I beat the final levels with only Epic units?**

A: You must upgrade your Epics to "Mythic Star Level." Units like **Demon Hunter**, **Technomancer**, and **Cyber DJ** are fully capable of clearing end-game content once they have been ascended and properly paired with supportive towers.
