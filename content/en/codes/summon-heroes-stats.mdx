## Analyzing Summon Heroes Stats and Banner Probabilities

The core of your progression lies in the summoning area. Unlike many other gacha games, Summon Heroes features two distinct packs that rotate every 30 minutes. This rapid rotation means you must check back frequently if you are hunting for a specific hero. Each banner contains a curated selection of seven units, each with varying influence on your team's overall power.

| Rarity | Number of Units | Pull Chance | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mythic** | 1 | **0.1%** | Extremely rare; high base stats |
| **Legendary** | 1 | Variable | Guaranteed within 100 pulls (Pity) |
| **Epic** | 2 | Moderate | Solid mid-game carries |
| **Rare** | 3 | High | Essential for early game and fusion |

One of the most player-friendly features is the legendary pity system. You are guaranteed a legendary unit within 100 summons. However, a critical detail to remember is that the pity counter is **not shared** between different banners. If you invest 50 pulls into Banner A, those do not carry over to Banner B. Always commit to a single banner until you trigger the pity or pull your desired unit.

## Character Progression: Leveling and Fusion Mechanics

In many games, you need to pull the exact same hero multiple times to increase their star rating. Summon Heroes deviates from this by allowing you to use any unit of the same rarity as fusion material. This means a four-star Dragoon can be upgraded to a five-star unit by consuming any other four-star unit you currently own.

> **💡 Tip:** Avoid the "Auto Sell" feature for Rare and Epic units. These are vital "fodder" for fusing your main team members to higher star levels.

To facilitate this, you should keep a close eye on the Item Shop in the main hub. This shop resets every 5 minutes and often stocks:
*   **Summon Tickets:** For additional pulls.
*   **Rice Cakes:** Food items used to level up your units.
*   **Fusion Crystals:** Specialized currency required to perform the fusion process.

By consistently checking the [official Summon Heroes game page](https://www.roblox.com/) for updates and visiting the shop, you can stockpile the materials needed to boost your **summon heroes stats** without spending excessive gems.

## Dungeons and the Mythic Shard Economy

The 2026 update introduced a dedicated dungeon system, accessible through portals in the central hub. These dungeons are the primary source of high-tier resources and follow a strict 6-hour reset cycle. This means you can run these four times a day to maximize your gains.

| Dungeon Type | Boss Name | Boss HP | Primary Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Food Dungeon** | Sarif | **4,000+** | Level-up food, 250 Gems |
| **Fusion Dungeon** | Divine | **10,000+** | Fusion Crystals, 250 Gems |

The Mythic Dungeon is particularly important because it drops Mythic Shards. Collecting 50 of these shards allows you to purchase a guaranteed Mythic unit, such as the Emperor. If you are diligent with your daily tasks—running the Mythic Dungeon four times and completing the Nightmare Circus quest—you can earn a guaranteed Mythic hero every 10 days.

2.  **Mermaid (Epic):** Specializes in massive AoE (Area of Effect) damage. She is perfect for clearing waves in the Nightmare Circus or story missions quickly.

Viewing the specific **summon heroes stats** for these units is now easier than ever. Always look for units whose specials complement each other, such as a tank that can hold enemies in place for the Mermaid's AoE blast.

## Resource Management and Daily Activities

Efficiency is the name of the game in Summon Heroes. Since story missions now only provide rewards once every 24 hours, you must diversify your activities to keep your gem count high.

*   **Nightmare Circus:** This endless wave mode can be played three times a day. Reaching high levels (such as Wave 130) can net you over 300 gems in just a few minutes.
*   **AFK Dojo:** Even when you aren't actively playing, you can earn rewards. Staying in AFK mode generates approximately 500 gems per hour, along with gold and food items.
*   **PvP Arena:** Even if you lose a match, you are now rewarded with one PvP token. Winning grants three tokens. These can be exchanged for valuable growth materials in the arena shop.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be aware that the 24-hour reward lock on story missions was implemented to combat automated scripting. Do not rely on story farming for your primary gem income.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I check my Summon Heroes stats for specific units?**

A: You can view detailed **summon heroes stats** by navigating to your unit inventory and selecting a hero. This menu displays their current HP, attack patterns, and special ability descriptions.

**Q: Does the pity system carry over if the banner rotates?**

A: No. Each banner has its own independent pity system. If a banner rotates after 30 minutes, the progress you made on that specific banner does not transfer to the new set of heroes.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get a Mythic unit?**

A: The most reliable method is collecting 50 Mythic Shards. You can earn up to five shards per day by completing all four Mythic Dungeon resets and the Nightmare Circus daily quest, resulting in a guaranteed Mythic every 10 days.

**Q: Should I use gems to buy food in the shop?**

A: Generally, it is better to save your gems for summoning or dungeon resets. Level-up food is easily obtained through the AFK Dojo and the Food Dungeon without spending your premium currency.
