## Daily Quests and Achievement Milestones

Consistency is key in Summon Heroes. The Quest tab is divided into Daily Quests and long-term Achievements, both of which are essential for long-term gem accumulation.

### Daily Quests
Every 24 hours, the game refreshes a list of tasks. Completing the entire set of daily objectives typically nets around **350 to 500 gems**. These tasks usually involve:
*   Summoning a specific number of units.
*   Completing a set number of story stages.
*   Participating in challenge modes.

### Achievements
Achievements are one-time milestones that reward you for overall account progress. If you are a new player, focusing on these can net you nearly **5,000 gems** in your first week of play. Key achievements include reaching player level milestones and discovering new hero rarities.

## The Unit Index (Unit Decks) Method

A frequently overlooked method in **summon heroes how to get gems** is the Unit Index. Every time you summon a new hero that you haven't owned before, they are recorded in your "Unit Decks." 

To claim these rewards:
1.  Open the **Units** tab.
2.  Select **Unit Decks**.
3.  Browse through the different categories (Rare, Epic, Legendary).
4.  Click on any newly discovered unit icons to claim gem rewards.

This system rewards you for the "trash" units you pull while hunting for Legendaries. Even Rare and Epic units provide a small amount of gems that add up quickly as you fill out your collection.

## The Ultimate AFK Strategy: Story Mode Automation

The most effective method for high-level players to master **summon heroes how to get gems** is through AFK (Away From Keyboard) farming in Story Mode. This method allows you to accumulate thousands of gems overnight without active play.

### Setting Up the AFK Loop
To perform this correctly, you will need a basic auto-clicker tool. Since this is a common practice in Roblox tower defense and summoning games, it is generally accepted as long as it doesn't involve hacking the game files.

1.  **Select a Stage:** Go to the highest world you can comfortably clear quickly. Rookie Island or Ever Crisis Academy are popular choices.
2.  **Pick the Final Stage:** The last stage of any island usually offers the best resource-to-time ratio.
3.  **Positioning:** Start the stage and place your mouse cursor over the area where the **"Repeat Stage"** button appears after a victory.
4.  **Auto-Clicker Settings:** Set your auto-clicker to click every 5–10 seconds. This prevents the Roblox "Idle Kick" (which happens after 20 minutes of inactivity) because the game registers the clicks as active input.
5.  **Overnight Results:** Leaving this running for 8 hours can easily net you **10,000 to 15,000 gems**, depending on your clear speed.

> **💡 Tip:** Use units with fast animations and large AOE to decrease your "Time Per Clear." The faster you finish the stage, the more gems you earn per hour.
