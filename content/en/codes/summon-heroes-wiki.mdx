## Hero Classes and Team Synergy

Building a balanced team is more important than simply having the rarest heroes. A well-constructed team utilizes class synergies to overcome difficult bosses and PvP opponents. In the current 2026 meta, the "Trinity" setup (Tank, DPS, Support) remains the most reliable foundation for most content.

### Understanding Roles

1.  **Tanks:** These heroes have high health and defense stats. Their primary job is to soak up damage and protect your squishier units.
2.  **DPS (Damage Per Second):** These are your primary attackers. They can be physical (Melee/Ranged) or magical (Mages).
3.  **Support/Healers:** Essential for long battles, these units provide health regeneration, shields, and offensive buffs to your team.

| Role | Primary Stat | Best Position |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tank** | HP / Defense | Front Line |
| **Assassins** | Crit Rate / Speed | Flank / Back Line |
| **Mages** | Magic Power | Middle / Back Line |
| **Healers** | Mana Regen / Healing % | Back Line |

By consulting the **summon heroes wiki** for specific hero stats, you can determine which units provide the best "Elemental Resonance." Matching heroes of the same element often grants passive bonuses such as +15% Attack or +20% Health, which can be the difference between victory and defeat in high-level raids.

## Progression and Resource Farming

As you progress through the 2026 campaign, you will find that the cost of leveling up heroes increases exponentially. Efficient resource management is the hallmark of a skilled player. You should prioritize your "Main Carry" unit—usually a high-damage Epic or Legendary hero—to ensure you can clear content quickly.

### Daily Checklist for Maximum Efficiency

*   **Daily Quests:** Complete all five daily tasks to earn the "Daily Chest," which contains 50 Gems and upgrade materials.
*   **Resource Dungeons:** Use your daily entries for the Gold and XP dungeons. These are the most time-efficient ways to gather basic resources.
*   **PvP Arena:** Even if you don't win every match, participating in the Arena grants "Honor Points" that can be traded for rare hero shards in the shop.
*   **Guild Boss:** Join an active guild to participate in daily boss raids. The rewards are distributed based on total damage dealt, making it a great way for mid-game players to earn endgame gear.

> **Warning:** Avoid "spreading your resources too thin" by leveling up every hero you summon. Focus on a core team of five to six units until they reach the level cap.

## Evolution and Ascension

Once a hero reaches their initial level cap, you must "Evolve" them to continue their growth. This process usually requires duplicate copies of the hero or specific "Evolution Stones" found in elemental trials.

1.  **Ascension (Star Level):** Increases the hero's base stats and unlocks new passive skills.
2.  **Awakening:** A late-game mechanic that requires "Awakening Orbs." This transforms the hero's ultimate ability into a more powerful version.
3.  **Skill Upgrades:** Use "Skill Books" to increase the percentage modifiers on your hero's active abilities.

| Upgrade Type | Primary Requirement | Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level Up** | XP Potions / Gold | Small Stat Increase |
| **Evolution** | Duplicate Heroes | Level Cap Increase |
| **Awakening** | Awakening Orbs | Ability Transformation |

Managing these upgrades requires a steady stream of materials. This **summon heroes wiki** recommends focusing on "Ascension" first, as the raw stat boost from a 5-star unit often outweighs the benefits of skill upgrades for early to mid-game players.

## FAQ

**Q: How often are new codes added to Summon Heroes?**

A: New codes are typically released every two to four weeks, usually coinciding with major game updates or holiday events. You should check this **summon heroes wiki** regularly for the most up-to-date list of active rewards.

**Q: What is the best way to get Gems as a free-to-play player?**

A: The most consistent way to earn Gems is by completing daily and weekly quests, participating in the PvP Arena, and clearing new campaign stages for the first time. Special events and developer compensation codes are also significant sources of free currency.

**Q: Can I reset a hero to get my resources back?**

A: Yes, the game features a "Reset" function in the Hero Menu. For a small Gold fee, you can revert a hero to level 1 and reclaim 100% of the XP potions and Gold spent on them. Note that Evolution materials are usually not refunded.

**Q: Which hero should I pick from the "Selector" ticket?**

A: If you receive a Hero Selector ticket, it is generally best to choose a versatile Support or a high-tier Tank. While DPS units are exciting, a strong Support unit like "Aria" or a Tank like "Ironclad" can fit into almost any team composition and remain relevant much longer in the meta.
