## Evaluating What is the Best Legendary in Summon Heroes

When choosing a legendary unit, you must consider the specific role they fill in your trio. A balanced team usually requires a mix of single-target burst damage and wide-area crowd control. Recent updates have introduced two major contenders that have shifted the ranking of all existing units.

### The Technomancer: The Queen of Crowd Control
The Technomancer has quickly risen to the top of the tier list due to her incredible versatility in clearing waves of enemies. Her kit is designed for players who need to manage large groups of mobs simultaneously.

*   **Regular Attack:** Releases two massive waves of digital energy. These waves have an impressive range, often reaching across a significant portion of the battlefield to damage multiple enemies at once.
*   **Special Ability:** Summons eight seeking orbs. These orbs automatically track down targets, making her ideal for stages where enemies are scattered or highly mobile.

Many veteran players compare the Technomancer to "The Reaper." While she may not match the absolute raw damage output of a Mythic Reaper, she is significantly easier to obtain through the standard banner system. For the majority of the player base, her accessibility and high-impact AoE make her a primary candidate when asking **what is the best legendary in summon heroes**.

### Frame Rate: The Speed King
If your goal is efficiency and single-target elimination, Frame Rate is the undisputed champion. He currently holds the title of the fastest unit in the game, which is a vital stat for players looking to speed-run story missions or farm repetitive stages.

*   **Regular Attack:** A 20-hit combo that provides consistent DPS.
*   **Special Ability:** A lightning-fast 110-jab combo. This move is specifically designed to melt the health bars of bosses and elite enemies.

## Understanding Summoning Mechanics and Banners

To acquire these legendary units, you must master the summoning system. Banners in Summon Heroes are unique because they rotate every 30 minutes. This fast rotation means you need to keep a close eye on the summon area to ensure you are spending your gems when your desired legendary is featured.

Each banner typically contains:
1.  **One Legendary Unit** (The primary target)
2.  **Two Epic Units**
3.  **Three Rare Units**
4.  **One Mythic Unit** (0.1% drop rate)

> **💡 Tip:** The legendary pity system guarantees a legendary unit within 100 summons. However, remember that pity is **not shared** between the two different banner packs. Always stick to one pack until you hit your pity.

## Unit Progression: Fusing for Power

Getting a legendary is only the first step. To truly answer **what is the best legendary in summon heroes**, you have to look at their potential when fused. Unlike many other gacha games that require duplicate copies of the same hero, Summon Heroes allows you to use any unit of the same star rarity as fusion material.

For example, if you have a four-star Frame Rate, you can sacrifice any other four-star units (even common ones) to elevate him to five stars. This makes the progression of legendary units much more manageable for free-to-play players.

### How to Optimize Fusion:
1.  **Don't Auto-Sell:** Avoid the temptation to auto-sell Rare and Epic units. They are essential "fodder" for upgrading your Legendaries.
2.  **Check the Item Shop:** The shop resets every 5 minutes. Look for Fusion Crystals, which are the primary currency used to facilitate these upgrades.
3.  **Farm Food:** Use rice cakes and other food items to level up your units before fusing to ensure they are at peak performance.

## Farming Gems for Legendary Summons

Since a 10-pull costs 1,000 gems, you need a reliable way to generate currency. There are two primary methods to keep your gem count high so you can pull whenever a top-tier legendary appears on the banner.

### The Nightmare Circus
This is a daily activity that can be performed three times. It is an endless wave mode where the rewards scale with your progression. A strong AoE unit like the Technomancer is highly recommended here. Reaching level 130 can net you approximately 300 gems in under five minutes.

### AFK Mode
Located in the main dojo, the AFK mode is the most consistent way to earn gems while not actively playing. You can expect to earn roughly 500 gems per hour. This is the best way to prepare for a "pity run" on a banner featuring a unit like Frame Rate.

## Early Game Alternatives to Legendaries

While everyone wants a legendary, you may need strong lower-tier units to help you clear content until you hit your first pity. Some Rare units perform surprisingly well and can even outperform lackluster Legendaries in specific scenarios.

*   **Bear Tamer (Rare):** Widely considered one of the best value units in the game. He is easy to pull and provides excellent utility for early-game progression.
*   **Mermaid (Rare):** Offers solid AoE damage. A team consisting of two Mermaids and one Bear Tamer is a classic "starter meta" that can carry you through the initial story chapters.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not over-invest in Rare units once you have acquired a top-tier Legendary. Save your high-level Fusion Crystals for units like Frame Rate or Technomancer.

## Strategic Team Building in 2026

To maximize the effectiveness of your team, you should aim for a composition that covers all bases. A popular high-level strategy involves using two Technomancers for total battlefield control and one Frame Rate to handle the boss at the end of the stage.

If you are lucky enough to pull a Mythic like The Reaper, he should replace one of your Technomancers. However, for 99% of players, the Technomancer remains the most practical answer to **what is the best legendary in summon heroes** because of her balance between power and ease of acquisition.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best legendary in summon heroes for free-to-play players?**

A: The Technomancer is generally considered the best for F2P players. Her massive AoE digital waves and seeking orbs allow her to clear difficult content without requiring the hyper-specific gear or duplicate pulls often needed for other units.

**Q: Is Frame Rate better than Technomancer?**

A: It depends on the task. Frame Rate is superior for single-target damage and speed-running stages due to his 110-jab combo and high movement speed. Technomancer is better for clearing waves of multiple enemies and general progression.

**Q: How often do the summon banners change?**

A: The summon banners rotate every 30 minutes. Each rotation features a different legendary unit, so if you are looking for a specific hero, you should check the summon area frequently.

**Q: Should I use the auto-sell feature for Rare units?**

A: No, it is highly recommended that you keep your Rare and Epic units. They are vital for the fusion system, allowing you to upgrade your legendary units to higher star rarities.
