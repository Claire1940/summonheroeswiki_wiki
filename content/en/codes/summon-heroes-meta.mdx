## Understanding the Summoning System and Banners

The foundation of your progress lies in the summoning area. Unlike many other hero collectors, this title uses a high-frequency rotation system that can be overwhelming for new players. Banners rotate every 30 minutes, meaning if you don't see the unit you need for your specific **summon heroes meta** build, you only have a short wait before the pool refreshes.

Each banner pack contains a fixed distribution of rarities. Understanding these odds is crucial for managing your Gem economy.

| **Mythic** | 1 | **0.1%** |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Pity counters are unique to each banner and are not shared. If you start pulling on one pack, ensure you have enough Gems to hit the 100-pull threshold before the 30-minute timer expires, or you risk losing your progress toward a guaranteed Legendary.

## Mastering the Summon Heroes Meta: Fusion and Growth

One of the biggest mistakes new players make is enabling the "Auto-Sell" feature for Rare and Epic units. In the current **summon heroes meta**, every unit is a potential resource for power. The fusion system allows you to upgrade a unit's star rating by consuming any other unit of the same rarity.

For example, if you have a four-star Dragoon, you do not need another Dragoon to upgrade it. You can use any four-star unit in your inventory to ascend it to five stars. This makes "trash" pulls incredibly valuable as fodder for your primary carries.

### The Fusion Process

1.  **Identify your Carry:** Choose a unit with high base stats or essential utility (like Frame Rate or Bear Tamer).
2.  **Collect Fodder:** Keep all units of the same star level.
3.  **Acquire Fusion Crystals:** Visit the Item Shop frequently. The shop resets every 5 minutes and often sells Fusion Crystals of various rarities.
4.  **Ascend:** Combine the crystals and fodder units to increase your main unit's star rating, significantly boosting their health and damage output.

The **summon heroes meta** is currently defined by a few standout performers. While Legendary units like Techno Master offer flashy AoE (Area of Effect) capabilities, some lower-rarity units are essential for early-game progression due to their ease of acquisition.

As you transition into the late-game **summon heroes meta**, focus shifts toward **Frame Rate**. This unit is a single-target specialist known for extremely high attack speeds and a special move that involves dashing back and forth, dealing massive rapid-fire damage.

## Farming Resources and the AFK Loop

To sustain your summoning habits, you must maximize your Gem income. The 2026 update environment offers three primary ways to stay stocked on currency and upgrade materials.

### 1. The Nightmare Circus
This is an endless wave mode that can be challenged three times daily. It is the most efficient way to earn Gems quickly. Reaching Level 130 can net you approximately 300 Gems in under five minutes. Always use your best AoE units here to clear early waves as fast as possible.

### 2. AFK Mode (The Dojo)
For passive income, the AFK mode in the main dojo is indispensable. Staying in this mode yields roughly 500 Gems per hour, along with a steady stream of gold and food items (like rice cakes) used to level up your heroes.

### 3. The Item Shop
Because the shop resets every 5 minutes, it is a goldmine for dedicated players. You can find:
*   Summon Tickets
*   High-rarity Fusion Crystals
*   Food for XP boosts

> **💡 Tip:** Set a timer for 5 minutes while playing to remind yourself to check the shop. It is the most reliable way to stock up on legendary fusion crystals without spending Gems.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the most important unit in the current summon heroes meta?**

A: For early game, the **Bear Tamer** is widely considered the best due to his accessibility and high power ceiling. For late-game bossing, **Frame Rate** is the premier choice for single-target DPS.

**Q: How do I get more Gems quickly?**

A: The fastest active way is completing your three daily **Nightmare Circus** runs. For passive income, leaving your character in **AFK Mode** in the Dojo provides 500 Gems per hour.

**Q: Should I sell my Rare units?**

A: No. You should use them as fusion fodder to upgrade your stronger units. The only time you should consider selling is if you have already maxed out the star ratings of your entire main roster and are low on gold.

**Q: How does the pity system work?**

A: You are guaranteed a Legendary unit within 100 summons on a specific banner. However, the pity does not carry over to other banners, and because banners rotate every 30 minutes, you must be careful with your timing.
