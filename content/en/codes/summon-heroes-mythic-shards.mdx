## Understanding the Mythic Summoning System

The introduction of Mythic units has shifted the endgame focus significantly. Unlike lower-tier units that rely heavily on RNG (random number generation) during standard summons, Mythic units are tied to a guaranteed progression system. By accessing the summon screen, you will find the **Mythic Pack**. This pack requires a specific threshold of shards to activate.

Currently, players need to collect exactly **50 Mythic Shards** to claim a guaranteed Mythic unit. This system provides a clear "pity" mechanic, ensuring that consistent gameplay is rewarded regardless of luck. The first major Mythic unit released under this system is **The Emperor**, a powerhouse character that has quickly become a staple for clearing high-difficulty content and dominating the PvP leaderboards.

## How to Farm summon heroes mythic shards

Acquiring shards is a multi-faceted process that involves daily engagement with several game modes. Because the rewards are time-gated, missing a single day can set your progression back significantly. Below are the primary methods to secure your daily intake of shards.

### The Mythic Dungeon
The most consistent source of shards is the Mythic Dungeon. Located in the central hub’s new portal area, this dungeon is part of a three-part system (Food, Fusion, and Mythic). The Mythic Dungeon specifically pits you against **The Reaper**, a boss with approximately 46,000 HP. While the health pool seems daunting, a well-geared team at level 100+ should be able to clear this encounter with ease.

The dungeon operates on a **6-hour reset cycle**. This means that in a 24-hour period, you can potentially run the dungeon four times, yielding a total of 4 shards per day.

### Daily Quests and Nightmare Circus
Beyond the dungeons, the developers have integrated shards into the daily quest rotation. Specifically, look for the quest requiring you to complete the **Nightmare Circus** three times. This map is slightly more complex than standard story missions but remains manageable for mid-to-high level players. Completing this daily quest grants an additional shard, bringing the daily free-to-play total to 5 shards.

> **💡 Pro Tip:** If you are efficient with your 6-hour dungeon resets and complete your daily quest, you can earn a guaranteed Mythic unit every **10 days**.

### PvP Leaderboard Rewards
For those who enjoy competitive play, PvP offers a lucrative, albeit challenging, way to supplement your shard income. The daily rankings provide shards based on your standing at the end of the day.

## Dungeon Boss Mechanics and Rewards

To successfully farm **summon heroes mythic shards**, you must be prepared for the various bosses within the dungeon portals. Each dungeon offers distinct rewards that are vital for unit progression.

### 1. Food Dungeon (Boss: Sarif)
*   **HP:** ~4,000
*   **Rewards:** Coins, 250 Gems, and various Food items.
*   **Strategy:** Use high-speed attackers to quickly deplete Sarif’s low health pool. This is the easiest dungeon and should be used to stock up on gems for other summons.

### 2. Fusion Dungeon (Boss: Divine)
*   **HP:** ~10,000
*   **Rewards:** Coins, 250 Gems, and Fusion Crystals.
*   **Strategy:** Divine has more sustain than Sarif. Utilize units with high single-target damage to bypass her defenses.

### 3. Mythic Dungeon (Boss: Reaper)
*   **HP:** ~46,000
*   **Rewards:** Coins, 250 Gems, and **1 Mythic Shard**.
*   **Strategy:** The Reaper is the tankiest boss currently in the dungeon rotation. It is recommended to use units like **Frame Rate** (for fast single-target DPS) or **Techno Master** (for consistent AoE if adds are present). Despite the high HP, the Reaper does not possess high burst damage, making it a battle of attrition that most level 100+ players will win.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Story missions now only provide rewards once every 24 hours to combat automation. Focus your active playtime on dungeons and PvP to ensure you don't waste time on non-rewarding story replays.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I trade summon heroes mythic shards with other players?**

A: No, Mythic Shards are currently an untradeable currency bound to your account. You must earn them through dungeons, quests, or PvP rewards.

**Q: Does the Mythic Dungeon difficulty scale with my level?**

A: The dungeon difficulty is currently static. While the Reaper has 46,000 HP, it does not get stronger as you level up, making it easier to farm as you reach the new level 120 cap.

**Q: What happens if I already have the featured Mythic unit?**

A: If you collect 50 shards and already own the featured unit, you may want to save your shards for the next rotation. Check the Unit Index frequently to see when new Mythic units are scheduled for release.

**Q: Is there a way to get more than 5 shards a day without PvP?**

A: Currently, the 4 shards from dungeons and 1 from the daily quest are the only guaranteed PvE sources. Occasionally, the developers release codes (like the "Sunset" code) that may provide materials, but shards are strictly limited to daily activities to maintain game balance.
