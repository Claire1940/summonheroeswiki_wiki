## Optimizing Your Demon Knight: Stats and Traits

To truly unlock the potential of the **summon heroes roblox demon knight**, you must focus on the stat rerolling system. In Summon Heroes, stats are ranked by letters, with **"X"** being the highest possible grade for any given attribute.

### Stat Rerolling Priorities

1.  **Attack (Grade X):** This is non-negotiable for the Demon Knight. Since its main utility is AoE burst, you need the highest possible base damage.
2.  **Cooldown Reduction:** Minimizing the time between "Demon Lord's Wrath" activations is key for PvP dominance.
3.  **Critical Chance:** Enhances the burst potential of the Shadow Wave projectile.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Rerolling stats becomes exponentially more expensive the more stats you "lock." Only lock a Grade X Attack stat if you have a significant gold surplus, as the price can quickly drain your reserves.

Traits are another critical component, though they are much harder to acquire. Currently, in 2026, traits are primarily obtained through limited-time [official Roblox game codes](https://www.roblox.com/) or the in-game item shop. Look for traits that provide "Lifesteal" or "Tenacity" to keep your Demon Knight in the fight longer.
