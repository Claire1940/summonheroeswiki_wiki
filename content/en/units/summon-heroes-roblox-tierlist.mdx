## S-Tier: The God-Tier Conquerors

Units in the S-tier are the pinnacle of power within the **summon heroes roblox tierlist**. These heroes do not just defend; they dominate. They are capable of rewriting map history by clearing waves that would otherwise result in an instant wipe for lower-ranked squads.

### Reaper
The Reaper is widely considered the ultimate DPS unit in 2026. Utilizing massive AOE scythe swings, this hero consumes enemy health pools with a "scythe surge" that scales as the waves get tougher. If you are looking to turn your defense into a "feast" of gems, the Reaper is your primary target. Its dark regal aesthetic is matched only by its ability to rule the battlefield through aggressive placements.

### Bear Tamer
The Bear Tamer offers a unique playstyle by summoning bear phantoms to nuke packs from a distance. This unit is perfect for solo pushes and players who enjoy "placement kings" strategies. The bear judgment ability allows you to loop enemies into a stun-lock cycle, making it a nightmare for high-health bosses.

### Demon Knight
As the ultimate combo DPS, the Demon Knight provides AOE pinnacle power. Its demon buffs echo through the match, providing spikes in damage that can shatter even the most resilient foes. It is a versatile god carry that shines particularly bright when its "awakening" is triggered, turning any map into a legend zone.

### Seraph & Divine
While the Reaper and Demon Knight focus on destruction, the Seraph and Divine units focus on empowerment.
*   **Seraph:** Provides holy buffs that act as a heavenly guardian for your other towers.
*   **Divine:** Offers supreme power buffs that are essential for deep leaderboard runs.

## B-Tier: The Situational Fillers

The B-tier represents a significant step up from the starters, but these units fade quickly when high-level bosses appear. They are the "decent" picks you keep while hunting for better rolls.

*   **Ice Mage:** Offers freeze effects that are useful for controlling trash packs. However, deeper waves often shrug off the ice, leading to leaks.
*   **Fire Mage:** Provides a burn DOT (Damage Over Time). While it feels powerful on starter lanes, it lacks the burst damage required for chaos waves.
*   **Archer:** A safe range pick for beginners. The lack of AOE (Area of Effect) means it gets looped easily by swarms.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not over-invest your upgrade materials into B-tier units. They are temporary placeholders and will eventually be replaced by A or S-tier heroes.

## C-Tier: The Reroll Candidates

If your first roll lands in this category, it is generally recommended to reroll immediately. These units offer basic damage that "fizzles" in real waves, often feeling like you are placing paper units against legends.

1.  **Swordsman:** A basic melee unit that feels like a "toothpick" in boss fights. It is fine for absolute beginners but lacks range and sustain.
2.  **Bandit Nick:** A sneaky unit with minor gold-stealing mechanics. Unfortunately, its damage is so low that enemies usually ignore your tricks and walk straight to your base.

## Mastering the Summon Heroes Roblox Meta

To truly succeed, you must treat your heroes like map treasures rather than gambles. Mastery of the **summon heroes roblox tierlist** involves more than just knowing who is at the top; it requires understanding how to pair towers and manage your gem economy.

### Effective Tower Pairing Strategies
*   **Slow & Burn:** Pair an Ice Mage (B-Tier) with a Fire Mage (B-Tier) in the early game to maximize DOT while enemies are slowed.
*   **The Divine Shield:** Place your Seraph near your Reaper. The holy buffs will significantly increase the Reaper's scythe cleave damage.
*   **Bear Traps:** Use the Bear Tamer's summons at the start of a lane to group enemies together, allowing an AOE unit like the Demon Knight to wipe them all at once.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the absolute best hero in the summon heroes roblox tierlist?**

A: Currently, the Reaper is widely considered the best hero due to its massive AOE damage and scaling potential in infinite modes. However, the Bear Tamer is a close second for those who prefer a summoner-based playstyle.

**Q: Should I spend gems to upgrade a B-tier unit?**

A: It is generally better to save your gems for summoning higher-tier units. Only upgrade a B-tier unit if you are stuck on a specific map and need a small power boost to progress to the next gem-farming stage.

**Q: How often does the meta change in Summon Heroes?**

A: The developers frequently release updates that introduce new towers and balance changes. It is important to check the latest **summon heroes roblox tierlist** every few months to stay ahead of the curve.

**Q: Is Bandit Nick actually useful for gold farming?**

A: While Bandit Nick has gold-stealing "nudges," the amount of gold gained is usually offset by the fact that he cannot clear waves effectively. You will earn more gold by clearing waves faster with a higher-damage unit like the Diver or Captain Dreadmore.
