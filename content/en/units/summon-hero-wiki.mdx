Ascension requires duplicate hero shards and elemental essences. Each Ascension tier unlocks a new passive skill or significantly enhances an existing ability.

1.  **Collect Shards:** Obtain these via the Summoning Altar or the "Hero Shop" using duplicate currency.

## Mastering the Elemental Advantage

The elemental system is a core pillar of the **summon hero wiki** strategy guide. Ignoring these matchups can lead to a 25% damage penalty, while exploiting them grants a 25% damage bonus and increased critical hit chance.

*   **Fire** beats **Earth**
*   **Earth** beats **Water**
*   **Water** beats **Fire**
*   **Light** and **Dark** are mutually effective against each other but neutral to the basic triad.

When facing a difficult boss in the campaign, check the boss's element first. Even a lower-tier hero with an elemental advantage will often outperform a top-tier hero with an elemental disadvantage. This is why maintaining a diverse roster is more effective than "maxing out" just five units.
