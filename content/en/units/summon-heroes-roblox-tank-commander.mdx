## Strategic Placement in Leviathan's Eye

Leviathan's Eye remains one of the most challenging environments to test your **summon heroes roblox tank commander**. Because this stage features multiple lanes and high-health "sponge" enemies, placement is everything.

1.  **The Choke Point:** Place your Tank Commander at the first major bend in the path. This ensures that the splash damage from its shells hits the maximum number of enemies as they cluster together.
2.  **The Support Buffer:** Surround the Tank Commander with units like the Technomancer. The Technomancer’s digital waves can soften up the shields of incoming mobs, allowing the Tank Commander's physical shells to deal lethal damage.
3.  **The Clean-up Crew:** Position Frame Rate further down the line. If any high-health enemies survive the **summon heroes roblox tank commander**'s initial barrage, Frame Rate's 110-jab combo will ensure they don't reach your base.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not place the Tank Commander too close to the spawn point on maps with teleporting enemies. Its slow turn speed makes it vulnerable to units that can bypass the front line.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the summon heroes roblox tank commander better than the Technomancer?**

A: It depends on the situation. The Tank Commander is superior for raw physical AoE damage and holding a specific point. However, the Technomancer is better for wide-area crowd control and is generally easier for free-to-play players to obtain and utilize effectively.

**Q: What is the best map to use the Tank Commander on?**

A: Any map with narrow corridors or tight turns, such as Leviathan's Eye Stage 1 or the Abandoned Factory. These maps force enemies to bunch up, maximizing the effectiveness of the Tank Commander's splash damage.

**Q: Can I use multiple Tank Commanders in one match?**

A: Yes, you can place multiple units, but keep in mind the diminishing returns on placement space. In 2026, it is often more efficient to have one highly leveled **summon heroes roblox tank commander** supported by speed units like Frame Rate rather than multiple low-level tanks.

**Q: Does the Tank Commander hit air units?**

A: By default, the Tank Commander targets ground units. You will need to equip a "Radar Module" or pair it with an anti-air specialist to handle flying enemies effectively in later waves.
