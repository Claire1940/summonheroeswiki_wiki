## FAQ

**Q: What is the best map for summon heroes resource farming as a beginner?**

A: For new players, Chapter 3-8 is the most efficient. The enemies are weak enough to be cleared with the starting hero, and the Gold-to-Stamina ratio is excellent for early-game upgrades.

**Q: How often should I spend my Gems on summons?**

A: It is generally recommended to save your Gems for "Pity Banners" or "Double Rate" events. Spending Gems haphazardly on standard banners will slow down your overall progress. Wait until you have enough for at least 50 pulls to guarantee a high-tier unit.

**Q: Are the Auto-Farm scripts safe to use in 2026?**

A: While many players use them to speed up their **summon heroes resource farming**, they are never 100% safe. Game moderators frequently update their anti-cheat detection. It is always safer to use the official in-game Auto-Battle system, even if it is slightly slower.

**Q: Does player level affect drop rates?**

A: Player level does not directly increase the drop rate percentage, but it does unlock higher-tier difficulty modes which have significantly better loot tables. Reaching Level 50 should be your first priority to unlock the "Hell" difficulty story maps.
