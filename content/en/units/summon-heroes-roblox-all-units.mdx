## The Unit Index and Gem Rewards

A major quality-of-life improvement added in 2026 is the **Unit Index**. This feature allows players to view all **summon heroes roblox all units** categorized by their rarity. Not only does this help you track your collection, but it also serves as a significant source of free currency.

When you summon a new unit for the first time, you can claim gems directly from the index. For example, summoning a Legendary unit like Techno Master can grant up to **800 free gems**. Even if you owned the units prior to the update, you can still go back into the menu to claim your rewards.

1.  Open the **Unit Tab** in the main menu.
2.  Click the **Unit Decks UI** in the top right corner.
3.  Scroll through the rarity tabs (Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythic).
4.  Claim gems for every unique hero you have summoned.

> **Tip:** Use the gems earned from the index to buy Legendary Fusion Crystals, which are essential for evolving your S-tier heroes.
