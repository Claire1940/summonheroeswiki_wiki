## Strategic Combat Applications

When deploying the **technomancer summon heroes** unit, positioning is key. Her digital waves have an impressive range, often extending further than the visual indicators might suggest. This makes her particularly potent in narrow corridors or stages where enemies spawn in dense clusters.

### Farming Story Missions
Because her Digital Energy Waves can "take over the battlefield," the Technomancer is an S-tier unit for farming lower-level story missions. By the time the enemies have fully spawned, her waves have usually already crossed the screen, resulting in instant clears. If you pair her with a movement-speed buff or a unit like Frame Rate, your clear times will drop significantly.

### Mastering Leviathan’s Eye
In high-level content like Leviathan's Eye, the Technomancer’s value comes from her Seeking Orbs. These eight orbs do not miss. In stages where enemies move quickly or have erratic flight patterns, the auto-seeking nature of her special ability ensures that no damage is wasted. 

1. **Initiate with Waves:** Cast your regular attack as soon as the wave spawns.
2. **Observe Health Bars:** If the enemies are at 30% health or lower, save your special.
3. **Deploy Orbs:** For elite enemies or scattered mobs, trigger the Seeking Orbs to maintain momentum.

> **Warning:** While the Technomancer is excellent at crowd control, she can be vulnerable to high-burst assassins if she is left without a frontline. Always pair her with a tank or a high-speed distractor.
