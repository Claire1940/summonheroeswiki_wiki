## The Role of Healers and Support in 2026

The 2026 meta for Summon Heroes has shifted significantly with the introduction of high-armor enemies and complex map layouts. While early-game maps can be cleared with basic melee units like the Swordsman, late-game content requires sophisticated utility. A **summon heroes roblox healer** or support unit typically provides one of three things: health regeneration for the base/units, damage buffs, or crowd control.

With the level cap increased to 120, the scaling of enemy health means that your towers need to stay active longer. Support units like the Seraph and Divine have become mandatory for players looking to survive the "Sunset City" stages. These units don't just sit on the sidelines; they act as force multipliers that decide if you are "base trash or a leaderboard emperor."

## Top Tier Healer and Support Units Ranked

When looking for a **summon heroes roblox healer** equivalent, you are looking for units that provide "Holy" or "Divine" buffs. These are the S-Tier anchors that top-tier summoners use to push deep into infinite waves.

The Seraph is widely considered the premier "Angel" unit in the game. If the Seraph is the guardian, the Divine is the god of the battlefield. This unit provides the ultimate buffs that scale with your level. When paired with high-DPS units like the Reaper, the Divine ensures that every scythe swing deals maximum devastation. While not a traditional "healer" in the sense of a medic, the Divine’s ability to prevent damage through superior buffing makes it an essential part of any **summon heroes roblox healer** search.

### Mermaid (A-Tier)
The Mermaid serves as a specialized support unit that uses "Charm" to disrupt enemy flow. By turning enemies against each other or simply stopping them in their tracks, she provides the breathing room your towers need to recover. She is particularly effective in the darker, more atmospheric maps where her siren queen vibes shine.

## How to Optimize Your Healer Placements

To get the most out of your **summon heroes roblox healer**, follow these strategic placement steps:

1.  **Identify the "Kill Zone":** This is the area of the map where your highest DPS units (like Reaper) are concentrated.
2.  **Centralize Support:** Place your Seraph or Divine units so their aura covers the entire Kill Zone.
3.  **Chain Your Buffs:** If you have multiple support units, space them out to ensure that enemies are constantly under the effect of slows or charms while your units stay buffed.
4.  **Monitor Cooldowns:** Use the new UI features to track when your support abilities are active, especially during boss fights like Cyber DJ Nikki.
