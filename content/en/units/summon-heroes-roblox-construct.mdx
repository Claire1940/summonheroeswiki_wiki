## Understanding the Construct Mechanic in Summon Heroes

The "Construct" system in Summon Heroes refers to the specialized units and structures that provide utility, buffs, and automated defense. Unlike standard hero summons that move across the field, constructs are typically stationary or have limited mobility, focusing instead on controlling the battlefield. In 2026, the developers expanded this system to include "Modular Constructs," which allow players to swap out components mid-match to adapt to enemy compositions.

To effectively utilize a **summon heroes roblox construct**, you must first gather "Blueprint Shards" found in the higher-tier dungeons. These shards allow you to unlock the construction menu during a match. Once unlocked, your hero can spend Mana or specialized "Scrap" currency to deploy these structures. The synergy between your active heroes and your passive constructs often determines the efficiency of your resource generation.

> **💡 Tip:** Always prioritize placing a "Resource Extractor" construct within the first two minutes of a match to ensure a steady flow of Scrap for late-game upgrades.

**Q: Do constructs benefit from hero buffs?**
