## Understanding the Summoning Banners

Before you can dive into fusion, you must first acquire the units that serve as your "fodder" and your "leads." The summoning area in Summon Heroes is unique because it features two distinct packs that rotate every 30 minutes. This fast rotation means that if you don't see a unit you need for a specific fusion recipe, you only have a short wait before the pool refreshes.

Each banner contains a total of seven units, ranging from common rarities to the elusive Mythic tier. Understanding the pull rates is essential for managing your Gem economy.

| Unit Rarity | Number per Banner | Pull Chance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mythic** | 1 | 0.1% |
| **Legendary** | 1 | Variable (Pity at 100) |
| **Epic** | 2 | Moderate |
| **Rare** | 3 | High |

A single summon costs 100 Gems, while a "10-pull" costs 1,000 Gems. One of the most player-friendly features in 2026 is the legendary pity system, which guarantees a legendary unit within 100 summons. However, a crucial tactical note: **pity is not shared between banners**. If you are chasing a specific legendary, ensure you commit your Gems to a single banner type before it rotates.

## The Summon Heroes Fusion System Explained

The **summon heroes fusion** mechanic is the primary way to increase the star rarity of your favorite characters. Unlike many other gacha games that require "duplicates" of the exact same hero to evolve, Summon Heroes allows for much more flexibility. As long as the units share the same star rarity, they can be used as fusion material.

For instance, if you have a four-star Dragoon that you want to elevate to five stars, you do not need another Dragoon. You can take any other four-star unit in your inventory and fuse it into the Dragoon.

### Step-by-Step Fusion Process

1.  **Navigate to the Hero Menu:** Select the unit you wish to upgrade (the "Base Unit").
2.  **Verify Star Rarity:** Ensure you have "Fodder Units" that match the current star level of your Base Unit.
3.  **Apply Fusion Crystals:** Ensure you have the required rarity of Fusion Crystals (found in the Item Shop).
4.  **Execute Fusion:** Confirm the selection to consume the fodder and crystals, resulting in a higher-star hero.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Never enable the "Auto-Sell" feature for Rare and Epic units in the summon menu. While it may seem like an easy way to clear inventory, these units are the essential "fuel" for your high-level fusions. Selling them early will severely bottleneck your progress in the mid-game.

## Resource Management: Gems and Fusion Crystals

To keep the **summon heroes fusion** engine running, you need a steady supply of Gems and Fusion Crystals. The game provides several avenues to farm these, but some are significantly more efficient than others.

### The Item Shop Strategy
The Item Shop in the main hub is a goldmine for progression. It resets every 5 minutes, making it one of the most frequently updating shops in the genre. You should check back often to buy:
*   **Fusion Crystals:** These come in different rarities and are mandatory for the fusion process.
*   **Summon Tickets:** Occasionally appear for a discount compared to direct Gem summons.
*   **Rice Cakes/Food:** These items are used to "bubble up" or level your units, which is necessary before they can be fused.

### Farming the Nightmare Circus
The Nightmare Circus is a daily endless wave mode that serves as your primary Gem income. You can enter this mode three times per day. The further you progress through the waves, the higher the rewards.

| Wave Reached | Time Spent | Gem Reward (Approx.) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 50** | 2 Minutes | 100 Gems |
| **Level 100** | 3.5 Minutes | 220 Gems |
| **Level 130+** | 4.5 Minutes | **300+ Gems** |

## Maximizing Gains with AFK Mode

If you are unable to actively play, the AFK mode in the main dojo is your best friend. In 2026, the passive income rates have been balanced to ensure that even casual players can keep up with the **summon heroes fusion** requirements. By simply staying in the dojo, you gain a variety of rewards every hour.

*   **Gems:** Approximately 500 per hour.
*   **Gold:** Used for shop purchases and basic upgrades.
*   **Unit Food:** Essential for leveling up units to prepare them for fusion.

> **💡 Tip:** Always leave your character in the AFK dojo before logging off for the night. A standard 8-hour sleep cycle can net you 4,000 Gems, which is enough for four 10-pulls the following morning.

## Advanced Fusion Strategies for 2026

As you progress, the cost of fusion increases. To optimize your roster, you should adopt a "Tiered Fusion" approach. Instead of trying to upgrade every unit you pull, focus on one "Carry" unit.

| Fusion Priority | Unit Type | Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Priority 1** | Main DPS (e.g., Dragoon) | Reach 5-Stars ASAP |
| **Priority 2** | Support/Healer | Reach 4-Stars for survivability |
| **Priority 3** | Fodder Units | Level up to match Base Unit stars |

By focusing your Fusion Crystals on a single powerful unit, you can push deeper into the Nightmare Circus, which in turn provides the Gems needed to pull more fodder for your secondary units. It is a self-sustaining cycle that rewards specialization over generalization.

## FAQ

**Q: Does the summon heroes fusion process ever fail?**

A: No, fusion in Summon Heroes is 100% guaranteed to succeed as long as you have the correct star-rarity fodder and the required Fusion Crystals. There is no "break" or "fail" chance.

**Q: Can I use a higher-star unit as fodder for a lower-star unit?**

A: No. The units used as fodder must exactly match the current star rarity of the base unit you are trying to upgrade. For example, a 4-star unit requires other 4-star units to become a 5-star.

**Q: Should I save my Gems for Mythic units only?**

A: While Mythic units are powerful (0.1% chance), the **summon heroes fusion** system makes Legendary and Epic units highly competitive. It is often better to have a 5-star Legendary than a 1-star Mythic. Use your Gems to build a consistent team first.

**Q: How often should I check the Item Shop?**

A: Ideally, every 5 to 10 minutes if you are actively playing. Since it resets every 5 minutes, frequent checks are the only way to stockpile enough Fusion Crystals for high-level evolutions.
