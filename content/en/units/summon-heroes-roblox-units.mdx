## Top Team Compositions for 2026

Building a team is about more than just stacking Mythics. You need synergy between your **summon heroes roblox units** to handle different enemy types. A balanced team usually consists of a tank/crowd controller, a high-damage single-target unit, and a utility or AoE specialist.

### Recommended Meta Team: The Immortal Frontline
1.  **The Reaper (Mythic):** Functions as your primary crowd control and off-tank through his spirits.
2.  **The Emperor (Mythic/High Legendary):** Provides massive damage buffs and secondary frontline support.
3.  **The Demon Knight (Legendary):** Acts as a pure tank to soak up damage while the Reaper's spirits deal chip damage.

This composition is particularly effective in narrow passages, such as the bridges in Sunset City, where enemies are forced into a bottleneck. By combining the Reaper’s red energy waves with the Demon Knight’s defensive stance, you can clear stages with minimal health loss.

## Exploring Sunset City

Sunset City is widely considered one of the most visually stunning maps in the game. Beyond its looks, the level design forces players to adapt their strategies. The map features holographic billboards, hotel lobbies, and tight corridors that favor units with piercing attacks or large AoE footprints.

When tackling Stage 5, the final boss **Cyber DJ Nikki** presents a significant challenge. She uses high-frequency attacks that can quickly deplete your units' HP. Using a unit like Frame Rate, who can dash out of the way of her choreographed attacks, is often more effective than trying to "tank and spank" with slower units.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the best summon heroes roblox units for beginners?**

A: For players just starting out, focusing on Epic units like Spectre is a great choice. However, the new Legendary units Frame Rate and Techno Master are incredibly powerful and relatively easy to use if you manage to pull them early.

**Q: How do I use the "Sunset" code in Summon Heroes?**

A: Open the game menu, navigate to the codes section, and enter "Sunset" to receive five legendary fusion crystals. These are vital for upgrading your units to their maximum potential in 2026.

**Q: Is the Reaper better than the Barbette boss?**

A: While the Reaper is one of the best player-controlled units, bosses like Barbette have significantly higher HP pools (over 1.5 million). The Reaper can deal substantial damage (300k+) solo, but he requires a full team to actually take down a boss of that caliber.

**Q: How does the Unit Index work?**

A: The Unit Index tracks every hero you have ever summoned. By clicking the icon in the top right of the unit menu, you can claim gem rewards for reaching collection milestones. This works retroactively, so even units you owned before the update will count toward your rewards.
