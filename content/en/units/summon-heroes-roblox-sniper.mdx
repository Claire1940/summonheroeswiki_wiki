## How to Get Guaranteed Mythic Units

One of the most praised additions in the 2026 update is the deterministic path to Mythic units. You no longer have to rely purely on luck. By collecting **50 Mythic Shards**, you can purchase a guaranteed Mythic Pack from the summon screen.

### Daily Shard Farming Routine
To maximize your efficiency, follow this daily checklist to earn your shards:
*   **Dungeon Runs:** Complete the Mythic Dungeon every 6 hours (up to 4 shards per day).
*   **Daily Quest:** Complete the "Nightmare Circus" three times (1 shard per day).
*   **Total:** 5 shards per day.

By maintaining this routine, you can secure a new Mythic unit every **10 days**. This makes the **summon heroes roblox sniper** units much more accessible to free-to-play players who are willing to put in the time.

## Navigating the Story Mission Controversy

Not all changes in 2026 have been met with universal acclaim. The developers implemented a strict limit on story missions to combat the use of auto-clickers and scripts. Currently, you can only receive rewards from story missions **once every 24 hours**.

While this has frustrated some players who enjoy "grinding" for hours, it has shifted the focus toward Dungeons and PvP. To make up for this, the rewards for the first clear of the day have been significantly buffed, but once you've finished your daily run, your progression options are limited to other game modes.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt to use third-party scripts to bypass the story mission lockout. The 2026 anti-cheat system is highly aggressive and can lead to permanent account bans.

## Advanced Sniper Tactics for Boss Fights

When facing high-HP bosses like the Reaper, your positioning is everything. Follow these steps to ensure your snipers stay active throughout the fight:

1.  **Identify the Aggro:** Place a high-HP tank (like the Paladin or Golem) directly in front of the boss.
2.  **Max Distance:** Place your **summon heroes roblox sniper** units at the absolute edge of their range.
3.  **Manual Ult Timing:** Don't use your special attacks immediately. Wait for the boss to finish their "Invulnerability" or "Shield" phase before unleashing your snipers' bursts.
4.  **Buff Stacking:** Use support units that provide "Range Up" or "Crit Damage" buffs to multiply your sniper's effectiveness.
