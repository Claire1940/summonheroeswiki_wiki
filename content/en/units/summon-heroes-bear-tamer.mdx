## Advanced Strategies for the Bear Tamer

Mastering the **summon heroes bear tamer** requires more than just placing the unit and watching it work. To maximize its potential, you must understand the "summon flow" and how to pair it with specific towers.

### Optimal Placement Chains
The Bear Tamer thrives when placed in a way that its phantoms can intercept enemies at choke points. Unlike the Reaper, which needs to be in the thick of the fight to use its scythe, the Bear Tamer can be tucked slightly behind your primary towers. This positioning keeps the hero safe while its bear summons do the heavy lifting.

> **💡 Expert Tip:** Use the Bear Tamer in conjunction with the Seraph or Divine units. The holy buffs from these S-tier supports will significantly increase the damage of the bear nukes, allowing you to clear boss waves that would otherwise wipe your base.

### Gem Management and Rerolling
Because the **summon heroes bear tamer** is an S-tier unit, the odds of rolling it are lower than common units like Swordsman. In the 2026 update, it is recommended to save your gems for "Hero Lord" events where the drop rates for beast-type heroes are slightly boosted. Avoid "rage-quitting" after a string of bad rolls; the meta shifts frequently, but the Bear Tamer has remained a consistent top-performer due to its unique mechanics.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the summon heroes bear tamer better than the Demon Knight?**

**Q: What happens if I roll a C-tier unit like Bandit Nick?**
