## Strategic Placement and Team Synergy

To get the most out of your **summon heroes emperor**, you cannot simply place him and walk away. He is most effective when supported by "tank" or "defender" heroes. The core strategy in 2026 involves using high-durability heroes to stall the enemy advance, keeping them within the Emperor's kill zone for as long as possible.

Once the enemy heroes or bosses have their stamina or health depleted by your frontline, the Emperor acts as the "closer." His projectiles and beams will effectively execute weakened units, preventing them from reaching your base. This synergy is vital for clearing the hardest difficulty settings in [Roblox Summon Heroes](https://www.roblox.com), where enemy health scaling becomes aggressive.

### Recommended Team Roles

1.  **The Frontline Tank:** Units with high health or stun abilities to group enemies together.
2.  **The Buffer:** Support units that increase the attack speed or damage of Mythic units.
3.  **The Emperor:** Your primary damage source, positioned slightly behind the frontline.
4.  **The Slow/Stunner:** Units that reduce enemy movement speed, making the Emperor's tracking even more lethal.

## Advanced Tactics for Boss Elimination

When facing giant bosses, the **summon heroes emperor** should be your primary focus for upgrades. Because his projectiles track, you don't have to worry about the boss moving out of range as much as you would with other units. However, the timing of the "5 beams" skill is critical.

Wait for the boss to enter a "vulnerable" state or a slow-moving phase before triggering the skill. If the boss has a dash or teleport mechanic, wait until immediately after they use it. This ensures that all five beams connect for the full duration of the ability. Furthermore, ensure that your other heroes are drawing "aggro" (enemy attention), allowing the Emperor to fire his ballistics without interruption.

> **Warning:** Do not leave the Emperor unprotected. While he has immense offensive power, his defensive stats are lower than specialized tank units. A single breach in your frontline can lead to his swift defeat.
