## Enhancing Your Heroes: Fusion and Leveling

One of the most unique features of Summon Heroes in 2026 is the fusion system. Unlike other Roblox games that require you to have multiple copies of the exact same unit to upgrade them, this game allows you to use any units of the same star rarity as "fodder."

For example, if you have a four-star **summon heroes roblox bear tamer**, you can take any other four-star unit you own—even a basic Swordsman or Bandit Nick—and fuse it into the Bear Tamer to elevate it to five-star status.

### Efficient Leveling Materials
1. **Fusion Crystals:** Available in the Item Shop. These come in different rarities and are essential for the fusion process.
2. **Rice Cakes/Food:** Use these items to "bubble up" your units, increasing their base level and stats before fusion.
3. **Item Shop Rotation:** The shop resets every 5 minutes. Check back frequently to buy summon tickets and fusion supplies.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid using the "Auto-Sell" feature for Rare and Epic units. While it might seem like an easy way to get quick cash, these units are vital fusion fodder for your S-tier heroes.

## Advanced Strategy: The "Bear and Siren" Combo

When starting out, one of the most effective team compositions involves pairing the Bear Tamer with the Mermaid. The Mermaid provides a "charm" ability that slows and groups enemies together. Once the enemies are clustered, the Bear Tamer’s "Bear Step" nukes can hit the maximum number of targets simultaneously.

To optimize this:
1.  Place the Mermaid near the start of the lane to apply the charm/slow effect.
2.  Place the Bear Tamer in a central location where its summon range covers the "kill zone" created by the Mermaid.
3.  Focus your fusion resources on the Bear Tamer first, as its damage scaling is superior to the Mermaid’s in the late game.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get the summon heroes roblox bear tamer if it's not on the current banner?**

A: Banners rotate every 30 minutes. If the Bear Tamer isn't currently available, wait for the next rotation. You can also occasionally find summon tickets in the Item Shop that might help you pull from specific pools.

**Q: Is the Bear Tamer better than the Reaper?**

A: They serve different roles. The Reaper is a melee-range DPS powerhouse, while the Bear Tamer is a ranged summoner. In many high-level strategies, players use both units together to cover the entire map.

**Q: Can I use any unit for fusion in Summon Heroes?**

A: Yes, as long as the units have the same star rarity. You do not need duplicate Bear Tamers to upgrade your primary Bear Tamer; you can use any common or rare unit that you have leveled up to the same star rank.

**Q: What is the fastest way to farm gems for summoning?**

A: The most efficient active method is completing your three daily Nightmare Circus runs. For passive farming, leaving your character in the AFK Dojo will grant you approximately 500 gems per hour.
