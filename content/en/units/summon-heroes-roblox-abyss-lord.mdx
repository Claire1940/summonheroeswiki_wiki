## Understanding the Summoning System

The heart of progression in Summon Heroes lies in the summoning area. Unlike many other Roblox titles, this game utilizes a dynamic banner system that rewards patience and timing. Banners rotate every 30 minutes, meaning if you don't see the unit you want, you only have a short wait before the pool refreshes.

Each summon pack contains a specific distribution of rarities. Understanding these odds is crucial before you start spending your hard-earned gems.

| Unit Rarity | Pull Chance | Units per Banner |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mythic (Abyss Lord)** | **0.1%** | 1 |
| **Legendary** | **1.0%** | 1 |
| **Epic** | **10.0%** | 2 |
| **Rare** | **88.9%** | 3 |

A single summon costs 100 gems, while a 10-pull costs 1,000 gems. One of the most player-friendly features in 2026 is the **Legendary Pity System**. You are guaranteed a legendary unit within 100 summons. However, it is vital to remember that pity is not shared between the two different packs. If you are hunting for a specific unit, stick to one banner until you trigger the pity or pull your target.

## Efficient Gem and Resource Farming

To pull for the **summon heroes roblox abyss lord**, you will need a steady stream of gems. Relying solely on quest rewards will slow your progress. Instead, utilize the specialized game modes designed for resource accumulation.

### Nightmare Circus
This is a daily activity that can be completed three times per day. It functions as an endless wave mode where rewards scale based on your performance.
*   **Time Efficiency:** A strong run to wave 130 can net you approximately 300 gems in under 5 minutes.
*   **Strategy:** Use AoE units like the Mermaid or the Abyss Lord to clear early waves quickly.

### AFK Mode
For players who cannot actively play throughout the day, the AFK Dojo is a lifesaver. By simply leaving your character in the Dojo, you passively accumulate wealth and experience.
*   **Gem Rate:** Approximately 500 gems per hour.
*   **Bonus Rewards:** You will also receive food items (like rice cakes) and gold, which are necessary for leveling up your heroes.

## The Fusion System: Building 5-Star Heroes

One of the most innovative features in Summon Heroes is the flexible fusion system. In many other games, you need duplicates of the exact same unit to upgrade them. Here, the requirements are based on rarity rather than name.

To upgrade a four-star unit (like a Dragoon) to a five-star unit, you simply need any other four-star units to use as fodder. This makes the **summon heroes roblox abyss lord** even more potent, as you can use common legendaries or high-tier epics to boost its star level without needing to pull multiple 0.1% copies.

> **💡 Tip:** Regularly visit the Item Shop in the main hub. It resets every 5 minutes and often carries Fusion Crystals, summon tickets, and high-quality food. Stocking up on these materials is the fastest way to prepare for a major unit upgrade.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get the summon heroes roblox abyss lord?**

A: The Abyss Lord is a Mythic unit that can be pulled from the summon banners. It has a 0.1% drop rate. You should save your gems and wait for a banner rotation where the Abyss Lord is the featured Mythic unit to maximize your chances.

**Q: Does the pity system carry over between different banners?**

A: No. Each of the two summon packs has its own independent pity counter. If you have 90/100 pity on Pack A, it will not apply to Pack B. Always focus your summons on one pack at a time to ensure you hit the 100-pull guarantee.

**Q: Is the AFK mode worth using in 2026?**

A: Absolutely. With a rate of 500 gems per hour plus gold and food items, it is the most consistent way to progress while you are not actively playing. It is highly recommended to leave your character in the Dojo whenever you are away from your device.

**Q: Which units should I use for fusion fodder?**

A: Generally, you should use units that you have multiple copies of or those that do not fit into the current meta. Since any unit of the same star rarity can be used as fodder, focus on using Rares and Epics that you have already maxed out to upgrade your Legendaries and Mythics.
