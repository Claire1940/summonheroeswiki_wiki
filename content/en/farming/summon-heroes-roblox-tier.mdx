## S-Tier: The God-Tier Guardians

Units in the S-Tier are the pinnacle of power. These heroes don't just defend; they rewrite the history of the map by obliterating waves and melting bosses before they can even touch your base.

### Reaper
The Reaper is widely considered the king of DPS in the current **summon heroes roblox tier**. Utilizing a massive scythe with a wide AOE (Area of Effect), this unit consumes the health of enemies to fuel its own power. Its scythe surges can clear entire packs in a single swing, making it the premier choice for players looking to push deep into infinite mode.

### Bear Tamer
If you prefer a more tactical approach, the Bear Tamer offers unparalleled "nuke" potential. By summoning bear phantoms from afar, this hero can loop enemies into a cycle of "bear judgment," effectively stalling and destroying swarms simultaneously. It is a versatile pick for those who enjoy aggressive unit placement.

### Demon Knight
The Demon Knight is the ultimate combo-based hero. Its strength lies in its ability to provide demon buffs that scale over time. Many top-tier players use the Demon Knight to anchor their defense, as its awakening state turns the map into a "legend zone" where even the toughest bosses vanish under its historic might.

### Seraph & Divine
These two units function as the supreme support and buffing duo. The Seraph provides holy buffs that act as a heavenly guardian for your other towers, while the Divine offers supreme power boosts. When paired together, they create a "holy route" defense that is nearly impossible for enemies to penetrate.

## A-Tier: Reliable Mid-to-Late Game Anchors

A-Tier heroes are the backbone of any successful squad. While they might not possess the raw "one-shot" potential of S-Tier units, they are highly reliable and can carry you through most of the game's content.

| Hero | Specialty | Key Strength |
|---|---|---|
| **Diver** | Aquatic Control | Closes gaps and combos well with placement chains. |
| **Captain Dreadmore** | Cannon Support | Excellent for naval battleground maps and trail chains. |
| **Mermaid** | Charm Ability | Siren queen vibes that allure and distract tanky waves. |
| **Laser Cyborg** | Precision Beams | Futuristic damage that excels at focused boss melting. |

The **Diver** is particularly notable for its "dive" ability, which allows it to reposition or strike from unexpected angles. Meanwhile, **Captain Dreadmore** and the **Mermaid** are favorites for players who enjoy "theme" builds that utilize specific map mechanics to their advantage.

## B-Tier: Situational Utility Picks

The B-Tier consists of heroes that are "decent but replaceable." They often provide a specific elemental effect, such as freezing or burning, which can be useful in the mid-game but tends to fall off during high-wave chaos.

*   **Ice Mage:** Provides essential slow effects. While the damage is mediocre, the freeze control can buy your heavy hitters more time.
*   **Fire Mage:** Focuses on DOT (Damage Over Time). Great for clearing trash mobs on starter maps, but often "flops" against bosses with high health regeneration.
*   **Archer:** A safe, long-range pick. It lacks the AOE required for later waves but is a precise tool for picking off stragglers.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid over-investing your upgrade materials into B-Tier units. Save your rarest resources for the A and S-Tier heroes you will eventually pull.

## C-Tier: The Starter Struggle

Every player starts somewhere, but in the **summon heroes roblox tier**, the C-Tier is a place you want to leave quickly. These units offer basic damage that "fizzles" when faced with real pressure.

1.  **Swordsman:** A basic melee unit. It feels like a "toothpick" in boss fights and lacks the range or sustain to handle leaks.
2.  **Bandit Nick:** While his gold-stealing "nudges" sound useful for farming, his damage is so low that he often becomes a liability in later waves.

## Strategic Summoning and Gem Management

To climb the **summon heroes roblox tier** rankings, you must manage your gems with precision. The summoning system is built on RNG, but smart players know how to tip the scales in their favor.

*   **Reroll Early:** If your first few summons are only C-Tier, consider starting fresh or focusing all your early quest gems on a high-tier banner.
*   **Tower Pairing:** Even an S-Tier unit like the Reaper performs better when paired with a support unit like the Seraph. Always look for synergies that multiply your damage output.
*   **Map Awareness:** Some heroes shine on specific maps. For example, the Diver and Captain Dreadmore are significantly more effective on maps with water lanes or naval themes.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best unit in the current summon heroes roblox tier?**

A: Currently, the **Reaper** and **Bear Tamer** are tied for the top spot. The Reaper excels at massive AOE damage and wave clearing, while the Bear Tamer offers superior crowd control and burst damage through its summons.

**Q: Should I keep Bandit Nick for the extra gold?**

A: While Bandit Nick's gold-stealing mechanics are a nice "tease" for beginners, he lacks the damage to survive mid-game waves. Most pros recommend swapping him for a Fire Mage or Archer as soon as possible to avoid "bandit flops" during boss waves.

**Q: How often does the summon heroes roblox tier list change?**

A: The meta typically shifts with every major update or when new "Divine" or "Seraph" units are introduced. It is important to check community discords and updated guides at least once a month to ensure your team is still viable for the leaderboards.

**Q: Can I beat the game using only A-Tier units?**

A: Yes! A-Tier units like the Diver and Laser Cyborg are strong enough to clear most standard maps and boss defenses. However, to reach the top of the global leaderboards or survive past wave 100 in infinite mode, you will eventually need S-Tier carries.
