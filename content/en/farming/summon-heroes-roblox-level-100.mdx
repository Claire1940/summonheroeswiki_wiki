## The Fusion and Evolution System

One of the most player-friendly aspects of **summon heroes roblox level 100** is the fusion system. Unlike other games that require you to pull multiple copies of the exact same unit to evolve it, this game utilizes "Fusion Crystals" and fodder units of the same rarity.

To evolve a unit to a higher star rating, you generally need:
1.  A **Fusion Crystal** of the appropriate rarity.
2.  Three units of the same rarity (they do not need to be the same character).
3.  A specific amount of gold.

### Evolution Requirements

| Target Star Level | Required Fodder | Currency Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **4-Star** | 3 Rares + Rare Crystal | 5,000 Gold |
| **5-Star** | 3 Epics + Epic Crystal | 15,000 Gold |
| **6-Star** | 3 Legendaries + Legendary Crystal | 50,000 Gold |

This flexibility allows you to turn a three-star Rare into a five-star powerhouse by sacrificing less useful Epic units you may have pulled. It makes every summon valuable, even if you don't get the specific Mythic you were hunting for.

## Economic Strategy: Gold vs. Gems

In many Roblox titles, gold becomes useless in the late game. In **summon heroes roblox level 100**, however, gold remains a vital resource throughout your journey.

### Gold Utility
Gold is primarily used for **Stat Rerolls**. While many games force you to spend premium gems to fix a unit's bad stats, Summon Heroes allows you to use your farmed coins. This makes the grind for a "perfect" unit much more accessible for free-to-play participants.

### Gem Utility
Gems are still the primary currency for summoning new units. Since the nerf to story mode repetition, you should focus on:
*   Completing daily loyalty rewards.
*   Using active codes (e.g., "Dungeons" for Mythic Shards).
*   Utilizing the AFK Chamber when not actively playing.

## PvP Mechanics and Strategies

The PvP system in Summon Heroes is a free-for-all battle within a randomized teleportation circle. Up to four players are placed in an arena where their units battle automatically.

To succeed in PvP:
1.  **Prioritize Targets:** You can click on specific enemy monsters to command all your units to focus fire on that target.
2.  **Trait Synergy:** Ensure your units have traits that complement their roles (e.g., Attack Speed for Jester, Damage for Reaper).
3.  **Token Management:** Winning a PvP match nets you three tokens, while losing still grants one. These tokens are essential for the PvP shop, which often contains exclusive evolution materials.

## Advanced Tips for Leveling

Reaching the level cap requires a combination of story progression and efficient farming. By the time you reach the later worlds, you will encounter bosses that require specific elemental or role compositions.

*   **Map Exploration:** Don't ignore the chests scattered around the world maps. In 2026, these chests have a revised drop table that includes Epic and Legendary Fusion Crystals.
*   **Unit Prioritization:** When stuck on a boss, use the "Focus" mechanic to ensure your high-damage units aren't wasting their attacks on minor minions.
*   **Trait Rerolling:** Even a Rare unit with a "Godly" trait can outperform a Mythic with a "Poor" trait. Use your gold to optimize your main team's traits early on.
