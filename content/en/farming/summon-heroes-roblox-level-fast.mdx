## Troubleshooting Your AFK Setup

Even the best setups can run into issues. If you find that your character is standing idle or the level isn't restarting, check the following:

1.  **Screen Resolution:** Macros are often resolution-dependent. If you recorded your macro in windowed mode and switched to fullscreen, the click coordinates will be wrong.
2.  **Internet Stability:** A brief lag spike can cause the "Ready" button to appear later than expected. Using the 2x speed "multi-click" method in TinyTask helps mitigate this.
3.  **Hero Death:** If your heroes are under-leveled for the stage, they might die before reaching the boss. Always test your setup for at least three manual cycles before leaving it unattended.
