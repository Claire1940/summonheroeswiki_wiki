## Understanding Mythic Shards and the Mythic Pack

Mythic Shards are a specialized currency introduced to provide a "pity" alternative for the rarest units in the game. Unlike standard gems, which are used for randomized pulls on the main banners, shards are accumulated toward a guaranteed reward. 

To use these shards, you must navigate to the Summoning area and locate the **Mythic Pack**. This pack requires a total of **50 Mythic Shards** to open. Once you have collected the full amount, you are guaranteed a Mythic-tier unit, such as the Emperor. This system is designed to reward consistent daily play, allowing dedicated players to obtain top-tier units every 10 days if they maximize their efficiency.

> **💡 Tip:** Do not spend your shards individually; they have no use until you reach the 50-unit threshold required to trigger the Mythic Pack opening.

## Primary Methods to Farm Summon Heroes Roblox Mythic Shards

As of the latest 2026 updates, there are two primary ways to obtain shards. Both methods are time-gated, meaning you cannot simply grind them indefinitely in a single sitting. Consistency is the key to success here.

### 1. The Mythic Dungeon
The Dungeon system is the most lucrative source of shards. Located in the central hub, the dungeon portals offer three distinct challenges: Food, Fusion, and Mythic. To get your shards, you must focus on the **Mythic Dungeon**.

*   **Boss:** The Reaper (approx. 46,000 HP).
*   **Cooldown:** 6 Hours.
*   **Rewards:** 1 Mythic Shard, 250 Gems, and Gold Coins.

Because the dungeon resets every six hours, you can technically run it four times in a 24-hour period if you manage your schedule effectively. This provides a total of 4 shards per day from dungeons alone.

### 2. Daily Quests: Nightmare Circus
The second method involves completing your daily objectives. The most important quest for high-level players is the requirement to run the **Nightmare Circus** three times. 

The Nightmare Circus is an endless wave mode where rewards scale based on how long you survive. Reaching level 130, for instance, can net you around 300 gems in just a few minutes. Once you complete the three required runs for the daily quest, you will be rewarded with **1 Mythic Shard**.

## Optimizing the Summoning Banner System

While **summon heroes roblox mythic shards** provide a guaranteed path, you will still be spending a significant amount of time at the standard summon banners. These banners rotate every 30 minutes, changing the available units.

### Banner Odds and Costs
Each pull costs 100 gems, or 1,000 gems for a 10-pull. The game features a legendary pity system that guarantees a legendary unit within 100 summons. However, it is vital to remember that **pity is not shared between banners**. If you start pulling on one banner, try to reach the pity threshold before the banner rotates, or you may lose your progress toward that specific legendary.

| Unit Rarity | Pull Chance | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Mythic** | 0.1% | Extremely rare; use shards for a guarantee. |
| **Legendary** | ~1-2% | Pity at 100 summons. |
| **Epic** | Varies | Useful for early-game and fusion. |
| **Rare** | Common | **Bear Tamer** is a top-tier rare to watch for. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid using the "Auto-Sell" feature for Rare and Epic units. These units are essential for the fusion system, which allows you to upgrade your main team's star rating.

## Advanced Progression: Fusion and AFK Farming

To make the most of your **summon heroes roblox mythic shards**, you need a team strong enough to clear the Mythic Dungeon quickly. This is where fusion and gem farming come into play.

### The Fusion Mechanic
In Summon Heroes, you don't necessarily need duplicates of the same unit to upgrade them. As long as two units share the same star rarity, you can fuse one into the other to increase its power. For example, any 4-star unit can be used as "fodder" to upgrade your primary 4-star Dragoon into a 5-star powerhouse.

To perform these upgrades, you need **Fusion Crystals**. These can be obtained from:
1.  **The Fusion Dungeon:** Rewards 250 gems and various crystals every 6 hours.
2.  **The Item Shop:** Resets every 5 minutes in the main hub. Check this frequently to buy crystals and summon tickets.

### Gem Farming in 2026
Since you need gems for both summons and shop items, you should utilize the **AFK Mode** located in the main dojo. While active, you will passively earn approximately 500 gems per hour, along with food items to level up your units. This is the most efficient way to maintain your gem count while you are away from your computer or console.
