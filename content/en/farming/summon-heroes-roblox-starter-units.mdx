## Understanding the Summoning Mechanics

Before diving into specific units, you must understand how to acquire them. The summoning area in the main hub features two distinct packs that rotate every 30 minutes. Each pack contains a specific pool of heroes, making timing your summons crucial if you are hunting for a particular character.

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| **Summon Cost** | 100 Gems per unit / 1,000 Gems for a 10-pull |
| **Banner Rotation** | Every 30 minutes |
| **Legendary Pity** | Guaranteed Legendary within **100 summons** |
| **Mythic Chance** | 0.1% (Extreme rarity) |
| **Pity Sharing** | No; each banner maintains its own separate pity counter |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not enable the "Auto-Sell" feature for Rare and Epic units early on. While it might seem like an easy way to clear inventory, these units are vital for the Fusion system, which is the primary way to increase your team's star rating.

## Top-Tier Summon Heroes Roblox Starter Units

Many new players make the mistake of assuming only Legendaries are viable. In the current 2026 meta, several Rare and Epic units outperform higher-rarity heroes due to their ease of acquisition and specific skill sets. When looking for **summon heroes roblox starter units**, keep an eye out for the following:

### The Bear Tamer (Rare)
Despite being a 3-star Rare unit, the Bear Tamer is widely considered one of the best units in the game. His ability to tank damage while dealing consistent melee strikes makes him an anchor for any early-game team. Because he is a Rare unit, you can easily pull multiple copies or find him frequently on banners, making him a staple for your roster.

### The Mermaid (Rare)
The Mermaid is the queen of Area of Effect (AoE) damage in the early stages. Her ability to strike multiple enemies at once is essential for clearing waves of slimes and bandits quickly. A common high-efficiency strategy involves running two Mermaids alongside a frontline tank like the Bear Tamer.

### The Dual Wielder (Epic)
If you manage to pull the Dual Wielder, you have secured a premier frontline fighter. With a 14.5% drop rate on certain banners, he is more accessible than Legendaries but offers significantly higher HP and defense than standard sword-users. He excels at "tanking" bosses while your ranged units chip away at their health bars.

## How to Farm Gems for More Summons

To acquire the best **summon heroes roblox starter units**, you need a steady stream of Gems. The game provides several avenues for resource gathering that every player should utilize daily.

1.  **The Nightmare Circus:** This is an endless wave mode that you can attempt three times per day. The further you progress, the higher the Gem reward. Reaching level 130 can net you approximately 300 Gems in under five minutes.
2.  **AFK Dojo Mode:** Located in the main hub, the AFK Dojo allows you to earn rewards passively. You can expect to gain roughly **500 Gems per hour** simply by leaving your character in this zone, along with substantial amounts of food and coins.
3.  **The Item Shop:** Check the shop every 5 minutes. It frequently stocks Summon Tickets and Fusion Crystals for coins, allowing you to save your Gems for high-value banner rotations.
4.  **Stage Progression:** Each new stage you clear for the first time provides a Gem bonus. Focus on pushing through the main map to build your initial bank.

> **💡 Tip:** Always prioritize the Nightmare Circus runs before logging off for the day. It is the most time-efficient way to boost your Gem count.

## The Fusion System: Making Units Stronger

One of the most unique aspects of Summon Heroes is the Fusion system. Unlike other Roblox titles that require identical duplicates to upgrade a character, this game allows you to use any unit of the same star rarity as "fodder."

For example, if you have a 4-star Dragoon and want to make it a 5-star, you do not need another Dragoon. You can take any other 4-star unit you don't plan on using and fuse it into your main hero. This makes every single pull from a banner valuable, as even "trash" units contribute to the growth of your top-tier heroes.

To facilitate this, you will need **Fusion Crystals**. These can be found as loot drops from bosses or purchased in the Item Shop. Keep a healthy stock of these crystals so you can upgrade your units the moment you have enough fodder.

## Early Game Strategy Walkthrough

When you first start, you will be given three basic heroes to fight the Slime Summoner boss. This is a tutorial phase that will naturally bring your units to level 2. Once you reach the lobby, your first priority should be your first "real" summon.

Use your starting gems to pull from a banner. If you get an Epic or a high-tier Rare, immediately equip them. Use the **"Equip Best"** button in your inventory to ensure your highest-statted units are on the field. From there, head to Stage 1.

As you fight through the forest and castle maps, watch your units' health bars. If your frontline (like the Dual Wielder) is taking too much damage, consider using "Rice Cakes" or other food items found in the shop to level them up mid-session. Higher levels provide a significant boost to base HP and damage, which is often the difference between victory and defeat against the area bosses.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best way to get summon heroes roblox starter units as a new player?**

A: The most efficient way is to farm the AFK Dojo for an hour to get 500 Gems, then wait for a banner rotation that features the Bear Tamer or Mermaid. These units are easier to pull than Legendaries but provide excellent early-game power.

**Q: Should I save my Gems for a 10-pull?**

A: While there is no direct discount for a 10-pull (it costs exactly 1,000 Gems), it is often faster. However, if you are looking for a specific unit on a 30-minute rotation, doing single pulls can help you stop the moment you get what you need, saving Gems for the next banner.

**Q: How does the pity system work in Summon Heroes?**

A: You are guaranteed a Legendary unit within 100 summons. However, be careful: the pity does not carry over between the two different banners. You should pick one banner and stick with it until you hit your Legendary drop to avoid wasting your progress.

**Q: Why can't I fuse my units?**

A: Ensure that both the unit you are upgrading and the fodder unit have the same star rarity. You also need a Fusion Crystal of the corresponding rarity, which can be bought from the Item Shop that resets every 5 minutes.
