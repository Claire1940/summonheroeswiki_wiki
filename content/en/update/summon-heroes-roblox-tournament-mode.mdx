## Advanced Strategies for Tournament Success

Once you have your team and your resources, it is time to focus on the gameplay tactics that separate the top players from the rest. In the **summon heroes roblox tournament mode**, timing is everything. 

1. **Mana Management**: Do not spend all your mana in the first wave. Save a portion of your resources for "Emergency Summons" if a boss enemy breaks through your initial line of defense.
2. **Placement Synergy**: Place your support units in the center of your formation to ensure their auras cover as many DPS units as possible.
3. **Target Prioritization**: Manually target high-threat enemies like healers or speed-boosters. Letting a healer stay alive in the backlines can make a wave nearly impossible to clear.
4. **Hero Awakening**: Use your duplicate heroes to "Awaken" your main tournament team. This provides a significant stat boost that is often the difference between a win and a loss in the Grand Finals.

> **Warning:** Be cautious of "Glass Cannon" builds. While they deal massive damage, one lucky hit from a boss can wipe your entire front line, leading to an instant defeat in tournament play.
