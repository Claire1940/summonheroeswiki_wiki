## Understanding the Gacha and Pity System

Summoning is the heart of the game. You utilize Gems and Tickets to pull for units ranging from Rare to Mythic. While the allure of Mythic units is strong, the current 2026 meta suggests that a well-built Legendary team is more than sufficient for most content.

The game features a **Legendary Pity System** at 100 pulls. This means if you fail to pull a Legendary unit within 99 summons, the 100th is guaranteed. Currently, there is no hard pity for Mythic units, making them exceptionally rare and prestigious.

### Recommended Units for Progression

*   **Abyss Lord (Legendary):** A premier tank with a striking blue underworld aesthetic. While his animations are slower than some, his ability to soak up damage in PVP and high-level PvE is unmatched.
*   **Jester (Legendary):** A high-mobility DPS unit that attacks from a safe distance. The Jester is excellent for "hit and run" tactics against bosses with large AOE attacks.
*   **Mermaid (Legendary):** A consistent ranged attacker with crowd control capabilities. She is vital for slowing down waves in the Sakura Village maps.
*   **Bear Tamer (Rare):** Surprisingly, the Bear Tamer is considered one of the best non-Legendary units. Her summon provides an extra meat shield on the field, which is essential for solo players.

### Optimizing Stats (S and X Tier)

Every unit has four main stats: Health, Attack, Speed, and Cooldown (CD). Speed affects how quickly you clear stages, while Cooldown allows your units to spam their special abilities more frequently.

> **💡 Tip:** Use your Gold to reroll unit stats. Always aim for **S or X Tier** stats in Attack and Cooldown. Once you land an X-tier stat, remember to **lock it** before rerolling the remaining slots to avoid losing your progress.

## Farming Strategies and Map Progression

If you find yourself stuck at a specific level, such as the Evercrest Academy or the daunting Leviathan's Eye, it is time to farm. The **summon heroes roblox console update** has optimized several maps for efficient resource gathering.

### Best Farming Locations

1.  **Map 4, Stage 4 (The Canyon):** This is widely considered the best spot for mid-game farming. The map is small, the waves spawn quickly, and the drop rates for Food (used for leveling) and Fusion Crystals are high.
2.  **The AFK Lobby:** If you need to step away from your console, head to the AFK map. Your units will attack training dummies, generating a passive stream of Gold and Gems. The server will not kick you for inactivity in this specific zone.
3.  **Nightmare Circus:** This endless mode tests your endurance. It is the primary source for high-tier equipment and cosmetic rewards.

When tackling the final map, **Leviathan's Eye (7-5)**, positioning is key. Do not let your units huddle together, as many bosses in this area use massive AOE (Area of Effect) attacks that can wipe a clustered team in seconds. Use the manual control to spread your heroes into separate corners of the arena.

## FAQ

**Q: Does the summon heroes roblox console update support cross-play?**

A: Yes, players on console can play seamlessly with those on PC and mobile. Your progress is saved to your Roblox account, allowing you to switch between platforms.

**Q: What is the best stat to focus on for legendary units?**

A: For most DPS units like the Jester, focusing on **Cooldown (CD)** and **Attack** is the most effective strategy. Lower cooldowns allow for more frequent use of special abilities, which often provide invincibility frames or massive AOE damage.

**Q: How do I get more Fusion Crystals?**

A: Fusion Crystals are common drops from boss stages in Maps 4 and above. You can also purchase them in the daily shop using Gems or earn them through the Nightmare Circus mode.

**Q: Is there a pity system for Mythic units in 2026?**

A: Currently, there is no guaranteed pity for Mythic units. They remain the rarest tier in the game, though players can survive and clear all current content using optimized Legendary and Epic units.
