## Mastering the New Dungeon System

The central hub now features two glowing portals leading to the Dungeon area. These are not just standard missions; they are timed challenges that reset every six hours. This means efficient players can run these up to four times a day to maximize their rewards. 

There are currently three distinct dungeons available, each catering to a specific resource need:

### 1. Food Dungeon
Designed for players looking to level up their units quickly. The boss, **Sarif**, is relatively easy for high-level players but provides essential experience materials.

### 2. Fusion Dungeon
This is the go-to spot for players looking to evolve their units. The boss, **Divine**, guards a hoard of fusion crystals. Since the update moved some crystals from regular rewards to first-time rewards, this dungeon is now the primary repeatable source for these items.

### 3. Mythic Dungeon
The most important dungeon for those seeking the **summon heroes roblox new mythic**. You will face the **Reaper**, a boss with significantly higher health than the others, though still manageable with a well-built team like the Reaper/Jester meta.

| **Fusion** | Divine | 10,000+ | 250 Gems, Coins, Fusion Crystals |
| **Mythic** | Reaper | 46,000+ | 250 Gems, Coins, **Mythic Shard** |

## How to Farm Mythic Shards Efficiently

Since you need 50 shards for the **summon heroes roblox new mythic**, efficiency is key. Currently, there are limited ways to acquire these shards, making a daily routine essential for any player aiming for the top tier.

1. **Dungeon Completions:** The Mythic Dungeon provides one shard per completion. Since it resets every 6 hours, you can earn 4 shards every 24 hours.
2. **Daily Quests:** A new daily quest requires players to complete the "Nightmare Circus" three times (Level 25+ requirement). Completing this task awards 1 Mythic Shard.
3. **Limited-Time Codes:** Developers occasionally release codes (like "Dungeons") that provide a small boost to your shard count.

By combining the daily quest with four dungeon runs, you can secure **5 shards per day**. This means a guaranteed The Emperor unlock every **10 days**.

## Controversial Story Mission Changes

One of the most discussed aspects of the 2026 update is the change to story mission rewards. To combat the rise of auto-clickers and scripts that were inflating the game's economy, developers have implemented a "once-per-day" reward limit on story stages.

While this protects the game's long-term health, it removes the primary method many players used for endless grinding. If you have already cleared your daily story missions, your best bet for progression is now the **AFK Mode**. The developers have increased the incentives for AFK play, making it the most viable way to earn gems and experience while waiting for dungeon timers to reset.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Attempting to bypass the daily story reward limit with scripts may result in account flags. Stick to the intended Dungeon and AFK cycles to stay safe.

## Quality of Life: Unit Details and PvP

## Combat Strategy for Dungeons

When tackling the Mythic Dungeon to get your **summon heroes roblox new mythic** shards, team composition is vital. Many top-ranked players are currently utilizing a combination of the Reaper and Jester units. 

A popular community tip involves the **Jester's Auto-Ability**. Many players recommend turning *off* the auto-use for Jester's special ability. Because his basic melee attack has a much faster animation and higher DPS (Damage Per Second) than his slower special moves, keeping him on basic attacks often results in faster boss clears.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get the summon heroes roblox new mythic unit, The Emperor?**

A: You must collect 50 Mythic Shards. These are obtained by completing the Mythic Dungeon (resets every 6 hours) and finishing the daily Nightmare Circus quest. Once you have 50, you can redeem them in the Mythic Pack section of the summon screen.

**Q: Why am I not getting rewards from story missions anymore?**

A: As of the 2026 update, story missions only provide rewards once per stage every 24 hours. This was implemented to prevent the use of auto-clickers from ruining the game's economy.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get gems in Summon Heroes now?**

A: The most consistent way to earn gems is by completing all three Dungeons every 6 hours (250 gems each, totaling 3,000 gems per day if all cycles are hit) and utilizing the AFK Mode during your downtime.

**Q: What level do I need to be to enter Dungeons?**

A: Dungeons have varying entry requirements. The Food Dungeon starts at Level 30, the Fusion Dungeon at Level 50, and the Mythic Dungeon at Level 80. Additionally, the Nightmare Circus daily quest requires Level 25.
