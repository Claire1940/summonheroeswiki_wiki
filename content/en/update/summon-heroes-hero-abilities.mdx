## The Fusion System: Enhancing Hero Power

A unique aspect of the 2026 meta is the flexible fusion system. Unlike other hero collectors that require identical duplicates to rank up, this game allows you to use any unit of the same star rarity as fodder. This makes every summon valuable, regardless of whether you intend to use the specific unit in combat.

### How to Fuse Units Effectively
1.  **Identify your Primary Unit:** Choose a high-tier unit (e.g., a 4-star Dragoon).
2.  **Gather Fodder:** Collect any other units of the same star rarity (the "fodder" does not need to match the primary unit).
3.  **Apply Fusion Crystals:** Use Fusion Crystals—which can be purchased in the Item Shop or earned via the "Sunset" code—to bridge the gap and evolve your unit to the next star level.
