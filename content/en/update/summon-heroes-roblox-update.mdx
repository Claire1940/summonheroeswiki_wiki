## Mastering the Tournament in the Summon Heroes Roblox Update

Located conveniently next to the PvP section in the main hub, the new Tournament Mode is perhaps the most significant mechanical addition in the latest **summon heroes roblox update**. Unlike standard PvP, this mode operates on a weekly reset and uses an asynchronous bracket system.

### How the Tournament Works
1.  **Party Creation:** You must create three separate teams of heroes. This requires a deep roster, as you cannot simply rely on one "carry" unit to win all three rounds.
2.  **NPC Battles:** When you enter a match, you fight against an NPC version of another real player’s team. This means you are facing actual player-built compositions without the pressure of real-time lag or human error.
3.  **Progression:** Winning battles earns you points that move you up in your specific bracket.
4.  **Rewards:** At the end of the week, your final rank determines your prizes.

| Rank | Gem Reward | Mythic Shard Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Rank 1** | 10,000 | 20 |
| **Rank 2** | 7,500 | 15 |
| **Rank 3** | 5,000 | 10 |
| **Consolation** | Variable | Small amount |

This mode encourages players to diversify their training. Instead of focusing all resources on four units, you now need at least 12 highly leveled units to ensure all three of your tournament teams are viable.

## Major Dungeon and Economy Rebalancing

The developers have listened to community feedback regarding the "daily grind" fatigue. Previously, players felt forced to log in every six hours to maximize their dungeon runs. The current **summon heroes roblox update** changes this by implementing a 24-hour cycle.

### Dungeon Limits and Reruns
You are now limited to three dungeon runs within a 24-hour period. While this sounds like a nerf, the rewards have been buffed to compensate. For example, Mythic Dungeon 2 now drops **two Mythic Shards** per completion instead of one. Furthermore, you can now choose to use all three of your daily attempts on the exact same dungeon, allowing you to target specific resources more effectively.

### The Rise of Gem Dungeons
Gems are more important than ever, and this update introduces dedicated Gem Dungeons to help players fund their summons.

*   **Gem Dungeon 1:** Rewards 500 Gems per completion.
*   **Gem Dungeon 2:** Unlocks at level 130 and rewards 1,000 Gems per completion.

Additionally, the Story Mode rewards have been overhauled. Completing a story stage now grants 100 Gems initially. However, there is a decay mechanic: each subsequent completion of that same stage reduces the reward by 10 Gems. This encourages players to push forward into new content like the Aetherwell Citadel rather than farming the easiest possible stage indefinitely.

> **Warning:** Make sure to use your three daily dungeon attempts before the server reset. Unused attempts do not stack into the next day!

## Progression Strategies for 2026

To make the most of the **summon heroes roblox update**, players should adjust their daily routine. The shift from a 6-hour cycle to a 24-hour cycle means your play sessions can be more focused and less frequent.

First, prioritize reaching level 130 to unlock Gem Dungeon 2. The 1,000-gem reward is the most efficient way to build your currency reserves for new unit banners. Second, focus on the Aetherwell Citadel to push toward the level 140 cap. The stat increases gained in those final ten levels are often the deciding factor in high-ranking Tournament matches.

When building your three Tournament teams, try to balance them rather than making one "super team" and two weak ones. Since the tournament is a best-of-three format, having two solid, reliable teams is often better than having one unbeatable team and two that lose instantly.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I access the Aetherwell Citadel in the summon heroes roblox update?**

A: The Aetherwell Citadel is available in the Story Mode menu once you have completed the previous story maps. It features five stages of increasing difficulty and is the primary area for reaching the new level 140 cap.

**Q: Is the Tournament Mode real-time PvP?**

A: No, the Tournament Mode pits your three teams against NPC versions of other players' teams. This allows for a more relaxed competitive experience where you can focus on strategy and team composition rather than reaction speed.

**Q: Why can I only run three dungeons a day now?**

A: This change was made to reduce the need for players to log in every six hours. To ensure players don't lose out on progress, the rewards for dungeons like Mythic Dungeon 2 have been doubled, and new Gem Dungeons have been added to streamline the farming process.

**Q: What happens when the Story Mode gem reward hits zero?**

A: The reward decreases by 10 Gems each time you complete a stage. Once it reaches the minimum threshold, it will no longer provide a significant gem payout, signaling that it is time to move on to more difficult maps or focus on dedicated Gem Dungeons.
