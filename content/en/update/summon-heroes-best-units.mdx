## S-Tier: The Gods of the Battlefield

Units in the S-tier are considered the "Godhood" of Summon Heroes. These characters do not just participate in the fight; they rewrite the rules of the map. If you manage to roll one of these, your account value increases exponentially.

### Reaper
The Reaper is arguably the most coveted unit in the game for 2026. Its primary mechanic revolves around a massive AOE scythe swing that consumes enemy health to fuel its own power. In infinite maps, the Reaper becomes a "feast" unit, growing stronger as the waves become denser. It is the ultimate choice for players who want to clear entire lanes with a single placement.

### Bear Tamer
Summoners are currently dominating the meta, and the Bear Tamer is the queen of this playstyle. By summoning Bear Phantoms and Cubs, she provides a secondary layer of defense that can block enemies and deal heavy nuke damage from afar. This makes her perfect for solo pushes where you lack a full team of players to cover every lane.

### Demon Knight
For those who prefer aggressive, high-speed combat, the Demon Knight offers the highest combo DPS in the game. His "Demon Awakening" ability allows him to shatter boss health bars in seconds. When paired with support units that provide attack speed buffs, the Demon Knight becomes an unstoppable force that can carry a team through the final stages of the game.

> **💡 Tip:** S-tier units are rare, but they are worth the gem grind. Never settle for a C-tier unit if you have the resources to reroll for a high-tier carry.

Many top players keep a Mythic-star Demon Hunter in their loadout even when they have access to higher-rarity units.

In end-game scenarios, bosses will often target the slimes instead of your actual heroes, giving you the window needed to burst them down.

## Understanding Unit Synergy and Tower Pairings

Even the **summon heroes best units** can fail if they aren't supported correctly. The 2026 meta emphasizes "Placement Chains" and "Command Buffs."

1. **The Tank and Spanker:** Pair a high-HP unit like **Cyber DJ** or **Diver** at the front of a lane to stall enemies. Place your high-DPS units like **Reaper** or **Demon Hunter** just behind them to clear the pack safely.
2. **The Slow and Burn:** Use **Ice Mage** to freeze large groups of enemies in place, then follow up with **Fire Mage** or **Laser Cyborg**. The DOT (Damage Over Time) from fire and lasers is significantly more effective when enemies cannot move out of the hazard zones.
3. **The Divine Buff:** Units like **Seraph** and **Divine** provide "Holy Buffs" that increase the damage of all nearby heroes. Placing these in the center of your formation is crucial for clearing high-wave boss chains.

> **Warning:** Avoid stacking too many melee units in one spot. Bosses in the late game often have large AOE stomps that can wipe out your entire front line if they are clustered too closely.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the absolute summon heroes best units for beginners?**

A: For beginners, the **Demon Hunter** and **Slime Summoner** are the best choices. They are relatively easy to obtain and can be upgraded to "Mythic Star Level," allowing them to remain relevant even in the late game.

**Q: Is it worth keeping Bandit Nick for the gold bonus?**

A: Generally, no. While Bandit Nick offers a minor gold boost, his damage is so low that you will likely lose more gems by failing waves than you would gain from his passive ability. It is better to roll for a high-damage unit that ensures you clear the map.

**Q: How do I get my units to Mythic Star Level?**

A: You must sacrifice duplicate units or use specific "Star Shards" obtained from high-level raids and daily quests. This process increases their base stats and often unlocks new secondary effects for their special abilities.

**Q: Which S-tier unit is best for solo players?**

A: **Bear Tamer** is the best for solo players. Her ability to summon multiple minions allows her to cover more ground than a single-target DPS unit, making it easier to manage multiple lanes without help from other players.
