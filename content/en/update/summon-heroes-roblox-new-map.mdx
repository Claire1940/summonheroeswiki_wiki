## Quality of Life: The Unit Index System

One of the most praised additions in the Sunset City update is the **Unit Index (Unit Decks UI)**. This feature allows players to track every unit they have collected, categorized by rarity. It serves as both a completionist's checklist and a source of free resources.

When you summon a new unit for the first time, you can navigate to the Unit Index in the top right corner of the screen to claim **Gems**. For example, summoning the new Legendary Techno Master grants a massive 800 Gems. This system is retroactive, meaning players who owned units before the update can still go back and claim their rewards for units already in their collection.

## Strategy: Defeating Cyber DJ Nikki

The final boss of the **summon heroes roblox new map** is Cyber DJ Nikki. She features high mobility and several area-denial attacks that can disrupt your tower placements. To defeat her, you should focus on a "Stun and Burn" strategy.

1.  **Placement:** Place your high-damage single-target units (like Frame Rate) at the beginning of the path loops to maximize their uptime.
2.  **Support:** Use units that can slow or stun Nikki to keep her within the range of your heavy hitters for as long as possible.
3.  **Special Moves:** Save your hero special moves for when Nikki is halfway through the track. This ensures that you don't waste damage on her initial shield phase.

By following these tactics and utilizing the new level 120 cap, you will find Sunset City to be a rewarding and manageable challenge.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the summon heroes roblox new map?**

A: Open the Unit Decks UI in the top right corner of the screen. Any unit you have summoned will have a "Claim" button next to it if you haven't collected the Gem reward yet. New legendary units typically offer the highest Gem rewards.

**Q: Is Frame Rate better than Techno Master for the new map?**

A: It depends on the stage. Frame Rate is superior for the boss fight against Cyber DJ Nikki due to his high single-target DPS. However, Techno Master is much better for clearing the dense waves of enemies found in the Hotel Lobby stage of the **summon heroes roblox new map**.

**Q: What is the fastest way to reach level 120?**
