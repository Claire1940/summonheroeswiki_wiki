### Frame Rate: The Single-Target King
Frame Rate has quickly become a fan favorite among the **summon heroes roblox new units** due to his incredibly fluid animations and high-frequency attacks. Designed specifically to melt boss health bars, Frame Rate utilizes a dashing special move where he zips back and forth across the target, delivering a flurry of punches. If you are struggling with high-HP bosses like Cyber DJ Nikki, Frame Rate is your go-to solution for raw physical damage.

### Techno Master: Dominating the Crowd
In contrast to Frame Rate, Techno Master focuses on Area of Effect (AoE) capabilities. His kit is designed to clear waves of minions efficiently, making him a staple for the five new stages in Sunset City. His moveset utilizes holographic technology to strike multiple enemies simultaneously, ensuring that your team isn't overwhelmed by the high-density spawns found in the later stages of the game.

## Sunset City: Map Layout and Level Cap Increase

The update isn't just about the **summon heroes roblox new units**; it also introduces the Sunset City map. This neon-themed environment is widely considered the most detailed level design in the game to date. It features holographic orange trees, 8-bit animated windows, and a sprawling hotel lobby section that tests your team's positioning.

### Stage Breakdown and Bosses
Sunset City consists of five distinct stages. The difficulty spikes significantly at Stage 5, where players must face the Mythic boss, **Barbette**. This boss boasts over 1.5 million HP, requiring a team that can balance high DPS with sustainability.

1. **The Outskirts:** Introduction to holographic terrain.
2. **The Neon Bridge:** Narrow passages ideal for AoE units like Techno Master.
3. **The Hotel Lobby:** Multi-directional spawns that require strong tanks.
4. **The Billboard Plaza:** High-density waves with fast-moving enemies.
5. **The DJ Arena:** The final confrontation with Barbette and Cyber DJ Nikki.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The level cap has been increased to 120. Do not attempt Stage 5 of Sunset City unless your primary team is at least level 110, as the enemy scaling is tuned for late-game players.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock the new Unit Index rewards for summon heroes roblox new units?**

A: Simply open the Unit tab and click on the "Unit Decks" icon in the top right corner. Any unit you have summoned (new or old) will appear here. Clicking on a new entry will reward you with gems, typically around 800 for Legendary units.

**Q: What is the best trait for the Reaper unit?**

A: While "God Speed" is highly recommended for faster spirit summoning, traits that boost Attack or reduce Cooldown even further are also viable. The goal is to keep your four relentless spirits active at all times to act as a shield.

**Q: Can I get Mythic Shards without playing PvP?**

A: Yes, though PvP is the most consistent daily method. You can also obtain shards through high-level story stage drops in Sunset City and special event summons, though the drop rates are lower than the guaranteed leaderboard rewards.

**Q: Is Frame Rate better than Techno Master?**

A: Neither is objectively "better" as they serve different roles. Frame Rate is superior for single-target boss fights due to his attack speed, while Techno Master is essential for clearing large groups of enemies in the new Sunset City stages. For a balanced team, it is often best to run both.
